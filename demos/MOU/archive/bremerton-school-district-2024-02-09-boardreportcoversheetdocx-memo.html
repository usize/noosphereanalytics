Board Policy and/.or Procedure Alignment: Policy 3207: Harassment, Intimidation, and Bullying

Executive Summary/Background Information: As a result of the passage of House Bill 1207,
recommendations were made by WSSDA to clarify the HIB process so as to better align with Washington
state law. The District last updated this policy in December of 2020. Changes were made to the
procedures in April of 2021.

Policy and Governance

Following the enactment of House Bill 1207, the Washington State School Directors’ Association (WSSDA)
proposed recommendations to enhance the clarity of the HIB process, ensuring closer alignment with
state law. The District revised its policy most recently in December 2020, with procedural adjustments
implemented in April 2021.

Next Steps
The policy and updated procedures will be shared with building administrators and school staff who
support the reporting process. The annual reported will be submitted to OSPI in late February.
