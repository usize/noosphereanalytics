Section 2: Action Plan
Goal #1 SEL: Goal: 65% of our students will report high on social awareness and self
management based on a portion of the student panorama survey.
Strategies to leverage district-support initiative(s) to support your school’s SEL goal:
Weekly counselor lessons
Character Strong curriculum weekly lessons
Small group instruction for identified students
School wide focus on building relationships
School counselor and dean for Tier 1 students
Daily check ins with identified students
Restorative Practice Professional Development for all staff
Reflection and reevaluation of past practices
Monitoring tools and targets:
e DESSA/Panorama beginning and end of year data. Also progress monitoring with DESSA
e Pre and Post assessments with counselor meetings

Reading Goal: Goal: 85% of students will be proficient in iReady reading.

Strategies to leverage district-support initiative(s) to support your school’s reading goal:
Building Mentors: Models instruction, provides professional development for all staff,
support teachers with implementation of CKLA, explicit phonics lessons, syllable types,
morphology lessons, coaching cycles, observations, co-planning-in variety of settings,
Highlighting specific skills and lessons that focus on state standards assessed on SBA
Utilizing intervention programs - Sonday system, LLI and Heggerty phonics
SBA interim assessment calendar
Small group intervention programs (LAP)

Weekly Data meetings with LAP teacher
Monthly grade level meeting - focus on data
Consulting team meetings to identify specific interventions with follow up meetings.
Monitoring tools and targets:
e SBA Spring 2024
e Progress Monitoring - DIBELS
e iReady Diagnostic assessments

Math Goals: By the end of the year, 85 % of students will be at or above grade level on
iReady math assessment.

Strategies that leverage district-support initiative(s) to support your school’s instructional goal:
e Professional Development: small group instruction, flexible grouping.
Math specialists: models lessons, observations and provides support to all staff
iReady personalized instruction and specific remedial lessons assigned
iReady progress monitoring
Data review monthly in job alike
SBA interim assessments and use of on line resources through WCAP portal

Monitoring tools and targets:
e i-Ready Math - diagnostic and growth monitoring
