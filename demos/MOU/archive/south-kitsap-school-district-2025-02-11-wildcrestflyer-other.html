WILDCREST Expeditionary L

earning Program is a hands-on,

project-based approach to education that provides South
Kitsap students with the chance to explore the world around
them while developing critical skills for the future!

Learning
Expeditions

Students engage in long-term investigations of
real-world topics through outdoor adventures
and interactive learning. Exploration and
physical challenges help students build
resilience and teamwork. Consistent
attendance is key for success in the program.

— Stay On Track
For Graduation

Partnered with the well balanced educational
experience offered at South Kitsap High School,
WILDCREST allows students to meet their
academic requirements and prepare them for life
beyond high school. Yearlong program that
follows the South Kitsap High School schedule.
Students attend four class periods of WILDCREST
every other day, alternating with traditional
South Kitsap High School courses.

Classroom
Experience

Designed for curious students who learn best by
doing versus in a traditional classroom setting.
Students spend their time in and out of the classroom
with meaningful projects that connect several
content areas.
