PROGRAM EVALUATION

The board requires efficiency and effectiveness in all facets of its operations. In order to achieve this goal, the board will
provide:
A. A clear statement of expectations and/or standards for the district's instructional programs;

B. A plan to provide staff and resources in support of the stated expectations and/or standards; and

C. A plan for evaluating instructional programs and services to determine how well the expectations and/or standards
are being met.

The district will use a variety of assessment processes to:
A. Determine the effectiveness of the instructional programs;

B. Assess the progress of individual students in attaining student learning goals or standards;
C. Determine the needs of individual students who are not progressing at their expected rates; and
D. Identify the needs of individual students who are in need of specialized programs.

Parents who wish to examine any assessment materials may do so by contacting the superintendent or designee. Parent
approval is necessary before administering a diagnostic personality test.
