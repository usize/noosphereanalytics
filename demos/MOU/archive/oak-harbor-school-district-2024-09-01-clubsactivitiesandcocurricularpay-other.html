Clubs, Activities, and Co-Curricular Pay

Oak Harbor School District
2024-25

Section1

Each activity must have a minimum average of ten (10) students.

Selection of activities shall be decided at the building level with approval of the building principal

and the Superintendent or Superintendent's designee.

Unless pro-rated over a twelve (12) month period (or remainder of the year) or approved by the
Human Resources and the Payroll Offices, payment for after-school activities will be paid upon

completion of the activity and proper verification provided.

Section 2

K-8 After-School Activities

After-school activities, and clubs will be paid at a rate of $31 per day, not to exceed $2,050 per
year. Weekend activities will be paid $186 for a full day and $93 for a half day for K-6 activities only.

Principals are authorized to pay $1,025 stipends (including Safety Patrol at the Intermediate Level)
per semester at the Middle School or Intermediate Level and S683 stipends per trimester at the

Elementary School Level should it be more appropriate than the $31 per day.

Directors of approved evening (after 5 PM) or weekend elementary and intermediate level music

concerts will be paid $180 per event unless already compensated by a stipend.
