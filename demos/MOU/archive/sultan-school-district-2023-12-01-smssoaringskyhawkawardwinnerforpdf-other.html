Soaring Skyhawk Award winner for December

Tesla Halldorsson
Tesla exemplifies the true spirit of forgiveness in both their actions and attitude. Always kind
and considerate, they approach every situation with a willingness to understand and work
harmoniously with others. Their dedication to hard work is evident in everything they do, as
they strive for excellence while showing patience and compassion. What stands out most is
their remarkable ability to forgive both themselves and others, demonstrating emotional
resilience and a deep understanding of the importance of growth and empathy. Their maturity
and commitment to fostering a supportive environment make them a deserving candidate for
this award.
