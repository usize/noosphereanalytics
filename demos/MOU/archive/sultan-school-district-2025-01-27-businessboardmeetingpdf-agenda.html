K. EXECUTIVE SESSION

Subject : 1. Enter into Executive Session

Meeting : Jan 27, 2025 - Business Board
Meeting

Category : K. EXECUTIVE SESSION

Type : Procedural


Public Content

Pursuant to RCW 42.30.110 and the Open Public Meetings Act, discussions on certain topics may be held in an executive session. The
topics typically cited by a school district board of directors include the following, but these are not the only purposes provided by the law:

1. Security

2. Real estate acquisition or sales

3. Negotiations on a publicly bid contract

4. Complaints/charges against an employee

5. Evaluation of candidate qualifications or review the performance of a public employee

6. Evaluation of qualifications of a candidate for elective office
7. Legal council for litigation, potential litigation, or enforcement actions
