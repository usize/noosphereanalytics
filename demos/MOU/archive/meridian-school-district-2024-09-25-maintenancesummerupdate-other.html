MHS Septic Drain
Field

> Received a violation letter from DOH in October 2023 for
standing effluent in the drain field

> A months long investigation and plan of action was put
into place in partnership with DOH and our civil engineer

> It was determined that the drain field needed to
be excavated and have the plumbing and fill
material replaced

> In April 2024 the district was awarded a $550,000
Emergency Repair Grant through OSPI for engineering and
construction

> Iverson Earthworks was awarded the project and began
work in July

> 7,200 cubic yards of material was excavated

> 6,060 lineal feet of pipe was removed and
replaced

> The project received final inspection by DOH on August
7h and was completed on August 26%

No local dollars were expended on the emergency repair
