RESOLUTION 23-13
Authorization to Invest Funds

WHEREAS, Oak Harbor School District No. 201 will have General, Capital Projects, Transportation
Vehicle, Debt Service, and Associated Student Body monies during 2023-2024 that will not be
required for immediate use of the District; and

WHEREAS, it is the intent of the District to utilize resources so as to maximize use of the taxpayer’s
dollars,

IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED that the Board of Directors of Oak Harbor School District No. 201, Island
County, Washington, authorizes Michelle Kuss-Cybula, Superintendent, or her designee, Amber
Porter, Fiscal Officer, to continue to invest such monies as they become available effective February
12, 2024.

DATED this 12th day of February 24, pursuant to RCW 28A.320.310.320.

Board President

ATTEST:

Secretary to the Board
