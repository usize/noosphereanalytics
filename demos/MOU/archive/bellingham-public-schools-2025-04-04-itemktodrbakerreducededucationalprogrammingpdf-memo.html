MEMORANDUM
TO: Dr. Greg Baker, Superintendent
FROM: Matt Whitten, Assistant Superintendent of Human Resources

DATE: April 4, 2025

SUBJECT: Reduced Educational Programming

Due to projected decline in student enrollment coupled with continued rise in operational costs, a reduction in
certificated staff will be necessary.

Based on current projections, most staff reductions will be achieved through natural attrition, including
retirements, resignations and leaves of absence. This approach will help minimize the impact on our educational
programs and maintain stability within our workforce.

A key factor in addressing the projected budget deficit has been our recent agreements with the Bellingham
Education Association, Bellingham Association of School Employees and Bellingham Administrators’
Organization to reduce previously agreed-upon compensation increases. These agreements have been
instrumental in mitigating the extent of program reductions required.

Despite these efforts, the non-renewal of a small number of provisional certificated contracts may be necessary
to fully address the funding deficit. However, it is important to note that until the state finalizes its budget, we
cannot be certain if further reductions will be necessary.

Please let me know if you have any questions.
