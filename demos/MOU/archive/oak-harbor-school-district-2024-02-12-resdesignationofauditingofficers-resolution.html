RESOLUTION 23-14
Designation of Auditing Officers

WHEREAS, the Board of Directors of Oak Harbor Public Schools is required to appoint the Auditing
Officers of the School District,

IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED, by the Board of Directors of Oak Harbor School District No. 201, Island
County, Washington, that Michelle Kuss-Cybula, Superintendent, and Amber Porter, Fiscal Officer,
be designated as Auditing Officers of the District to perform duties as authorized.

DATED this 12th day of February 2024.

Board President

ATTEST:

Secretary to the Board
