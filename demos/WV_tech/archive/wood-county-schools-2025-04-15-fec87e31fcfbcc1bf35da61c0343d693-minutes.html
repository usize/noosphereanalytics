Wood County Board of Education
Statutory Meeting — Levy Rates
Tuesday, April 15, 2025
Bailey Board Room and YouTube
6:00 pm
Minutes
1. Opening of Meeting
A. Call to Order
The Wood County Board of Education met on Tuesday, April 15, 2025 at the
Bailey Board Room, 1210 13" Street, Parkersburg, WV and via Zoom and YouTube.
President Raber called the meeting to order at 6:00 pm and Superintendent Willis
recorded the minutes.

B. Moment of Silence/Pledge of Allegiance

A moment of silence was held, followed by the Pledge of Allegiance, led by
Ron Tice.

C. Roll Call

The following board members were present: Judy Johnson, Ron Tice, Debbie
Henderhot and Randy Modesitt. Justin Raber attended via Zoom.

A quorum was established.
2. Delegations/Public Comments
A. Delegations/Public Comment
There were none.
3. Regular Agenda
A. Approval of Levy Rates

Justin Raber moved and it was seconded by Debbie Hendershot to approve the levy rates
for the fiscal year ending June 30, 2026.

The motion passed unanimously.
4. Closing of Meeting
A. Adjournment

President Raber adjourned the meeting at 6:02 pm.
