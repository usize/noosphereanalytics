February 26, 2024

Dear Mr. Riggs and Boone County Board of Education members,

Mr. Bobby Miller and his Adventure Tourism students are requesting approval to take an out-of-state
field trip to The Wilds in Cumberland, Ohio on May 1, 2024. Our two-hour tour will begin at 1:30 p.m. We will
depart from the Boone Career and Technical Center at 8 a.m. We will return to the Boone Career and Technical
Center at 8 p.m. Our transportation costs will be paid through a travel grant from the West Virginia Department of
Education. We will use additional grant money or school funds to pay for the tickets for the 17 students and three
adults who plan to attend the trip. Mr. Miller will chaperone the trip along with a board-approved parent
volunteer. We will bring bag lunches from school for lunch. The only cost of the trip for the students is money for
a fast-food dinner on the way home. We also are asking approval to pay for the tickets for the trips before we
arrive, which is the policy of The Wilds. We understand that does not comply with Boone County Schools
financial practices, which requires us to pay for products or services only after they are received. Purchasing the
tickets before the trip is the only way we will be able to visit The Wilds. If this procedure is not permitted, we will
have to cancel the trip. Thank you for your consideration.

Sincerely,

th. (ace Messer

Dr. Jacob Messer

Boone Career and Technical Center Director and Principal
