INSIGHTS FOR LEADERS SUMMIT

Join renowned and fellow district leaders
for 2 days of professional development and
exploration. In partnership with HMH and
other district leaders, attendees will receive
various models to implement MTSS systems
and a customized set of district
visualization tools to identify which solutions
and services have the greatest potential to
impact student growth.

Collectively, through a series of workshops
and peer-to-peer conversations, we will
focus on improving how we interpret and
utilize complex data to both inform

instructional planning and to build teacher
capacity.



Professional Development designed
exclusively for senior district leaders.

Summit Keynote
Speakers

Jeff Rubenstein has been building teaching and learning technologies since the mid-
1990s and is an amateur historian of the industry. In addition to his day job at Google,
where he is a practitioner applying Al to the challenges of education, he is an active
participant in the 1EdTech consortium and helps develop interoperability standards
for the education industry.

Steve Pemberton's triumphant life story is about defying seemingly insurmountable
beginnings as a child orphaned into foster care. As the best-selling author of A Chance
in the World, Steve has shared his beginnings as a ward of the state and extraordinary
life journey to Boston College and the corporate world with millions. Steve has made
equality, access, and opportunity pillars of both his professional and personal life.

He currently serves as Chief Human Experience Officer for Workhuman (formerly
Globoforce}, a leading provider of social recognition and continuous performance
development solutions. In 2015, he was appointed by United States Secretary of Labor
Thomas Perez to serve on the Advisory Committee for the Competitive Integrated
Employment of People with Disabilities.
