July 24, 2024

Dear Superintendent Riggs and Boone County Board of Education Members,

The BCTC Cosmetology Academy has been invited to visit the Salt Cave and Spa in White
Sulphur Springs, West Virginia. They will attend on August 9, 2024. The adult cosmetology instructors,
Dena Wall and Sarah Fowler, are requesting board approval to participate in this exciting learning
experience. Mrs. Wall and Mrs. Fowler will visit the spa with the following adult cosmetology students:
Mishala Adams, Alexandria Austin, Kierra Bartley, Selise Bowen, Kyerston Bowman, Mattison Eskew,
Heather Griffith, Jayden Hawkins, Madison Lutsy, Brookyn Malcomb, Christina Nichols, and Olivia
Tomblin. The students and instructors will depart from and return to BCTC the same day. Students will
be responsible for their transportation. The cost of the trip will be $20 per person and paid from the adult
cosmetology fund. If you have any questions, please feel free to contact me, Mrs. Fowler, or Mrs. Wall at
304-369-4585. Thank you for your time and consideration.

Sincerely,

Dr. Jacob Messer

Boone Career and Technical Center Director and Principal
