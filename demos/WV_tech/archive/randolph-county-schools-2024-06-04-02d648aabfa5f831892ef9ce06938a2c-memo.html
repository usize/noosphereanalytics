REGULAR MEETING
June 4, 2024
TO: Randolph County Board of Education
FROM: Debra Schmidlen, Superintendent

SUBJECT: Contracted Services for Dietician

Randolph County Schools Child Nutrition Department is requesting approval to
contract with James Severino, RD who is a licensed Dietician with Davis Medical
Center. Randolph County Schools has over 145 children who have special dietary
needs. Mr. Severino has agreed to provide these services for $65.00 an hour. This
will be on an as needed basis.
