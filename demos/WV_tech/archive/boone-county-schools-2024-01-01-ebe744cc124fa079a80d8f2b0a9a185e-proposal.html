Boone County Board of Education Goals

Graduation Rate: The graduation rates of the students in
Boone County Schools will show improvement from 2024 to
2025.

School Safety: Boone County Schools will continue to
implement new safety initiatives to strengthen school safety for
our staff and students.

Academics: Students will demonstrate academic growth in
math and reading from 2024 to 2025.

Attendance: Boone County Schools will continue using new
initiatives and effective past strategies to increase the county's
attendance rate for students.
