BOARD OF EDUCATION MEMO

June 4, 2024
TO: RANDOLPH COUNTY BOARD OF EDUCATION MEMBERS
FROM: Debra Schmidlen, Superintendent
RE: Approval for all Randolph County Schools to participate in the Community

Eligibility Provision Program for the 2024-25 School Year

The Child Nutrition Department requests that the proposal to participate in the USDA’s
Community Eligibility Provision be approved. The West Virginia Department of Education on
April 1, 2024, released data that all schools in Randolph County are eligible for this program in
the 2024-25 school year.
