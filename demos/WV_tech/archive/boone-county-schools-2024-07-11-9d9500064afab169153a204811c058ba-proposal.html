| am writing this letter requesting to purchase our fruits and vegetables for the National School Lunch

Program through Crook Brothers. This request will allow us to purchase from West Virginia companies,

allowing us to meet the required percentage of purchases we must have from in-state companies. This |
company will be able to provide a wide variety of produce on a weekly basis that other companies could

not. Along with this letter you will see all the products they can provide to our school system.

| am also requesting we continue to use Crook Brothers for our Fresh Fruit and Vegetable grant to

provide unique vegetables and fruits to our elementary school students as an additional snack
throughout the school day.
