Board of Education Meeting
Monroe County Schools
Minutes, July 16, 2024

ROLL CALL — The Monroe County Board of Education met in regular session on Tuesday, July 16,
2024 at 6:00 p.m. at the Monroe County Schools Central Office with the following members
present: Vice President Sabrina Stutts, Dr. Scott Womack, Mr. Terry Utterback, and Dr. Joetta
Basile, Secretary. President Candace Sabol joined the meeting at 6:07 p.m. Everett Fraley was
absent.

CALL TO ORDER
Vice President Sabrina Stutts called the meeting to order.

PLEDGE OF ALLEGIANCE

APPROVAL OF AGENDA/ADJUSTMENTS

Vice President Sabrina Stutts requested approval of the agenda with the following adjustment,
pull vendor 12345 and vote separately. Dr. Womack made the motion to approve. Motion
passed 3-0.

EXECUTIVE SESSION
e Student Expulsion Hearing
At 6:03 p.m., Mr. Utterback made the motion to enter into Executive Session under the

authority of WV Code §6-9A-4 to discuss Student Expulsion Hearing. Motion passed 3-
0.

Upon return to open session at 6:15 pm, all members were present including President
Candace Sabol. Mr. Utterback made the motion to table this item until August 7, 2024.
Motion passed 4-0.

PUBLIC COMMENTS
e No one registered.

COMMUNICATIONS/REPORTS

1) Superintendent Basile congratulated Educator’s Rising students who placed at
the national competition in Washington, DC. 3, 300 students competing in DC

placed 9'" in Public Speaking.

2) Superintendent Basile congratulated FFA student Eli Broyles who placed first in
the state tractor driving competition.

Other Reports
Treasure’s Report

At the recommendation of Mr. John Ballengee, Chief Business Official, Dr.
Womack made the motion to accept and approve the Treasurer Report. Motion
passed 4-0.

CONSENT ITEMS

Mr. Utterback made the motion to approve Consent Items with the exception of Vendor 12345.
Motion passed 4-0. Mr. Utterback took over the meeting. President Candace Sabol made the
motion to approve Vender 12345. Motion passed 3-0 with Vice President Sabrina Stutts
abstaining. Vice President Sabrina Stutts took back over the meeting. Consent items are as

follows:

Approve Payment of Bills — July 3, 2024 through July 16, 2024

Approval of Training Provider Memorandum of Understanding with Workforce WV on
their Ace Programs.

Approval of Droplet proposal for the 2024-2025 School Year.

Approval to move the Tuesday, August 6, 2024 Board Meeting to Wednesday, August 7,
2024 at 6 pm.

Approval of Zonar annual subscription renewal.
