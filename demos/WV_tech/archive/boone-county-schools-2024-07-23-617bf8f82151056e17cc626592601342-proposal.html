Overview

For over 30 years, Renaissance has been a leader in delivering assessment and personalized practice solutions to
pre-K—12 schools. Our valid and reliable assessments, proven practice- and progress-monitoring tools, and
comprehensive school-improvement solutions are used in more than one-third of U.S. schools. These technology-
based items enhance curriculum and support differentiated instruction for students of all ability levels in reading and
math. Used by approximately 14.8 million students across the U.S., our solutions offer ongoing feedback that is both
relevant and easy to understand, helping educators make data-driven instructional decisions and helping students
learn and achieve. Our vast experience, capabilities, and solutions make Renaissance an ideal partner poised to help
Interlocal Purchasing System reach its diverse educational goals.
