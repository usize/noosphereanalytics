MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING BETWEEN HARRISON COUNTY SCHOOLS
AND THE SOUTHERN EDUCATIONAL SERVICES COOPERATIVE (SESC) FOR BUS
OPERATOR TRAINING/EXAMINATION SERVICES

2025-2026

This MOU dated July 1, 2025, between the Southern Educational Services Cooperative (SESC),
and HARRISON COUNTY SCHOOLS, covers Bus Operator Training/Examination services to
be provided by the SESC to Harrison County Schools for the 2025-2026 fiscal year.

Harrison County Schools will be charged for all related services incurred by the SESC for fiscal
year 2025-2026. The trainer will be paid $30 per hour, plus standard State of West Virginia
benefits. The SESC will bill Harrison County Schools for these services, based on actual
expenses incurred at the time of billing plus an administrative fee of 20%. It is understood this
contract is not contingent on any outside funding source — Harrison County will be responsible for
all related charges under this agreement.

This MOU is cancelable at any time by either party with 30 days written notice. Otherwise, this
contract covers the period July 1, 2025 through June 30, 2026.

To the best of our understanding, the terms of this contract reflect the intentions of both parties.

Ms. Dora Stutler, Superintendent

Chief Administrator
