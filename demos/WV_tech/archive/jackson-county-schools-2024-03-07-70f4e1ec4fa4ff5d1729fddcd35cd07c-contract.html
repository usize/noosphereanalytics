Contract Agreement Date: March 7, 2024

This agreement between the Jackson County Board of Education, P.O. Box 770, #1 School Street,
Ripley, West Virginia 25271, and Stanley Steemer, 4742 Washington Street West, Cross Lanes, West
Virginia 25313, is made this 7 day of March, 2024.

The contractor has agreed to complete this work in accordance with the scope of work, public notice to

information. This work will have a complete date of August 9, 2024.

acceptance of the project provided the Contractor has properly billed the Owner. Payment cannot be
mae until the invoice is turned in and is approved at the next board meeting. Check with Mr. Erwin for
board meeting schedule.

agreement.
