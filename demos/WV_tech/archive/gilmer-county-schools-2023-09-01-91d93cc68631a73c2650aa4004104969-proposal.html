Grow Your Own WV - Teaching Pathwa

Students who wish to participate in the Grow Your Own WV - Teaching
Pathway will begin taking courses in the fall of their junior year of high

school. Students will take courses either face-to-face at their high school or
enroll in online dual credit courses. The GYO pathway is comprised of
24-30 credit hours, giving students who wish to become teachers the
opportunity to earn one year of college credit prior to graduating high
school at no cost to them.

In collaboration with WV HEPC (Higher Education Policy Commission),
WVDE (West Virginia Department of Education), and Glenville State
University, students participating in the GYO pathway will receive all
required textbooks at no cost to them.

As participates of the GYO pathway students will receive the following
benefits:

e Free Tuition

e Free Textbooks Rentals

Students enrolled in the Grow Your Own WV- Teaching Pathway will be
advised by the Director of Dual Enrollment/Dual Credit - Rachel
Clutter.
