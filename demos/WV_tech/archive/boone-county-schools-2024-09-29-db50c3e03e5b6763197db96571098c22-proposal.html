July 24, 2024

Dear Superintendent Riggs and Boone County Board of Education Members,

The Boone Career and Technical Center’s adult cosmetology instructors and students want to
attend the Premiere Columbus beauty trade show on September 29-30, 2024.

Here is the event agenda:

September 29: Salon and Spas Owners Guide to Profits from 9:30 a.m. until 10:30 am.

September 29: The Six Secrets to a Profitable Beauty Business from 11:30 a.m. until noon.

September 30: Twelve Ways to Prepare for Success from 9:30 a.m. until 10:30 a.m.

September 30: So, I Graduated Beauty School. Now What? from 1:00 p.m. until 2:00 p.m.

In addition to those highlighted sessions, they will attend other classes, watch other
demonstrations, and visit other vendors.

Attending the trip will be instructors Sarah Fowler and Dena Wall and students Mishala Adams,
Alexandria Austin, Kierra Bartley, Selise Bowen, Kyerston Bowman, Mattison Eskew, Heather Griffith,
Jayden Hawkins, Christina Nichols, and Olivia Tomblin.

Our instructors and students will stay at the Hilton Columbus Downtown, which is located at 401
North High Street, Columbus, OH 43215-2005. The hotel cost is $199 per room per night. They will need
four rooms for a total of $796. The ticket price for the event is $45 per student. They will need 10 tickets
for a total of $450. The total for the trip will be $1,246. Our students will cover their transportation and
expenses for food and souvenirs. Our instructors and students plan to fundraise to cover the costs of the

hotel rooms.
