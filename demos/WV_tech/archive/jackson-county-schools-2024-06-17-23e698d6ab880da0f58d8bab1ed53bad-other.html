Summer School
2024

Summer School opportunities will be offered for the purpose of academic remediation,
credit recovery, and enrichment. Staff for summer school offerings will be employed on an as
needed basis, subject to change based on student enrollment.

Elementary School Summer School (Kindergarten-Grade 5
Teacher Contract Term: June 14-July 26, 2024

Student Attendance Days: June 17-July 25, 2024 (Mondays-Thursdays)
8:15 am-2:45 pm

Programs will be held at Ripley Elementary and Henry J. Kaiser only. Core academic
components of Reading Language Arts and Mathematics will be provided daily with a staffing
plan of 1 teacher for every 10-12 students. Support will be provided for students with special
needs. Transportation services will be offered this summer due to the inclusion of summer
programs being an option to avoid retention in HB 3035. The ten (10) bus routes established in
summers previous will be the general framework for pickup/drop-off locations throughout the
county. Transportation will work to make needed changes. Breakfast and lunch will be available
for participating students.

years participation:

The elementary summer school programs will be supported by a summer school administrator
at each location. Counseling services will be provided at each location, and nurse positions will
be evaluated based on student enrollment and student need.
