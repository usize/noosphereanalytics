State of West Virginia
County of Jefferson, to-wit:

The Board of Education of Jefferson County, West Virginia, met in Special Session
on February 19, 2024, at 5:30 p.m. at the Board of Education Office, 110 Mordington
Avenue, Charles Town, West Virginia, with the following members participating: Mrs.
Kathy Skinner, President, Mr. Mark Osbourn, Vice President, Dr. Donna Joy, Mrs. Laurie
Ogden, and Mrs. Carmen Bratton.

The purpose of the meeting was to meet with the LSICs of Wright Denny
Intermediate, C.W.Shipley Elementary, and Driswood Elementary Schools.

Following the call to order, the President led the audience in the Pledge of Allegiance.

The same format was used by each school, and each school was allotted 30 minutes for
its presentation and comments/questions from the Board. Board members were provided with a
complete PowerPoint presentation for each school, including demographics, achievement,
school climate and behavior, attendance, parent involvement, strategies for improvement,
athletics and wellness, facilities, challenges, requests for support, and areas of pride and
appreciation.

Presenting/Attending from each school were:
