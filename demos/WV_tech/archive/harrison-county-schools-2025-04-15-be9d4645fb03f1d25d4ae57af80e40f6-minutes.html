5. SUPERINTENDENT'S UPDATE
A. Superintendent's Update

The Superintendent will update the Board on the following:

1. Curriculum & Technology — | appreciate the new secondary curriculum specialist, she was

complimented while Mrs. Stutler was out visiting schools. Mrs. Stutler also thanked the folks
for getting all the stuff in order for the schools moving. Waiver was not approved so June 2, 3
& 4 will be added to the end of the year. June 5" will be teachers last day. Next years
Calendar will be put out as well.

2. Facilities — Nutter Fort’s roof is 95% finished, should be done this week. Mark Miller is coming
to review our sites for the MIP application Mrs. Stutler applied for sometime after spring
break. Bus routes for consolidation will be finished 1% part of May. RCB and MMS are on
target to be finished.

Finance — 109 employees met with our insurance rep. Amanda Sims at the schools.

Personnel —

Safety & Health — Mrs. Stutler is asking Jason Snider and Todd Poole to present in a future

meeting about an update on safety.

6. United Technical Center Update — Doug Hogue — UTC Graduation will be May 15" held at
the Robinson Grand at 6PM. 370 Enrollment. 12 Students should be recognized at upcoming
Board Meeting.

7. Miscellaneous — Survey update — The first phase was the survey, now the next phase is focus
groups. Which will happen in May. 6 of them. Also, schools will continue to have free lunches.
