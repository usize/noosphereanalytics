contract) for upgrading computers for schools. Funding will be from ESSERF Arp funds.

Approval of contract with Developmental Advantage Attachment 2011

The Superintendent recommends the approval of a contract with Developmental Advantage that
wil! be in excess of $50,000.00. The contract will provide consultation and behavior services for
Fayette County Schools. The contract will also provide behavior support in the form of staff
training. This service is needed to support students and staff.

Approval of contract with Mills Floor Covering, LLC Attachment 2012

The Superintendent recommends approval of the contract with Milis Floor Covering, LLC for the
replacement of remaining old sidewalk at the front of the Fayette Institute of Technology and
specific sections of sidewalk at Oak Hill High School for a total of $60,000.00, Funding for this
project will be maintenance funds.
