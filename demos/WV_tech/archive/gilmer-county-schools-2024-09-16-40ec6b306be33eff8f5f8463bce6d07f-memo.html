MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING

between
Calhoun- Gilmer Career Center

And

Gilmer County

Scope — To provide an opportunity for students who attend Calhoun-Gilmer Career Center an
opportunity to earn embedded credit in Gilmer County based upon this Memorandum of Understanding
between Calhoun-Gilmer Career Center and Gilmer County.

VALUES
This partnership will be guided by the following principles:

e Students will receive high quality instruction that will allow them to work toward mastery on
100% of the content standards and objectives for all embedded credit courses approved by the
county and WVBE.

e Embedded credit is not to create a time-shortened high school experience but rather provide
opportunities for students to participate in advanced academic and/or career/technical
education courses without having to choose one over the other.

INTENT

By implementing embedded credit courses at Calhoun-Gilmer Career Center, students in Gilmer County
will:

e Have continued opportunities to complete both a rigorous academic and career/technical
education courses/concentrations;

e Earn transcribed credit, and;

e Be prepared for college and career by improving their achievement.

From September 16, 2024, this MOU is effective immediately and will be reviewed annually.

Superintendent, Gilmer County Schools Date
