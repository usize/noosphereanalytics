Elementary Redistricting Proposal for the 2025-26 SY

Joyce White reviewed a revised PowerPoint update to the Board. In addition, J.R. Hollen
and Larry Willingham were there to answer questions. The three proposed options and their
impacts were reviewed. Donna Joy made a motion to table the item, seconded by Carmen
Bratton. Dr. Bishop clarified that the Board was not being asked to decide on a school closure or
the redistricting map at this time, the request is to allow staff to begin collecting data for moving
forward in this process. After extensive discussion, the initial motion was withdrawn by Donna
Joy and Carmen Bratton. A motion was made by Laurie Ogden, seconded by Mark Osbourn, to
approve staff to begin gathering data and exploring options 1-3 for the proposed elementary

redistricting/possible closure of a school.

Vote taken; motion carried 5-0.

Washington High School Auxiliary Gym

Joyce White along with Mr. Randy Jones from ZMM presented an update on Locations D
(retain parking spaces), D1 (remove 21 parking spaces), and E which were the preferences
decided upon during the February 26, 2024 board meeting. Detailed information concerning
location, construction, and cost for each option was provided. Discussion and questions from the

Board followed.

Upon the recommendation of the Superintendent, a motion was made by Donna Joy, and

seconded by Mark Osbourn, to approve the bid request for Location E.

Vote taken; motion carried 5-0.
