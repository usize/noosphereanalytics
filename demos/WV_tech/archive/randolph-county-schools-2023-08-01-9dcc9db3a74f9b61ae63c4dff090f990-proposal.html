TITLE:

Randolph County Board of Education
Elkins, WV

Job Description

PLC Teacher Leader

QUALIFICATIONS:

1. Hold a WV Teaching License
2. Must teach in the grade level or content specific area of the PLC

REPORTS TO: County Curriculum Team
JOB RESPONSIBLITIES:

1.

Exhibit knowledge and skill in the teaching of the assigned grade-level/subject-area
standards.

2. Help to lead the implementation of the county’s instructional framework within the assigned
grade-level/subject-area.

3. Plan for and facilitate the assigned county-wide grade-level/subject-area PLCs.

4. Promote the use of evidence-based materials and instructional practices within the
assigned grade-level/subject-area.

5. Plan for and implement activities to support county and state initiatives in the areas of
connection, engagement, and high-quality instruction.

6. Serve as a liaison between the district administrative staff and the grade-level/subject-area
staff regarding the implementation of the county's instructional framework including
attending BOY and EOY planning meetings and disseminating information within the
assigned grade-level/subject-area.

7. Support grade-level/subject-area staff in the implementation of the county's instructional
framework.

8. Collaborate with district-level staff to analyze data and create a continuous plan for
improvement.

9. Assist with updating subject area curriculum maps in collaboration with textbook adoption
committee representatives.

SALARY: $300.00 annual supplement paid out of Teacher Induction funds
EVALUATION: Performance of this job will be evaluated in accordance with the provisions of the

RCBOE Policy (3220).
