PROPOSAL FOR ORIENTATION AND MOBILITY SERVICES

Boone County Schools will agree to contract with Dot-One-Three Vision Services for the provision of
orientation and mobility services for the flat rate of $350.00 for a minimum of | hour and the maximum
of 2.5 hours per visit including travel time and travel expenses. The fee will include direct services,
assessment, consultation, and IEP meetings. Payment will be made within 30 days of receipt of invoice
for services rendered,

It will be mutually agreed upon and between the parties here too that the contractor will:

* Perform orientation and mobility services in accordance with the individualized education
(IEP) of each student.

* Set up appropriate lessons and establish short term and long-term goals for inclusion on the
TEP.

¢ Instruct the student, parent, and teachers in activities to be carried out between lessons.

« Prepare progress notes as documented on service logs the day the services are rendered.

* Provide professional liability insurance for orientation and mobility which holds harmless
Boone County Schools for possible incidents which may occur during instruction.

¢ Meet the certification requirements for certified orientation and mobility specialist from
Academy for Certification of Vision Rehabilitation aud Educational Professionals
(ACVREP).

* Work cooperatively with the Director of Special Education to develop a schedule of services
to meet the needs of students Individualized Education Program.

¢ This agreement is subject to review and modification upon request of either party.

¢ This agreement may be dissolved upon mutual agreement at any time upon 30 days prior
notification by either party.
