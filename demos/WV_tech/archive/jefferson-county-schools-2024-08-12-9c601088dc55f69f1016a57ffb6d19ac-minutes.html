6. Unfinished Business

7. New Business

A. Discussion and Action on McCormick Grant Committee Recommendations for 2024-25 SY

1.

Suzanne Swartz, the Chairperson of the McCormick Grant Committee, reviewed the
recommendations for the 2024-25 SY grants. Upon the recommendation of the
Superintendent, a motion was made by Rikki Twyford, seconded by Carmen Bratton,
to approve the recommended grants in the amount of $139,121.57. Vote taken;
motion carried 5-0.

B. Discussion and Action on Acceptable Use Policies

1.

Upon the recommendation of the Superintendent, a motion was made by Laurie
Ogden, seconded by Carmen Bratton, to put the Acceptable Use Policies, 7540.03 and
7540.04 out for public review. Vote taken; motion carried 5-0.

C. Discussion and Action on agreement with Jefferson County Parks and Recreation to build a
new public Park in Shepherdstown

1.

il.

A motion was made by Laurie Ogden, seconded by Rikki Twyford to approve the
ground lease agreement with Jefferson County Parks. Extensive questions and
discussions followed. Vote taken; the motion failed. 1 vote in favor, 3 against, and 1
abstention. Kathy Skinner voted in favor, Rikki Twyford, Carmen Bratton, and Laurie
Ogden voted against and Donna Joy requested to abstain due to needing more
information.

The item will be put on the next agenda for discussion and action. It was requested
that a re-evaluation clause be added and board members are to send any additional
questions to Dr. Bishop.

D. Action on Celebrate Freedom Week — September 10-17

1.

Upon the recommendation of the Superintendent, a motion was made by Rikki
Twyford, seconded by Laurie Ogden, to approve September 10-17 as Freedom Week.
After discussion, an amended motion was made by Rikki Twyford, seconded by
Laurie Ogden, to approve Celebrate Freedom Week for September 10-17, 2024 to
include grade level guidelines provided by the WVDE (W. Va. State Code §18-2-9).
Vote taken; motion carried 5-0.
