6.10 Make it Shine - The Celebration on the Little Coal River #5

Granted permission to the Boone Career & Technical Center to conduct the Sth annual - Make it Shine - The Celebration on the
Little Coal River roadside and river cleanup on Friday, May 3, 2024.

The targeted area for cleanup is Workman's Branch, Old River Road and Danville up to the confluence of Rock Creek.

The community partners Include West Virginia Rivers Coalition, West Virginia Department of Environmental Protection, Coal River
Group, Forks of Coal Foundation, Trash Your Kayak West Virginia, Mountain Mama Lodging, Boone County Foundation, and Boone
Memorial Health, as well as the municipalities of Madison and Danville and several small businesses throughout the area.

The Army National Guard, Danville Police Department, Danville Volunteer Fire Department, Madison Police Department, and
Madison Volunteer Fire Department have agreed to be on hand for the day. The Boone County Sheriff's Department has also
agreed to assist in keeping the students safe.

Transportation: School Buses

Cost: Grant funds will cover the costs of the event.

Schedule: 8:00 a.m. - 12:00 noon - Roadside and river cleanup

12:00 noon - 1:00 p.m. - Lunch at the Madison City Park provided by West Madison Grocery
1:00 p.m. - Buses will transport the students back to the Career Center
