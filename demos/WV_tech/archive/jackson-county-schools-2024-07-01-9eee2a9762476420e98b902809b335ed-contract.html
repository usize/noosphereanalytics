MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING BETWEEN JACKSON COUNTY SCHOOLS
AND THE SOUTHERN EDUCATIONAL SERVICES COOPERATIVE (SESC) FOR BUS
OPERATOR EXAMINATION SERVICES

2024-2025

This MOU dated July 1, 2024, between the Southern Educational Services Cooperative (SESC),
and JACKSON COUNTY SCHOOLS, covers Bus Operator Examination services to be
provided by the SESC to Jackson County Schools for the 2024-2025 fiscal year.

Jackson County Schools will be charged for all related services incurred by the SESC for fiscal
year 2024-2025. The trainer will be paid $30 per hour, plus standard State of West Virginia
benefits. The SESC will bill Jackson County Schools for these services, based on actual expenses
incurred at the time of billing plus an administrative fee of 20%. It is understood this contract is
not contingent on any outside funding source — Jackson County will be responsible for all related
charges under this agreement.

This MOU is cancelable at any time by either party with 30 days written notice. Otherwise, this
contract covers the period July 1, 2024 through June 30, 2025.

To the best of our understanding, the terms of this contract reflect the intentions of both parties.

Jason Butcher, Chief Administrator Mr. William Hosaflook, Superintendent
