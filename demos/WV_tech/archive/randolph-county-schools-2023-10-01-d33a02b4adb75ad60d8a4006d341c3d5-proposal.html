TITLE:

Randolph County Board of Education
Elkins, WV

Job Description

Capturing Kids Hearts (CKH) Process Champion

QUALIFICATIONS:

1. Hold a WV Teaching License
2. Exhibit knowledge and skill in implementing CKH as outlined in the CKH
implementation rubric

REPORTS TO: Principal/County Curriculum Team
JOB RESPONSIBLITIES:

1.

Help to lead the implementation of the county’s CKH initiative within the assigned school.

2. Collaborate with the principal and other Process Champions to plan for and facilitate CKH
activities at school-wide meetings and PLCs.

3. Serve as a liaison between the district administrative staff and the school regarding
implementation of the CKH initiative including attending school and county planning
meetings and disseminating information.

4. Support school staff in the implementation of the county's CKH initiative.

5. Collaborate with district-level staff to analyze data and create a continuous plan for
improvement.

SALARY: $500.00 annual supplement paid out of Teacher Induction funds
EVALUATION: Performance of this job will be evaluated in accordance with the provisions of the

RCBOE Policy (3220).
