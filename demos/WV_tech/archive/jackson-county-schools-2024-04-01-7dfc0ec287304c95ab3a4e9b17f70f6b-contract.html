The purpose of this Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) is to establish a
collaborative relationship between Marshall Universtiy (HE)
and the Allied Health

(list county, PK-12 private school or home school)
Both parties to this MOU share the goal of collaboration and collective
responsibility to help high school students earn college and high school credits at
the same time by taking courses from the State’s public institutions of higher
education.
This agreement is to promote rigorous academic pursuits while providing more
opportunities for high school students to obtain college credit prior to high school

graduation.
THIS AGREEMENT COVERS THE FOLLOWING COMPONENTS:

Before high school graduation:

e IHE partner will submit a crosswalk of transfer alignment between the core
education courses and required courses.

e IHE partner will provide support for adjunct faculty who teach the required
courses in the high school and ensure learning outcomes and assessments align
with the campus-based course.

e County and their partner IHE will clearly communicate and counsel/advise high
school students when choosing courses to ensure they are meeting state/county
high school graduation requirements as well as course requirements to

successfully complete Allied Health
