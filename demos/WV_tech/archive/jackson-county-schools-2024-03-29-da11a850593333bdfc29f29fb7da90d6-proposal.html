e Surplus Auction Items- Declare Miscellaneous Equipment, and Furniture as Surplus Items. We have
identified items to be sold at the board’s surplus auction on Friday, March 29, 2024. The auction is
held in conjunction with the Ravenswood FFA Consignment Farm Equipment Auction on Saturday,
March 30, 2024 and is conducted by Ravenswood FFA members, advisors, alumni and volunteers.

The list of board items for sale includes some unneeded miscellaneous office and kitchen
equipment and furniture, including 40 computer monitors with no power or video cables.
These items are typically broken, frequently in storage for years, and not worth the amount
required for their repair even if repair parts can be found.

All vehicles will sit on the transportation lot for public view and we will sale at the auction.
