License and Purchase Agreement between Carnegie Learning Inc. and
Randoph County Board of Education

Carnegie Learning, Inc. is a leading publisher of innovative, research-based curricula for middle
school, high school, and post-secondary students. We help students succeed, creating a gateway to
graduation and preparing them for 21st century careers.

Partnering with educators for continued success, Carnegie Learning is working side-by-side with
hundreds of schools and districts implementing our curricula, and we are dedicated to partnering
with you to increase teacher effectiveness and student achievement. Our goal is to support your
team of teachers, coaches and leaders to obtain the results your students deserve.

This agreement, dated May 1, 2024 is made by and between Carnegie Learning,
Inc. (the "Company"), located at 4 Smithfield Street, Pittsburgh, PA, 15222 and
Randolph County Board of Education, located at 40 11" Street, Elkins, WV
26241 ("Client").

1. Services to be provided. The company will provide Carnegie Learning’s
Elementary and Middle School Math Solutions, including consumable
student editions, practice books, digital resources, teacher materials and
professional learning over the 5 year term of adoption to Client in
accordance with the terms and conditions set forth in this Agreement. The
specific services to be provided shall be selected and agreed upon jointly
by Company and Client.

2. Term of Agreement. This Agreement will begin May 1, 2024 and conclude June 30, 2029.

3. Responsibilities of Company. Materials and services will be rendered
according to the agreed upon Exhibit A.

4. Contracting outside Company Agreement. Contracting with any Company employee or
assign independently is forbidden.

5. Payment and Terms.
a. First payment is due prior to or by July 15, 2024.
b. Payment will be subject to the terms as listed in Exhibit C.


1. Electronic Signatures

This Agreement and related documents may be signed in counterparts, and may be accepted in
electronic form (e.g., by scanned copy of the signed document, an electronic or digital signature or
other means of demonstrating assent) and each Party’s acceptance will be deemed binding on the
Parties. Computer maintained records of the Statement of Work and related documents when
produced in hard copy form shall constitute business records and shall have the same validity as any
other generally recognized business records.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, each party has read and agrees to be legally bound by this Agreement, and has

Date written above.
