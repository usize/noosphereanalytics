Important Information

Welcome to our 24" Annual Conference and Trade Show. We
have valuable information for you this year regarding
attendance. At sign-in, you will be issued a Numbered
Proximity Card (NPC). This will be your number throughout
the conference. You will also receive a lanyard to which you
can attach your NPC. You are responsible for the NPC card so
keep it safe. The numbered NPC is particularly important.
County Administrators are requesting attendance information
be sent to them for verification of training hours. You will be
required to swipe your NPC only at the end of each class to
receive credit for the class. Once you are in the class, you
must remain there until the class is over and you have swiped
your card. If you leave the class, before it is over your card will
not be swiped, you will not receive credit for the class and the
county administrators will know that you did not attend the
class. If you are attending the conference for only one day, you
must return your card at the end of the day to the designated
WVASFA rep to be deactivated. If you are attending both days,
you will return your card at the end of the second day of classes.
The lanyard is yours to keep. If you lose your card, you will not
be issued another card. You will also be responsible for any
costs to WVASFA for processes incurred for replacing the card
for future conferences. Without your card, you will not
receive credit for attending the classes or the conference.
