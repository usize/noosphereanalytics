BOARD OF EDUCATION MEMO

June 4, 2024

TO: RANDOLPH COUNTY BOARD OF EDUCATION MEMBERS
FROM: Debra Schmidlen, Superintendent

RE: Approval for Extension of Dairy Bid for the 2024-25 School Year

United Dairy the current milk and dairy vendor of products for Randolph
County Schools, has requested the opportunity to extend their bid for the 2024-25
school year. The original contract was accepted in June 2022 and has an option for
bid extension for up to three years.

The Child Nutrition Department is pleased with the service and quality of
services provided by United Dairy.

I respectfully recommend the Board to approve this extension request.
