Contracted Service Agreement
between

Applied Behavior Learning Center
325 6 Avenue
South Charleston, WV 25303

and
Boone County Schools
69 Avenue B
Madison, WV 25310

This agreement is between Boone County Schools and Applied Behavior

Learning Center for the 2024-25 school year. Each party agrees to the terms specified

below. The term of this contract shail begin on the date both parties signed and may be

terminated by either party with 30 days written notice.

Terms of agreement:

Boone County School District agrees to:

1.

Provide student records for assessment purposes after consent for treatment is
signed by the guardian(s}.

Facilitate the arrangements of appointments with teachers and guardian(s).
Pay $125.00 per hour for BCBA/BCaBA ABA consultation/therapy services.

Pay $125.00 an hour for BCBA/BCaBA travel time. Consultation visits will be
scheduled, if possible, on days the consultant is in the area.

Pay $50.00 per hour for Registered Behavior Technician (RBT) ABA therapy
services.

Pay $50.00 per hour for RBT travel time. Therapy services will be scheduled, if
possible, on days RBT is in the area.

Submit payment within 30 days of invoice.


Applied Behavior Learning Center agrees to:

iL.

Complete a functional behavior assessment and/or comprehensive assessment
on referred students.

Develop written ABA interventions and positive behavior supports.
Train/coach staff in intervention strategies and supports.
Screen students to determine if additional services are warranted.

Provide follow up services including but not limited to data analysis, on-going
coaching, and any necessary revisions to the ABA interventions and supports.

Provide direct ABA therapy if outlined in IEP’s.

All services will be conducted by or supervised by a Board-Certified Behavior
Analyst if it is an RBT.

Provide a summary of completed services with invoice.
