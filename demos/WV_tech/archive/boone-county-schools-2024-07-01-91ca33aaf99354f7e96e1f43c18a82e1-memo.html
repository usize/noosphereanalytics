Memorandum of Understanding

This Memorandum of Understanding, (hereafter called MOU), made this 1°t day of July 2024 of by and between the
West Virginia Division of Corrections and Rehabilitation — Bureau of Juvenile Services (WV DCR — BJS), Tri-County Youth
Reporting Center (TCYRC) and Boone County Schools Alternative Education (ALC) Boone County Schools (BCS) agree as

follows:

TCYRC will:

e Provide adequate classroom(s) for educational use

e Will maintain cleanliness of the classrooms as well as coordinate maintenance needs

e Provide supportive group activities and counseling

e Pick up and deliver breakfast as well as lunch to all students in (ALC)

e Provide security during school hours (8:00am - 2:00pm) and during transportation

e Provide crisis intervention

e Attend IEP and other educational meetings for students

e Provide training on safety, security and policy as needed

e Assist with BOE teacher to maintain visual supervision of the students

e Provide transportation to the students from Boone County Technical Center (BCTC) to ALC in mornings
and back to BCTC in afternoons. Will also comply with BOE policies and procedures

BCS will:

e Provide at least (1) teacher(S) for students

e Provide lunches and breakfast for students

e Provide academic performance assessments on students entering ALC

e Attend county SAT and IEP meetings

e Record attendance
Assist with TCYRC staff to maintain visual supervision of the students
Provide computers for teacher(s) and students

e Provide instructional supplies

e Provide summery of performance to TCYRC staff

e Provide phone and fax service for the classroom(s)


e Provide internet service

TRCYC and BCS shall hold ail information confidential regarding program participants and shall only release such
information with signed consent or in incorporation with law enforcement investigation in compliance with local,

Be prepared for students 5 days a week 8:00am until 2:00pm

state, federal laws, and statutes. This document is subject to change and review.

In witness whereof, the parties hereto have caused this MOU to be executed as of the day and year listed above. This
agreement will renewJuly 1, 2025, unless either party decides to decline within (60) days or by subsequent agreement

by both parties.
