on? From: Melissa Browning, Director of Special Education
Date: July 15, 2024
Re: CSI-ATS Grant Funds for FY 24

In Fall 2024, any remaining funds from fiscal year
2024 must be used for CSI-ATS schools. These funds
enabled Ripley Elementary to hire an instructional coach
dedicated to improving student outcomes. The grant
specifies a focus on students in intervention and
individual educational programs.

Education System Solutions, Inc. will provide two
days of co-teaching professional development and 10
days of onsite coaching support. Throughout the training
and coaching, staff will focus on inclusive best practices,
instructional best practices, and focused support.
