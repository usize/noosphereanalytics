State of West Virginia
County of Jefferson, to-wit:

The Board of Education of Jefferson County, West Virginia, met in Regular Session on
February 12, 2024 at 6:00 p.m. at Washington High School, 300 Washington Patriots Drive,
Charles Town, West Virginia, with the following members participating: Mrs. Kathy Skinner,
President, Mr. Mark Osbourn, Vice President, Dr. Donna Joy, Mrs. Laurie Ogden, and Mrs.

Carmen Bratton.
Following the call to order, the President led in the Pledge of Allegiance.

Prior to the regular agenda, Mrs. Skinner asked for a moment of silence in memory of

Jodi Brock, Principal at Wildwood Middle School.

Minutes

A motion was made by Laurie Ogden, seconded by Mark Osbourn, to approve Minutes of

Regular Board Meeting held January 22, 2024.

Vote taken; motion carried 5-0.

Citizens’ Comments

None

Consent Agenda

Prior to the Consent Agenda items, Amy Loring noted that item #25 will be pulled for

discussion in Executive Session.

Upon the recommendation of the Superintendent, a motion was made by Mark Osbourn,

seconded by Carmen Bratton, to approve the Consent Agenda items with change as noted.

Vote taken; motion carried 5-0.

Wallops Island: Ecology in Action Presentation

Mr. David Banks introduced Ms. Dayna Juraschek, a teacher at Jefferson High School.

Nicodemus who presented on the field trip they took to Assateague, Chincoteague, and Wallops
Island in October of 2023. Drew Cardis and Jillian Dillow developed the presentation with
Claire Burkhardt and Elisabeth Gottlieb.

Communications and Public Information, who will begin with JCS on February 26, 2024.

At 6:54 p.m. a motion was made by Mark Osbourn, seconded by Carmen Bratton and
