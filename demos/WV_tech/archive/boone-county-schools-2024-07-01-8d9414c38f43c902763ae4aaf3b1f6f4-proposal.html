| have attached the quotes for the materials | would like to purchase to enhance our current curriculum in both elementary
(Horizons) and secondary (Read 180). It has come to my attention that materials for remediation have not been
purchased in over seven years, and the special education secondary school's programs are in need of an updated and
comprehensive ELA curriculum.

Horizons - Elevate and Discovery

Teachers requested printed manuals for the Horizons - Discovery program. Due to the number of non-readers in our
middle school resource ELA classes additional student transfer books and teacher kits are needed for Horizons - Elevate.
Recently a coach from the Horizons program visited our school. Our teachers are excited about this research based
program for their students. The coach commented on the enthusiasm our teachers have. I'm requesting these additional
materials to keep that momentum, enthusiasm, and excitement going.

READ 180

There are two proposals for this program. The first is Digital Renewal July 2024. Those are not due until July, 2024. That
is the cost of renewing the 260 licenses for the July 2024-July 2025 date range. The cost is prorated the 45.00/license
cost to get us on a July-July billing schedule instead of the BCS renewal coming due in October.

The second cost proposal Is for the following:

¢ Gratis/no charge Teacher Licenses and Teacher Materials for the move to ED

e 5Classroom Libraries that were requested, Levels B and C to accommodate.

* Student workbooks. We may need to tweak this as the year gets started since we do not know where the students
may fall when they take that placement test yet—but we did 40% books to represent the code for those students that
may need extra foundational skills (they would be placed into this course first in the software and then move to the

Real book after completion depending on level and placement upon taking the initial tests when starting the
program). And 60% of the workbooks to represent Level B and Level C Real Book for Read 180.

e One no-charge 2 hour professional development session; this is virtual.
e One PD day was included.

Despite the consistent renewal payments made by BCS for 7 years, no new materials have been acquired, and the
training for the existing program is outdated. This situation poses a significant challenge to the quality of education we aim
to provide to our students.

To address this concern and ensure that our secondary schools have the necessary resources for effective remediation, |
would like to secure the funds for READ 180.

Thank you for considering these purchases.
