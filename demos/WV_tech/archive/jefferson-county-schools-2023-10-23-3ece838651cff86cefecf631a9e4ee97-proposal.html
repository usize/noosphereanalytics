STAND ALONE CONCESSION/RESTROOM ESTIMATE

Washington High School Concession/Restroom Building —ZMM Quote
October 19, 2023

For a concession / restroom building to serve the softball / baseball area at Washington High School we
would anticipate about a 900 SF building. | have attached a sketch showing a proposed location for
the building just for the purposes of estimating the utility costs.

Site utility costs are estimated as follows:

1. Pump Station $32,500
2. Sewer Force Main $40,300
3. Water line 535,100
4. Electric service 13,000

Total Site Utilities $120,900
Any other site preparation costs (grading, erosion control, storm drain, concrete sidewalk connections)
would be included in a square foot cost for the building. We would estimate the square foot cost to be

approximately $450 per SF resulting in a building cost of $405,000.

Combined with the site utility costs, we would anticipate the construction cost to be $405,000 +

$120,900 = $525,900

Funding Source currently available for this potential project: $250,000
