State of West Virginia
County of Jefferson, to-wit:

The Board of Education of Jefferson County, West Virginia, met in Special Session
on April 16, 2024, at 11:00 a.m. via Zoom conference, Charles Town, West Virginia, with
the following members participating: Mrs. Kathy Skinner, President, Mr. Mark Osbourn,
Dr. Donna Joy, Mrs. Laurie Ogden, and Mrs. Carmen Bratton.

The purpose of the meeting was to reconvene the Regular Board Meeting held on
March 25, 2024, to officially approve and adopt the FY25 Proposed Levy Rates as presented
at the March 25 meeting.

Following the call to order, the President led in the Pledge of Allegiance.

Schedules that had previously been made available reflected projected net taxes to be

collected for the fiscal year ending, as follows:

Current Expense Levy $24,971,873
Excess Levy $30,098,285
Bond $ 3,420,250

A motion was made by Mark Osbourn, seconded by Carmen Bratton, to officially adopt
and approve the FY25 Levy rates. Motion carried 5-0.

The officially adopted levy rates and levy order will be forwarded to the State Auditor’s
office.

There being no further business, the March 25, 2024 Board meeting was adjourned by

the President at 11:03 a.m.
