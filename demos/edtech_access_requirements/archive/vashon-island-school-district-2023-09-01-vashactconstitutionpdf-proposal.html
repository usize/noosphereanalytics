VashAct Constitution

Article |: Name
The name of this organization shall be Vashon Island High School Political Action Club or
VashAct.

Article Il: Purpose

The purpose of VashAct is to give students a basic understanding of political participation
through real actions and to provide an organizational structure for the student body to get
involved with local, national and international politics and the causes that are important to them.
This club aims to attend protests, raise money for charitable causes, meet and lobby
representatives.

Article Ill: Membership

Membership in VashAct is open to all students enrolled at Vashon Island High School,
regardless of race, ethnicity, or cultural background. Members must adhere to the school's rules
and regulations and actively participate in club meetings and activities.

Article IV: Officers

Section 1: The officers of VashAct shall be President (there can be two co-presidents in the
event of a tie), Secretary, and Treasurer.

Section 2: Officers shall be elected by a majority vote of the members present during the first
meeting of the school year.

Section 3: Officer duties are as follows:

e President: Presides over meetings, sets agendas, and represents the club at school
events.

e Secretary: Keeps minutes of meetings, maintains records, and handles club
correspondence.

e Treasurer: Manages club finances and prepares reports on the club's budget and
expenditures.

Article V: Meetings

Section 1: Regular meetings of VashAct will be held weekly, with additional meetings as needed.
Section 2: The President shall call special meetings as necessary, with notice given to all
members.

Section 3: A quorum, consisting of a simple majority of active members, is required to conduct
official club business.

Article VI: Elections and Terms of Office

Section 1: Elections for club officers will take place annually at the beginning of the school year.
Section 2: Officers shall serve for one academic year and may run for re-election.

Section 3: In the event of a vacancy, a special election shall be held to fill the position.


Section 4: At any given meeting, any member of the club can call for a vote to re-elect a
position. If the club, by majority vote, approves the motion, then there can be a re-election vote.
The person currently holding the position may be elected again.

Article Vil: Finances

Section 1: VashAct shall adhere to all Washington state laws and the school's Associated
Student Body (ASB) guidelines for handling finances.

Section 2: All funds raised by the club must be deposited into the school's ASB account and
managed in accordance with school policies.

Section 3: Expenditures must be approved by a majority vote of members and comply with ASB
financial regulations.

Article Vill: Amendments

Section 1: Proposed amendments to this constitution must be submitted in writing to the
President.

Section 2: Amendments shall be discussed and voted upon at the next scheduled meeting, with
a majority vote required for adoption.

Article IX: Advisor
VashAct shall have a faculty advisor, who is responsible for providing guidance, ensuring the
club adheres to school and state regulations, and attending meetings as necessary.

Article X: Ratification
This constitution shall be effective upon approval by a two-thirds majority of members and
recognition by the Associated Student Body of Vashon Island High School.
