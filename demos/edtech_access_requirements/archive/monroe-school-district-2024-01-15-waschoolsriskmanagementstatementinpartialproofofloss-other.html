Claim Number: 24-40390

WASHINGTON SCHOOLS RISK MANAGEMENT POOL
SWORN STATEMENT IN PARTIAL PROOF OF LOSS

At the time of loss WSRMP covered Monroe School District against loss by all risk of physical damage to

WSRMP member district property according to the terms and conditions of the coverage agreement and all
endorsements, transfers and assignments attached thereto.

1. Time and Origin: A property loss occurred on or about 1/15/2024. The cause and origin of the said
loss was: Freeze Claims, pipes froze causing damage to fire suppression system.

2. Occupancy: The building described, or containing the property described, was occupied at the time of
loss as follows, and for no other purpose whatever: N/A

3. Title and Interest: At the time of the loss the interest of the district in the property described herein
was one owner. No other person or persons had any interest therein or encumbrance thereon except:
None.

4. Changes: Since the Coverage Agreement was issued there has been no assignment thereof, or change

of interest, use, occupancy, possession, location or exposure of the property described, except: None.

5: Total Coverage: The total amount of coverage upon the property described by the Coverage
Agreement was, at the time of the loss, replacement value, besides which there was no policy or other
contract of insurance, written or oral, valid or invalid.

6. The Partial Actual Cash Value of said property at the time of loss was SNA

7. The Partial Loss was $185,000.00
8. Deductible S 0.00

9. The Partial Amount Claimed is $185,000.00

Statement of District: The said loss did not originate by any act design or procurement on the part of the district, or
this affiant; nothing has been done by or with privity or consent of the district or this affiant, to violate the
conditions of the coverage agreement, or render it void; no articles are mentioned herein but such as were
destroyed or damaged at the time of said loss; no property saved has in any manner been concealed, and no attempt
to deceive the Pool, as to the extent of said loss, has in any manner been made. Any other information that may be
required will be furnished and considered a part of this proof.
