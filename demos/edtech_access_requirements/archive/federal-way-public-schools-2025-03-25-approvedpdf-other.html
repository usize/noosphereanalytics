Administrative Policies and Procedures Series: 4000 — Community Relations

DISTRIBUTION OF MATERIALS: 4060

The District recognizes that valuable social, recreational, and educational opportunities are
available to families and students through nonprofit organizations and governmental entities
providing services for students.

Any nonprofit group or governmental entity wishing to distribute informational material must
submit information about activities for students for possible distribution through district channels,
but any information distributed must meet certain standards and be approved according to the
procedures accompanying this policy. The primary purpose of the standards is to prevent the
exploitation of students by individuals or groups.

No gifts or materials to students, staff, or schools shall be accepted that promote a religious or
political point of view. No gifts or materials to students, staff, or schools shall be accepted that
promote a commercial product without approval of the District, and no such gift or material shall
benefit a District employee or Board Member in violation of District policy or legal restrictions.
