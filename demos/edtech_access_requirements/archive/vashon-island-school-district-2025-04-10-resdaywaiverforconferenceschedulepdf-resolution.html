VASHON ISLAND SCHOOL DISTRICT #402
King County, Washington
Resolution No. 820

A RESOLUTION of the Board of Directors of Vashon Island School District #402, King County,
Washington, that the Vashon Island School District requests for WAIVER from the State Board of
Education.

WHEREAS, the Board of Directors of the Vashon Island School District #402 supports the Vashon School
District’s plans for parent/teacher/student conferences, consisting of 2.5 days in autumn at Chautauqua
Elementary School, and 2.5 days in spring at Vashon Island High School, McMurray Middle School, and
Chautauqua Elementary for the 2025-2026, 2026-2027, and 2027-2028 school years, and

WHEREAS, the conference day schedule shall consist of 175 days at Chautauqua Elementary School and
177.5 days of instructional time at McMurray Middle School and Vashon Island High School, respectively,
rather than the 180 days specified in WAC 180-16-215, while maintaining the required annual average of
instructional hour offerings (RCW 28A.150.220 and WAC 180-16-215),

THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the Board of Directors of the Vashon Island School District #402
that a request be made to the State Board of Education for a waiver of:

WAC 180-16-215 Minimum 180-day school year.

APPROVED by the Board of Directors of Vashon Island School District #402, King County, Washington, in
a meeting held on April 10, 2025.

Chair, Board of Directors
