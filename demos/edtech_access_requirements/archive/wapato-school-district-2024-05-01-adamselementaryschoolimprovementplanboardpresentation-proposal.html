Adams Elementary School
Im provement Plan 23-24

Our purpose at Adams is to empower and motivate each student
with the social and academic skills needed to achieve at grade
level and beyond, while working in partnership with our Wapato
families and community.



The Goals

5a. SY 20232024 SMARTIE Goal #1:

Students in three identified subgroups will increase their average performance
on Galileo ELA benchmark assessments from level 1 to level 2. The three
subgroups are: Native American Learners, English Language Learners, and
Special Needs Students. These three subgroups of students have not met the
WSIF benchmark for school improvement. The Galileo benchmarks are given
three times a year, growth will be measured from the first to the third
assessment.

5b. SY 2023-2024 SMARTIE Goal #2:

Students in three identified subgroups will increase their average performance

on Galileo Mathematics benchmark assessments from level 1 to level 2. The
three subgroups are: Native American Learners, English Language Learners, and
Special Needs Students. These three subgroups of students have not met the
WSIF benchmark for school improvement. The Galileo benchmarks are given
three times a year, growth will be measured from the first to the third

assessment.

5c. SY 2023-2024 SMARTIE Goal #3:

TBIP Students will exit the program at a rate of 10% per year from our current
exit rate of 4% in 2023. The annual WIDA Assessment, delivered in the spring of
2023 will be the instrument used to measure success in regards to this goal.
Efforts to meet this goal address needs on ELL students, enrolled Title III
students, and twice identified special needs students. These three subgroups of
students have not met the WSIF benchmark for school improvement.

5d. SY 2023-2024 SMARTIE Goal #4:

Adams will reduce the number of suspensions to less than 20 from a total of 24
for the 23-24 school year with proportionality being maintained in suspensions
relative to our ASB as a whole.
