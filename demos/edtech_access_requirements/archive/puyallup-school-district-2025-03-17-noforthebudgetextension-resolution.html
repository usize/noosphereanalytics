RESOLUTION NO. 107 2024-25

A RESOLUTION OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS OF PUYALLUP SCHOOL
DISTRICT NO. 3, PIERCE COUNTY, WASHINGTON FOR A BUDGET
EXTENSION OF THE 2024-25 GENERAL FUND.

BE IT RESOLVED BY THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS OF PUYALLUP SCHOOL
DISTRICT NO. 3, Pierce County, Washington, as follows:

WHEREAS, WAC 392-123-071 through 392-123-074 and WAC 392-123-078 and 392-
123-079 permits a first class district to file an increase of the amount of appropriation from any
fund, and

WHEREAS, the General Fund of Puyallup School District No. 3 has made additional
expenditures for staffing costs and operating expenses after enrollment was higher than
projected which will require an increase in appropriation of $3,948,048.00 and

WHEREAS, the General Fund beginning fund balance plus anticipated revenues is
sufficient to provide for the additional expenditures,

THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED the Board of Directors of the Puyallup School District
No. 3, Pierce County, Washington, hereby petition OSPI to increase the 2024-25 General Fund
appropriation amount by $3,948,048.00 from $422,051,952.00 to $426,000,000.00.

APPROVED by the Board of Directors of Puyallup School District No. 3, Pierce County,
Washington, in a regularly scheduled meeting thereof held on the 17th day of March 2025.

PUYALLUP SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 3
Pierce County, Washington
