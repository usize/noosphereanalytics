Standard 3: Curriculum, Instruction, and
Assessment

Goal 1: Create a coherent, supportive educational
environment that enables all students to thrive,
building a district culture devoted to learning,
equity, and academic excellence.

This goal will be achieved by aligning MTSS with
strategic priorities, enhancing instructional
practices through collaborative rounds, engaging
students in authentic feedback, and fostering a
culture of learning and continuous improvement
across the district.



1. Align MTSS with Strategic Plan

e)

Ensure MTSS practices are fully aligned with the
district’s strategic priorities, establishing a clear
system-wide focus on supporting student
academic achievement and well-being.

2. Enhancing Instructional Rounds

e)

Connect instructional rounds to the Prek-12 school
improvement plans to foster coherent and
consistent practices across all grade levels.
Promote collaboration among educators to share
best practices and improve instructional
approaches.



3. Authentic Student Engagement

O

Launch and support student advisories, including "Dr. K-C’s
Coffee Counsel," to engage student board representatives in
authentic feedback and input on their educational experience.
Develop initiatives like "Walking in the Shoes of Students" to
provide leaders and staff with firsthand perspectives of students'
daily successes and challenges.

4. Promote a Culture Focused on Student Learning

O

Foster values, beliefs, and behaviors that build an organizational
culture dedicated to student growth and achievement.

Establish clear project management systems for operational and
leadership teams to understand and support #OneOakHarbor,
enabling a collaborative and solutions-oriented approach to
continuous improvement.



Standard 6: Meaningful Engagement of
Families and Community

Goa! 2: Foster a culture of positivity and support
within the #OneOakHarbor community by focusing
on meaningful, reciprocal, and mutually beneficial
engagement with families and the community to
enhance the academic success and well-being of
every student.

This goal will be achieved by continuing to build
positive, trusting relationships that strengthen the
overall HOneOakHarbor community and support
student success and well-being.



Objectives and Evidence of Success:

1. Community Engagement
o Strengthen connections with families, local organizations, and

community members to support the district's goals and student
outcomes.

2. Community Presence
o Active participation in community events, social organization
presentations, and outreach initiatives.
o Form partnerships with the local Chamber of Commerce, City Council,
and other stakeholders to reinforce community ties.
3. Promotion of #OneOakHarbor Values
o Promote the strategic plan’s foundational goals and core values to
align the community's efforts under the #OneOakHarbor identity.

o Encourage collaboration and a unified approach to achieving district
objectives.

4. Utilizing Community Feedback

o Collect and analyze feedback from community surveys to drive
decision-making and improve engagement strategies.

o Use insights from community committees, such as the District Parent
Advisory Council (DPAC), Citizens Facilities Advisory Committee
(CFAC), and Budget Advisory Committee, to inform district actions
