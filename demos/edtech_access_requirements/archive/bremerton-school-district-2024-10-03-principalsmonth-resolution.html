Resolution 2024/2025-09
Proclaiming October 2024 as “School Principals Month”

WHEREAS, school principals and assistant principals play an integral role in the education and
growth of children in elementary, middle, and secondary schools across the state of Washington; and

WHEREAS, school administrators are responsible for promoting education and building
relationships with parents and teachers to ensure each child receives services that meet their needs to excel
in the classroom; and

WHEREAS, these educational leaders set the academic tone for their schools and work
collaboratively with teachers to develop and maintain high curriculum standards, develop mission
statements, and set performance goals and objectives for schools to achieve educational excellence, and

WHEREAS, the National Association of Secondary School Principals, the National Association
of Elementary School Principals, the American Federation of School Administrators and the Association of
Washington School Principals have set aside this October to honor the hard work and dedication of
America’s principals; and

WHEREAS, each year our state Governor Jay Inslee supports school principals and assistant
principals by proclaiming October to be School Principals Month in Washington; and

WHEREAS, in celebration of “National Principals Month,” the Bremerton School District wishes
to join with these national and state organizations, as well as the State of Washington, to honor all of our
school principals and assistant principals, and recognize the importance of school leadership in ensuring
that every child has access to a high-quality education.

NOW, THEREFORE, be it resolved that the Bremerton School District Board of Directors hereby
proclaims October, 2024, as School Principals Month in the Bremerton School District; and encourages
the staff of the Bremerton School District to honor and recognize the contributions of school principals and
assistant principals in the success of students in Bremerton elementary and secondary schools.

Signed this 3"? day of October, 2024.
