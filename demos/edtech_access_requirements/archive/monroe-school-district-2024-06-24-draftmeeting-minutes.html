5.03 OpenSciEd Middle School Science Instructional Materials Adoption
Presenter:

e Jennifer Murphy - Director of Curriculum and Assessment
Description:

e
It has been over 10 years since our last science adoption and our current
middle school science materials are not aligned to the Next Generation
Science Standards. Teachers have been working diligently to curate materials
or repurpose existing materials to better align them to the standards. This
adoption will support them in working more efficiently and effectively to meet
the needs of students. In addition, it will support our efforts in becoming a
district-wide PLC and aligning both materials and practices across buildings.

We are asking the board to review the OpenSciEd Instructional Materials for
grades 6-8 as put forth by the Instructional Materials Committee. These are
Inquiry-based curricular materials aligned to the Next Generation Science
Standards (NGSS). These instructional resources are part of the Creative
Commons license which means a continued low cost (online resources are
free) for yearly access to resources. Teacher editions and supplies to support
the curriculum are needed to fully implement the curriculum. This includes
science kits that contain both consumable and non-consumable products.
Consumable products will have to be replenished annually. Kendall Hunt has
teacher editions as well as student workbooks but these will not need to be
re-purchased on an annual basis. This will be the first reading of the
materials and there are many links embedded in the slide presentation for
the board to look at before and after the presentation.
