AMENDMENT to

Interlocal Agreement between WSIPC and Blaine School District

This Amendment to the Interlocal Agreement (this “Amendment”) is made and entered into as of the 29th
day of July, 2024 (the “Effective Date”) by and between Washington School Information Processing
Cooperative (“WSIPC”) and Blaine School District (“District”).

WHEREAS, Blaine School District and WSIPC are parties to the Interlocal Agreement dated April 18,
2006 (“the Agreement’); and

WHEREAS, WSIPC offers the IEP Online Connect to its clients, and Blaine School District wishes to
amend the Agreement to add the IEP Online Connect Solution; and

WHEREAS, Blaine School District and WSIPC wish to amend the Agreement by mutual agreement to
modify the provisions as set forth herein; and

NOW THEREFORE, in consideration of the foregoing recitals and the mutual promises set forth herein,
the parties hereto agree as follows:

1. In accordance with the terms of the Agreement, this Agreement is hereby amended to add the IEP
Online Connect.

2. Exhibit B Participating Entity Amendment to IEP Online Sublicense is hereby included and
incorporated into the Agreement during the Term of the Agreement.

3. As consideration for the addition of the IEP Online Connect, WSIPC will bill Blaine School
District for fees as outlined in Exhibit M.

4, Except to the extent expressly amended hereby, all other terms and conditions of the agreement
shall continue in full force and effect.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Amendment I, effective on the date above.

IEP Online Connect™

OVERVIEW

PCG is pleased to present this IEP Online Connect pricing for your review to Blaine School
District. The proposal outlines the pricing for the IEP Online Connect parent portal system
including system software, implementation, configuration, support, training, and system hosting
services. In this work, PCG will partner with the Blaine School District to ensure that your
district is successfully using IEP Online Connect.

SUMMARY

IEP Online Connect is an intuitive technology platform designed to encourage and support
communication with special education families. Our IEP Online Connect platform provides
families a secure ‘one-stop-shopping’ experience for accessing and signing student-related
documents accessible on most any device in real or near real-time.

SYSTEM
IEP Online Connect Software/Solution:
Software System
- Dual Authentication to ensure secure confidential sharing of information
- Option to integrate with IEP Online Electronic Signature functionality
- Share electronic Draft and Finalized documents
- Integration with IEP Online system
- Hosted Services with WSIPC

IMPLEMENTATION RESOURCES
PCG will assign a Project Manager to coordinate the implementation of IEP Online Connect with Blaine
School District. The responsibilities of the Project Manager will include:

- District Kickoff

- Central Train-the-Trainer Model

- District Support

Blaine School District will assign a Project Manager to manage project deliverables of the
district. The responsibilities of the District Project Manager will include:

- Manage project

- Confirm district readiness

- Coordinate system implementation, support, and training with PCG Project Manager

AMENDEMENT to
MASTER AGREEMENT
PARTICIPATING ENTITY SUBLICENSE

This amendment to Exhibit B of the Agreement (the “Exhibit B Amendment”) is entered into between
the Washington State Information Processing Cooperative (“WSIPC”), Public Consulting Group LLC
(PCG), with its corporate office located at 148 State Street, 10" Floor, Boston, MA 02108, and its
successors and assigns (“PCG”); and Blaine School District, with offices located at 765 H St Blaine, WA
98230 (“Sub-licensee”) and is made effective as of 29th day of July, 2024 (“Effective Date”).

WHEREAS, PCG offers IEP Online Connect to its clients, and Sub-licensee wishes to amend the General
License Agreement (“License Agreement”) to add the IEP Online Connect solution.

NOW THEREFORE, in consideration of the foregoing recitals and the mutual promises set forth herein,
the parties hereto agree as follows:

1. The second sentence of Section 1(a) of the License Agreement is hereby amended to add the
following additional services (during the Term of the Agreement) at the end of the sentence:

“and PCG’s IEP Online Connect.”

2. Except to the extent expressly amended hereby, all other terms and conditions of the License
Agreement shall continue in full force and effect.

3. This Exhibit B Amendment shall be construed and interpreted in accordance with the laws of the
State of Washington without giving effect to its principles of conflict of laws.
