Tuesday, March 25, 2025
Regular Board Meeting - 6:00 p.m.
The Federal Way School Board will hold a Regular Meeting scheduled for 6:00 p.m. at Federal Way High
School, 30611 16th Avenue S., Federal Way, WA 98003.

Board of Directors: President Dr. Jennifer Jones, Vice President Trudy Davis, Legislative Liaison
Luckisha Phillips, Quentin Morris, Joan Marie Murphy
Scholar Representatives: Yandel Barron-Toledo, Sofia Majors, Izabelle Owens

Following current Washington State guidelines for board meetings, this meeting will be held in-person
with an option to attend remotely.

ATTENDING THE BOARD MEETING IN-PERSON:
The board meeting will be held at Federal Way High School, 30611 16th Avenue S., Federal Way, WA
98003.

ATTENDING THE BOARD MEETING REMOTELY (via Zoom):
https://lwps.zoom.us/j/91 912008083
Join via audio: +1 253 215 8782 US (Tacoma), +1 253 205 0468 US

PUBLIC COMMENT:

You are welcome to provide public comment in-person or submit to the Secretary of the Board at
superintendent@fwps.org to be shared with the board. Please find more information within the public
comment agenda item or public comment notice on the featured page within BoardDocs.
