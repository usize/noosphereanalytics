TO: Board of Directors
THROUGH: Donna Colosky, Superintendent
FROM: Garth Steedman, Assistant Superintendent Finance, Operations, & Human Resources

SUBJECT Final Acceptance of the Mountain View Middle School Camera upgrades as performed by Cable
& Technology Services

DATE: December 12, 2024

ACTION:
Recommend final acceptance by the Bremerton School District (BSD) Board of Directors of the Mountain
View Middle School camera upgrades project as performed by Cable & Technology Services.

BACKGROUND:

On August 1, 2024 the Board approved a contract with Cable & Technology Services in the amount of
$243,157.14 plus Washington State sales tax. The project involved the purchase and installation of new
Verkada cameras for the building.

BENEFIT TO THE DISTRICT:
The Board’s approval of final acceptance will allow a Notice of Completion to be filed and subsequent
final project close-out actions to occur.

COST TO THE DISTRICT:
Total cost of the Mountain View Middle School camera upgrades project are as follows:

Original Cable & Technology Services contract S 243,157.14
Washington State Sales Tax S 22,370.46
Total Project Cost S 265,527.60
