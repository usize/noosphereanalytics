INTERDISTRICT AGREEMENT FOR EDUCATIONAL SERVICES TO
SUPPORT STUDENTS WITH DISABILITIES

THIS AGREEMENT is made as of the 5 day of March 2025, by and between PUYALLUP
SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 3, Pierce County, Washington and STEILACOOM HIST. SCHOOL
DISTRICT NO. 001.

WITNESSETH

WHEREAS, the STEILACOOM HIST. SCHOOL DISTRICT is authorized by Chapter 28.155
RCW to participate in an interdistrict agreement to secure such an appropriate educational opportunity for
its’ resident students with disabilities, and

WHEREAS, the STEILACOOM HIST. SCHOOL DISTRICT has determined that certain students
with disabilities whose education presents unusual programming needs by reason of severity of disability,
multiplicity of disabilities, or other factors, may advantageously attend and be enrolled in the PUYALLUP
SCHOOL DISTRICT during the school year 2024-2025 and,

WHEREAS, the PUYALLUP SCHOOL DISTRICT is willing to enter into this agreement with the
STEILACOOM HIST. SCHOOL DISTRICT, and to provide its programming for certain students with
disabilities that have completed the enrollment service request form and that reside within the borders of
STEILACOOM HIST. SCHOOL DISTRICT in the manner and upon the terms and conditions hereinafter set
forth.

NOW, THEREFORE, for and in consideration of the promises and undertakings herein contained, the
parties hereto agree as follows:

1. PUYALLUP SCHOOL DISTRICT agrees that it will commence educational services
on September 4, 2024 and intends to complete the school year no later than June 17, 2025.

2: All program staff and facilities for the cooperative shall be District, except when contracted
services are needed.

3. The STEILACOOM HIST. SCHOOL DISTRICT has determined that it is less costly to
send students to the PUYALLUP SCHOOL DISTRICT for educational services than to
initiate a similar program(s) within the STETILACOOM HIST. SCHOOL DISTRICT.

4. The STEILACOOM HIST. SCHOOL DISTRICT has determined that it cannot provide an
appropriate educational program(s) for certain students with disabilities and maintains
ultimate responsibility for the education of its student(s) the PUYALLUP SCHOOL
DISTRICT agrees to provide such educational services to the students that are identified by
the completed enrollment service request form. Students attend STEILACOOM HIST.
SCHOOL DISTRICT schedule until notified of the acceptance of the Interdistrict
Agreement and its start date as evidenced by the completed enrollment service request form.

5. The STEILACOOM HIST. SCHOOL DISTRICT requests, and the PUYALLUP
SCHOOL DISTRICT agrees that the students with completed service enrollment forms or
as subsequently modified by agreement between the Executive Director of Special Education
or its assigned designee of the PUYALLUP SCHOOL DISTRICT and the
STEILACOOM HIST. SCHOOL DISTRICT, will be served. The students will be
considered full-time students in the PUYALLUP SCHOOL DISTRICT while participating
in the program, unless otherwise specified. Students in Grades K-12 will be 1.0 FTE and
students attending preschool will be 0.8 FTE.

The PUYALLUP SCHOOL DISTRICT will administer the program(s) in accordance with
the rules and regulations of Chapter 28A.155 RCW, Chapter 392-172A WAC and
PUYALLUP SCHOOL DISTRICT policies and practices.

The PUYALLUP SCHOOL DISTRICT will follow OSPI’s Special Education November
Federal Count Application User Guide for the November Federal Child Count Application.
Since a written agreement exists between the PUYALLUP SCHOOL DISTRICT and the
STEILACOOM HIST. SCHOOL DISTRICT to provide special education services to a
student, the PUYALLUP SCHOOL DISTRICT will count the student for this report and
the STEILACOOM HIST. SCHOOL DISTRICT will be displayed in the Certification
table.

The PUYALLUP SCHOOL DISTRICT will follow OSPI’s Guidance for Student
Transfers: Choice Transfers, Interdistrict Agreements, and Standard Choice Transfer System.
Since an interdistrict agreement exists between the PUYALLUP SCHOOL DISTRICT and
the STEILACOOM HIST. SCHOOL DISTRICT the PUYALLUP SCHOOL
DISTRICT will report the student(s) on the P-223 and P-223H forms as a resident of the
STEILACOOM HIST. SCHOOL DISTRICT.

The STEILACOOM HIST. SCHOOL DISTRICT retains responsibility for
providing transportation to and from each student’s home and place of learning within
or without the student’s resident district.

The PUYALLUP SCHOOL DISTRICT agrees to provide the educational services
described within the agreement provided that the PUYALLUP SCHOOL DISTRICT
residents do not subsidize students from other districts; therefore, the STEILACOOM HIST.
SCHOOL DISTRICT, agrees to reimburse the PUYALLUP SCHOOL DISTRICT for all
costs not reimbursed by the State of Washington, or by any other specific grant. The
PUYALLUP SCHOOL DISTRICT will submit an invoice to the STEILACOOM
HIST. SCHOOL DISTRICT three times each school year, approximately October,
February, and July. and if required, other forms setting forth the costs and expenses
incurred by the STEILACOOM HIST. SCHOOL DISTRICT. The
STEILACOOM HIST. SCHOOL DISTRICT will remit unto the PUYALLUP
SCHOOL DISTRICT within 30 days from receipt of each invoice.

The PUYALLUP SCHOOL DISTRICT will provide, and retain title to, all assets used in
the program. The PUYALLUP SCHOOL DISTRICT will assume all general liabilities
associated with the program. The STEILACOOM HIST. SCHOOL DISTRICT will
assume the liability to pay the PUYALLUP SCHOOL DISTRICT for each fractional or
full-time equivalent student and also to assume any liability resulting from specific actions by
the STEILACOOM HIST. SCHOOL DISTRICT.

The PUYALLUP SCHOOL DISTRICT agrees to submit final reports and evaluations of
the students with completed enrollment service request forms and the PUYALLUP
SCHOOL DISTRICT and the STEILACOOM HIST. SCHOOL DISTRICT agree
to assist each other with such reports and evaluations.

This contract shall apply for the period from March 5, 2025 through August 31, 2025.
The STEILACOOM HIST. SCHOOL DISTRICT will notify the PUYALLUP SCHOOL

DISTRICT in writing within 20 days if the student(s) change district of residence and are no
longer residents of STEILACOOM HIST. SCHOOL DISTRICT.
