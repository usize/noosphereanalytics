2022/2022P Electronic

Resources and Internet Safety

(Encouraged)

e Purpose: Outlines positive and effective digital citizenship for
students and staff
e Revisions:
o Recognizes the long term impact of digital presence
o Requires digital storage, transmission, and policies to conform to
FERPA and CIPA as well as support the mission of the district
o Adds destruction of equipment and unauthorized access to
unacceptable use
o Includes not sharing personally identifiable information with
internet safety
o Now includes guidelines for use of artificial intelligence

3110/3110P Qualifications of

Attendance and Placement

(Encouraged)

e Purpose: New procedure that may allow for early entrance
to kindergarten if criteria are met

e Revisions:

o Applicants must be 5 prior to November 1

o Must be residents of the school district

o Must meet screening requirements for social emotional, early
literacy, math, physical and fine motor skills as well as effective
communication skills

o Aselection committee will make blind recommendations based
on student evidence

o Monitoring of students throughout the first trimester

3131P District Attendance

Area Transfers (Discretionary)

e Purpose: Outlines transfer criteria within district
schools
e Revisions:
o Clarifies that transfers for children of school full-time
employees will be granted at the school to which the
employee is assigned

3141 Nonresident Students

(Essential)

e Purpose: Establishes guidelines for accepting
students that do not live In our school district
e Revisions:
o Aligns with state law 28A.225.RCW and now includes
the criteria that acceptance cannot displace a child who
is a resident of the district
