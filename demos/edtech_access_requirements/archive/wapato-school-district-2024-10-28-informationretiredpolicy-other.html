Policy No. 2210
2000 — Instruction

RETIRED: There is no legal requirement RCW or WAC that requires this is
a needed policy or information. This is no longer a WSSDA Model policy.

GRADE ORGANIZATION

Instructional programs will be coordinated between each grade and between levels of schools.
Grade organization may vary among the schools as individual schools and communities use
shared-decision making in recommending to the Board their preferred grade organization. The

Board will make the final decision to assure smooth transitions between the various buildings
as students progress.
