Oak Harbor Public Schools Board of Directors, Island County, Washington, will hold a regular meeting. The board president will
call the meeting to order.
Background
Policy 1400 states in part, "Regular meetings are held at 6:00 p.m. on the second Monday and last Monday of each month in the
district boardroom or at other times and places as determined by the presiding officer or by majority vote of the board. An
agenda of the business to be transacted will be posted on the district website not less than twenty-four (24) hours in advance of
the published start time of the meeting.
If the board holds regular meetings at places other than the district boardroom or if the board adjourns to times other than a
regular meeting time, the board will provide notice of the meeting in the same manner as provided for special meetings. All
regular meetings of the board are held within the district boundaries."

2.04 Resolution 23-22 Out of Endorsed Area
Assignment
Meeting :

Jun 10, 2024 - Regular Board Meeting &
Executive Session

Category :

2. Consent Agenda

Type :

Action (Consent)

Public Content
Oak Harbor Public School recruits teachers with academic majors in the subjects they teach. However, changes by the Office of
Superintendent of Public Instruction (OSPI) and the Professional Educator Standards Board (PESB) have increased the
complexity of certification compliance. The factors that have increased our number of teachers requiring Borad approval to teach
out of their endorsement are as follows:
Co-teaching model with special education paired with general education teachers
Alignment with State Course Codes
Complexstate endorsementrequirements
OHI Math/Science and English Language Arts/Social Studiesassignments
Regardless of these requirements, the teachers on the attached list are proficient in the subject areas assigned as determinedvia
annual evaluations.

The Crescent Harbor Elementary School Construction project opened for bids on April 17, 2024. The advertisement was posted
from April 17-April 24. A site walk was conducted on April 29, 2024, at which all potential bidders were required to attend. The
bidding window for contractors to submit bids closed on June 4, 2024, at 3:00 p.m. Six contractors submitted bids for the project.
The lowest qualified bidder was Tiger Construction.
