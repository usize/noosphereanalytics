Puyallup School District No. 3
Resolution No. 106 2024-2025
Cancellation of Warrants

WHEREAS, in compliance with RCW 39.56.0040, that interest-bearing
warrants not presented within one year of the date of their call, or other
warrants not presented within one year of issue, shall be canceled; and

WHEREAS, such warrants have been identified in the attachment to this
resolution;

NOW THEREFORE be it resolved that the Treasurer, County of Pierce, State
of Washington, be notified of the passage of this resolution by the Board of
Directors of the Puyallup School District, and that he be petitioned to cancel
such warrants.

ADOPTED by the Board of Directors at a regular meeting thereof held this 1*
day of April 2025, the following directors being present and voting.

PUYALLUP SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 3
BOARD OF DIRECTORS

Maddie Names, President
