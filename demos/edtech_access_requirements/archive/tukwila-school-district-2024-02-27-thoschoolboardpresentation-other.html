Goal #1 - Math Goal: In partnership with students & staff, Thorndyke Elementary School will close
the gap between the state average of 44.2% to 22% or more in mathematics for students who are
Black/African American, Latinx, students served with IEPs, and/or Multilingual Learners by June 18,
2024

Goal #2 - Reading Goal: In partnership with students & teachers, Thorndyke Elementary School will
close the gap between the state average of 49.3% passing by 24.1% or more in reading for students
who are Black/African American, LatinX, students served with IEPs, and/or Multilingual Learners by
June 18, 2024.

Goal #3 - Science Goal: In partnership with students & teachers, Thorndyke Elementary School will
close the gap between the state average of 50% passing by 20% or more in science for students who
are Black/African American, LatinX, students served with IEPs and/or Multilingual Learners by June
18, 2024

Goal #4 - SEL Goal: In partnership with students & teachers, Thorndyke Elementary School will
support targeted social learning interventions for students who are Black/African American, LatinX,
students served with IEPs and/or Multilingual Learners by June 15, 2024.

Race and Equity Plan Update - Identified Draft Goals

We will be able to identify the cultures/ethnicities/races and languages of all of
our students

- We will analyze data trends in school discipline, monthly, and help to
implement at least one method for making data less racially predictive by
2024-2025

- Lesson plans for Substitutes that are culturally relevant

- We will remove all racist, culturally diminishing, white supremacist reading
material from the library by September 2025-2025

- We will provide positive cultural reflection / representation of all
historically/continually marginalized groups through the use of classroom
resources (including, but not limited to videos, books, assignments (topics and
materials), class discussions (both in topic and language), website and

images/posted visuals) on a rotating monthly basis throughout the school year
