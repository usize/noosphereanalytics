Final Recommendation

Build a new Meridian Middle School as a 5-8 school

replacement (moving band class)

e MHS stadium work (new lighting and repairs only)

the spring for the community to vote on in November 2024.


Financial Impact

Bonds are for building
o Bonds cannot pay for day-to-day
costs of running the district
o Bonds require supermajority, 60%
of votes to be approved
Tax impact for $80.5 million bond
o $1.69 per $1,000 of assessed
value
The estimated total combined tax rate
would be $4.69 per $1,000 of assessed
value (includes levies and bonds).
Ballot measure in November 2024, if
approved by the community, additional
bond tax would begin in 2025.
