Memo

Public Schools

To: Board of Directors
From: Patrick Harrington, Assistant Superintendent HR & Operations
Subject: Resolution 23-22, Out of Endorsed Area Assignment

Date: June 10, 2024

Oak Harbor Public Schools recruits teachers with academic majors in the subjects they teach. However,
changes by the Office of Superintendent of Public Instruction (OSPI) and the Professional Educator
Standards Board (PESB) have increased the complexity of certification compliance. The factors that have
increased our number of teacher’s requiring Board approval to teach out of their endorsement area are
as follows:

e Co-teaching model with special education paired with general education teachers

e Alignment with State Course Codes

e Complex state endorsement requirements

e OHI Math/Science and English Language Arts/Social Studies assignments
Regardless of these requirements, the teachers on this list are proficient in the subject areas assigned as
determined via annual evaluations.

Exceptions to classroom teaching assignments are allowed as stipulated in WAC 180-82-110, Exceptions to
Classroom Teacher Assignment Policy, and, additionally for Special Education, in WAC 392-172-202, Rules
for the Provision of Special Education. In order to assign teachers in subjects other than their endorsed
areas, the district must determine that the teachers have the competencies to be effective in subjects
other than their endorsed areas. Such teaching assignments are approved by this formal approval of the
local school board. Resolution 23-22 is attached.

The teachers assigned to out of endorsement areas are:

WHEREAS, the Oak Harbor School District analyzed existing staff qualifications and could find no one
currently employed with the appropriate endorsement to be assigned to teach during that time without
severely disrupting the ongoing educational program in the district; and

WHEREAS, the Oak Harbor School District does find it necessary to assign the teachers listed above to
teach classes in areas other than the endorsed areas.

NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Oak Harbor School District Board of Directors grants a waiver
for Edvie Duarte, Corey Potter, Laura Voelker, David Smith, and Lindsey Smith to teach outside of their

endorsed area.
