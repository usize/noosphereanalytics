Regular Board Meeting
October 22, 2024

Responses to the Board’s questions/comments on Agenda Item 9.C — Risk Management Program for the October 22, 2024
Regular Board Meeting:

1. Agenda Item 9.C — Risk Management Program

1. On Page 2, under areas of concern, should cyber-attacks be added?
Aaron Draganov: The technology team remains highly vigilant and is taking appropriate measures to
mitigate risks. While these measures are not publicly shared, you are encouraged to meet with team
members to learn more about the steps being taken.

2. On Page 7, should “automotive loss run” be “insurance loss run”?
Aaron Draganov: We specifically highlighted the automotive loss run as it is an emerging area of concern
that requires close monitoring.

3. What steps are being taken to reduce the impact of any cyber-attacks?
Aaron Draganov: For details on the measures being implemented, please meet with a member of the
technology team. High-level actions currently include new multi-factor authentication requirements for
certain infrastructure access levels.
