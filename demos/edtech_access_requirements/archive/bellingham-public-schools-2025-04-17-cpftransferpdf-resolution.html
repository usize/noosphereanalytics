RESOLUTION NO. 10-25

REQUEST TO TRANSFER FUNDS FROM THE CAPITAL PROJECTS FUND
TO THE GENERAL FUND

WHEREAS, RCW 28A.320.330(2)(f)(ii) requires school districts to transfer to the
district’s general fund the portion of the capital projects fund used for costs associated
with the application and modernization of technology systems for operations and
instruction including, but not limited to, the ongoing fees for online applications,
subscriptions, or software licenses, including upgrades and incidental services, and
ongoing training related to the installation and integration of these products and services,
and

WHEREAS, the Bellingham School District has a committed qualifying payment
from the general fund totaling $133,559.11.

THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, by the Board of Directors of Bellingham School
District No. 501, Whatcom County, directing the Whatcom County Treasurer to transfer
funds from the capital projects fund to the general fund in the amount listed above.

ADOPTED THIS 17" DAY OF APRIL 2025.
