VASHON ISLAND

SCHOOL DISTRICT

The Vashon Promise: Every student is welcomed, known, and treasured, and
graduates confident and competent to thrive in a future they imagine.

GENERAL FUND

Fund balance is more in the General Fund this month compared to prior year to date.
We have slightly lower revenues and slightly higher expenditures compared to the
same month last year. Transfer of credit card revenue from General Fund into ASB is in
process.

Revenue in January includes basic apportionment payment based on actual AAFTE, and
local revenue from food service and fee collection.

Expenditures came in at 41.31% which is .69% less than normalized projection of 42%.
Expenditures are higher than this month in the previous year by 1.84% or
approximately $500,000.

CAPTIAL PROJECTS FUND

Revenues are lower than this time last year based upon tax collection. Transfer of tech
expenditures and salaries have been verified and will be posted in March. Nothing to
report on expenditure.

DEBT SERVICE FUND

The fund is performing as expected.

ASB FUND

Revenue is lower than in the previous year and expenditure is higher than the same
month last year. GoFan reconciliation is complete and credit card revenue
reconciliation is in process.

TRANSPORTATION VEHICLE FUND

Fund is performing as expected.

Analysis of the district’s finances are presented periodically at Board meetings and work
sessions. If you have specific questions, please reach out to Cassie Zizah.
