~ ELEMENTARY SCHOOL

The mission at Simcoe Elementary is to cultivate the
potential in every student to flourish as a global citizen.

ELEMENTARY SCHOOL

1. Safety: To maintain an emotionally and physically safe learning
environment, along with a positive climate for all stakeholders.

2. Community Engagement: To maintain and strengthen school and
community partnerships in order to support learning for all students.

3. Academic Achievement: To set high expectations and increase academic
achievement for all students, and to provide staff professional
development to support student growth.
