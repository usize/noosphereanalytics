§¢ Tukwila

SCHOOL DISTRICT 4640 S. 144th St. Tukwila, WA 98168 | (206) 901-8000 | tukwila.wednet.edu
October 8, 2024
Representatives Bergquist, Hackney, and Gregerson,
In the Tukwila School District, we have many families and students who are immigrants from around the
world. Many are new to the United States and do not have US citizen status but may have permanent
resident status. It would be very valuable to the Tukwila School District to have representatives from these
communities on the school board to help oversee the education system for their children.
Currently, state law requires school board members to be US citizens. In 2021, the Senate heard SB 5340
which would have allowed school board members to be permanent residents. The bill passed out of Senate
Early Learning and K-12 with a do-pass recommendation. It then died in Rules and never got to the floor.

We request that you reintroduce SB 5340 in the current legislative session and work to get it passed into law.

Thank you for your attention to this issue and for your work on behalf of our students.

Tukwila School Board Directors
