Rationale for Activity: Research

General self-efficacy beliefs have been shown to
positively predict academic achievement and school
engagement. Conversely, poor general self-efficacy
beliefs have been associated with school absenteeism
and school burnout.
-Bianchi et al.,2020 99

Rationale for Activity: Research

OSPI defines self-efficacy as the ability to motivate
oneself, persevere, and see oneself as capable.

66 [It is] the skills and mindsets needed to remain engaged,
