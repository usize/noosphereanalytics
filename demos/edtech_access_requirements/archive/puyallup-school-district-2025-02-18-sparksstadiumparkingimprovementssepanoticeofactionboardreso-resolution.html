RESOLUTION No. 98 2024-25

WHEREAS, in compliance with the State Environmental Policy Act ("SEPA"), Chapter
43.21C RCW, and Board Policy No. 6890, notice of the proposed South Hill Support
Campus Improvements Phase 1 (“project’”) was published and comments requested; and

WHEREAS, the Puyallup School District (“district”) prepared an environmental checklist
for the project signed December 3, 2024, and published notice of the Expanded SEPA
Checklist on December 12, 2024, with a 15-day comment period; and

WHEREAS, all comments received during the SEPA Expanded Checklist comment
period were reviewed by the district’s SEPA Responsible Official; and

WHEREAS, the district issued and noticed a Determination of Non-significance (“DNS”)
for the project on January 10, 2025, with a with a 15-day comment period; and

WHEREAS, all comments received during the DNS comment period have been reviewed
by the district’s SEPA Responsible Official; and

WHEREAS, the district has determined to proceed with the project, having fully reviewed
and considered the impacts of the proposed action, alternatives thereto, and any comments
received;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED THAT, the Puyallup School District Board
of Directors hereby authorizes the district superintendent (or his or her designee), on behalf
of the district, subject to and in accordance with all applicable law, to proceed with district
Capital Project #54-01-21, commonly referred to as the Sparks Stadium Parking
Improvements project.

APPROVED this 18" day of February 2025, in regular session.
