Board & Superintendent Goals
2024-2025

1. The Board will review and update the policies within the 1000 series- Legal Status and
Operations in order to provide responsible school district governance in alignment with and
focused on the Board’s priorities.

2. The Board will participate in broad communication of the newly adopted Strategic Plan
2024-2030 to the greater Bremerton School District community, including additional special
meetings for linkage with community stakeholders .

3. The Board, supported by the leadership team, will review the selected metrics in support of
the defined “measures of success” in support of the four goals of the Strategic Plan 2024-2030.

4. The Board will support the process of adoption of a refreshed Bremerton School District Logo
that will include student artist participation along with staff and leadership input.
