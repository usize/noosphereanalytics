BREMERTON SCHOOL DISTRICT 100-C

Administration and Service Center
134.N. Marion Avenue
Bremerton, WA 98312

Resolution 2024/2025-13
Mountain View Camera Upgrade

WHEREAS the Bremerton School District 100-C entered into an agreement with Cable &
Technology Services on August 1, 2024, for the camera upgrades at Mountain View Middle School

WHEREAS Cable & Technology Services has satisfactorily completed said construction in
accordance with the plans and specifications on September 6, 2024; and

WHEREAS district staff have made a final inspection of the work and has verified the work is
complete;

THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Board of Directors of the Bremerton School District
No. 100-C, pursuant to WAC 180-29-160, does hereby accept the camera upgrades at Mountain View
Middle School as final and accepted as of the 12th day of December, 2024.

APPROVED by the Board of Directors of Bremerton School District No. 100-C, Kitsap County,
Washington, in a meeting thereof held on the 12th day of December, 2024.
