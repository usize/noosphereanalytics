NOTICE OF ACTION
PUYALLUP SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 3
Project #54-01-21— Sparks Stadium Parking Improvements

Notice is given under SEPA, RCW 43.21C.080, that Puyallup School District No. 3 (the "District")
took the action described in (2) below on February 18, 2025.

1.

Any action to set aside, enjoin, review, or otherwise challenge such action on the grounds of
noncompliance with the provisions of chapter 43.21C RCW (State Environmental Policy Act)
shall be commenced on or before March 21, 2025.

Description of agency action: Resolution #98 2024-25 of the Board of Directors of the
Puyallup School District No. 3 authorizing the district to proceed with certain improvements
at Sparks Stadium as described below.

Description of proposal: Expand the onsite parking lot at Sparks Stadium from 158 to 209
parking stalls. The project will include landscaping, stormwater management and additional
parking lot lighting. There is no expansion of the stadium proposed.
