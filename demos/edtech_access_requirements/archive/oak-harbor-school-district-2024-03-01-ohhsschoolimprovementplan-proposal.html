Section 1: Annual Goals

Goal #1 SEL Goal: Improve our Panorama Survey growth area: Supportive
Relationships overall score from 78% to 82% by the Spring of 2024.

Specifically, Improve students’ positive responses to the Panorama question:
Do you have a teacher or other adult from school who you can count on to
help you, no matter what? from 72% to 77% by spring of 2024.

Supportive Relationships:

e Impacts all of the other five areas surveyed in Panorama
e The area OHHS staff can influence the most on a daily basis

overall score from 78% to 82% by the Spring of 2023.
Strategies to leverage district-support initiative(s) to support your school’ SEL goal:
e Early warning system
Advisory (with Character Strong)
Implementation of MTSS-determined core practices
Incentives for desired activities
Exploring AVID strategies
Admin and counselors (10-12) loop with students
Freshman orientation;
o LINK Crew connections and support
o_ Parent Informational Meeting/Night
Monitoring tools and targets:
e Panorama data
e Healthy Youth data
e Early Warning System Data

Goal #2 ELA Goal: 95% of the 9th grade cohort will pass their ELA classes.

Strategies to leverage district-support initiative(s) to support your school’s ELA goal:
e Common curriculum and lessons/assessments
Increase reading and writing activities in other content areas (EX: quick-writes)
Exploring AVID strategies
Co-taught classes
Freshman transition supports
Emphasize passing grades in other related courses
MLL support for ELA-specific needs

Monitoring tools and targets:
e English Course Data by Grade Level

identify how to grade more equitably

e Early Warning System Data
