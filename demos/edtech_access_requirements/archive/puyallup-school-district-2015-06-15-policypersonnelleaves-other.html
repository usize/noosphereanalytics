Upon the recommendation of the Superintendent and in accordance with the law and District policy, employees may be granted
leaves pursuant to the following conditions, unless the applicable collective bargaining agreement provides otherwise:

1. For non-supervisory certificated employees, leaves may be limited to units of half or full days only.

5. Leaves Prorated for Part-Time Staff. Part-time employees will shat be entitled to leave benefits, unless otherwise

be prorated according to the ratio of days and/or hours worked to the number of days and/or hours worked by a full-
time employee in the same or a similar position.

Leaves shall be non-cumulative from year to year unless otherwise stated.
