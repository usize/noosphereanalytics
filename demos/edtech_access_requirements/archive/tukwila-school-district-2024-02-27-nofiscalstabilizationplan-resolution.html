TUKWILA SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 406
KING COUNTY, WASHINGTON

RESOLUTION NO. 945

A RESOLUTION of the Board of Directors of the Tukwila School District No. 406,
King County, Washington, to direct the Superintendent to develop a Fiscal
Stabilization Plan

WHEREAS, even with a small enrollment increase in the 2023-24 school year the
District's student enrollment has been declining since 2014-15, and is projected to
continue to decline in future years;

WHEREAS, the District receives funding from the State and from local levies as
authorized by the State according to a per-pupil allocation formula;

WHEREAS, the State’s funding system is regressive and provides less per-student
funding to districts serving more students from low-income families;

WHEREAS, the funds received by the District through the federal Elementary and
Secondary School Emergency Relief Fund have been allocated and are ending;

WHEREAS, the Board, at a Special Board Meeting on February 13, 2024 approved
Resolution No. 944 - Accepting binding conditions as set forth by OSPI;

WHEREAS, the District does not have the assured financial resources to maintain its
program and services at the present levels for the 2024-25 fiscal year, it is necessary
to take certain actions, including reductions in the District's operations and
educational program;

WHEREAS, it is necessary at this time for the Board of Directors to direct the
Superintendent to present proposed reductions for the 2024-25 school year so that
certificated and classified employees who would be affected by said reductions may
be provided timely notification in accordance with applicable law and contract
provisions;

WHEREAS, planned actions and reductions set parameters for the program changes
necessary for the development of the 2024-25 district operating budget, to be brought
to the Board for a public hearing and adoption at a subsequent meeting;

WHEREAS, the Board has adopted a strategic plan that prioritizes the critical
functions of the District in pursuit of and accomplishment of outcomes for students,
and that must guide what reductions in the District's operations and educational
programs are made; and

WHEREAS, some expenditures are mandated by the legally protected rights of
students and costs associated with providing access to basic education must be
prioritized and protected in budgeting;


NOW, THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED, that the Board of Directors of Tukwila School
District #406 that:

1. The Superintendent is directed to present a plan for balancing the budget for
2024-25, with the goal of aligning general fund expenditures with reasonably
anticipated revenues;

2. The Superintendent is directed to present the Fiscal Stabilization Plan for review
by the Board at its February 13, 2024 Budget Work Session, and for Introduction
and Action by the Board at its February 27, 2024 Regular Board Meeting;

3. The Superintendent will take all such action as may be required by law, collective
bargaining agreements, and Board policy to implement the plan, including
notifying employees who will be affected by the implementation of the 2024-25
Fiscal Stabilization Plan;

4. The Superintendent will, prior to the introduction of the 2024-25 District operating
budget in a Regular Meeting of the Board, update the Board on any significant
variations in necessary program reductions if the final Washington State 2024-25
biennial operating budget results in a significantly different level of revenue for
the District than anticipated; and

5. The Superintendent is further directed to refine District planning and budgeting

processes to support the development of a sustainable multi-year plan to align
expenditures and revenues beyond the 2024-25 fiscal year.

ADOPTED this 27 day of February 2024 in Tukwila, Washington.
