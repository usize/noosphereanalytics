H.E.R.E Learning Happens

High Expectations & Excellence for All

Students and staff have high expectations, aiming to graduate responsible,
civically engaged students by using resources responsibly to achieve successful
outcomes.

Engaged Learners

Students engage with meaningful content and high-quality instruction, through
engaging learning experiences and service-oriented leadership to achieve their
highest potential.

Relationships

We prioritize positive relationships between students, staff, families, and
community organizations by partnering in education and holding ourselves
accountable for treating each other with civility.

Equity & Empathy

We value the diverse cultures, languages, and experiences of the Tukwila

community by nurturing cultural connectedness and racial equity and ensuring
schools are safe for everyone physically, socially, and emotionally.

Strategic Plan Link: https://www.tukwilaschools.org/about-our-district/district-overview/strategic-plan



Anti-Racist Goals

Name and Disrupt Racist Systems

Identify and dismantle policies and
practices that perpetuate racial bias and
inequalities within the Tukwila School
District.

Enforce Anti-Biased Discipline

Ensure disciplinary practices are fair and
free from bias, promoting restorative
justice.

BIPOC Represented Curriculum

Integrate a curriculum that reflects the
diverse histories and contributions of
BIPOC communities.

Anti-Racist Leadership Development

Provide training for TSD leaders to
cultivate anti-racist practices and
mindsets.

Student Voice and Engagement

Empower students to participate in
decision-making and share their
experiences to foster a welcoming and
inclusive TSD community.

Anti-Racist Policy Review and Advocacy

Regularly review and revise district
policies to align with anti-racist principles
and advocate for systemic change.

Weather Damage Mitigation

Winterization

Work scheduled for November 11, 2024 to winterize Dry System: preventative maintenance does
not guarantee freeze breaks cannot occur.

After the Confidence Test, the fire service company will make sure to blow all low points so as
much moisture as possible can be removed from the systems.

Administration Building/Cascade View Elementary

Layout a new direct access point (Admin. Bldg.). Heat trace protection will be applied to
domestic water pipes. Install wire sensors and modules. Reinsulate pipes with thicker
insulation wrap.

The cost for this project is $40,000.

FEMA - Application Ongoing

FEMA declared the weather incidents from January 2024 disastrous, and the Governor declared a
State of Emergency. FEMA has offered assistance with mitigation and reimbursement for costs not
covered by insurance. This is typically a very long process and does not guarantee funding.
