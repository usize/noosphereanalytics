EDMONDS SCHOOL DISTRICT NO 15
SNOHOMISH COUNTY, WASHINGTON
RESOLUTION NO. 25-02

ACCEPTANCE OF COMPLETED PUBLIC WORKS CONTRACT
FOR MEADOWDALE MIDDLE SCHOOL TRACK RESURFACING PROJECT

WHEREAS, the public works contract was awarded to Coast to Coast Turf
Inc. for the Meadowdale Middle School Track Resurfacing
Project.

WHEREAS, Coast to Coast Turf, Inc. was given Notice to Proceed on June,
17, 2024 to begin work, and

WHEREAS, Substantial Completion of the project was attained on August
28, 2024 and

WHEREAS, Final Completion was issued to Edmonds School District on
October 25, 2024 now therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED THAT; the Board of Directors does hereby accept the public works
contract for the Meadowdale Middle School Track Resurfacing Project.

Adopted this 14th day of January, 2025

EDMONDS SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 15
BOARD OF DIRECTORS

Thom Garrard, Board Member
ATTEST:

Rebecca Miner
Superintendent/Secretary to the Board
