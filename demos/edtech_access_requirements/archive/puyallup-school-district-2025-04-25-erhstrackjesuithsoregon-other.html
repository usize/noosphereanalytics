Hello,

We are excited to take our student-athletes to a fun, exciting, and competitive meet in
Oregon. In years’ past, we have traveled to the Oregon Relays but based on the schedule,
cost, and number of athletes likely to qualify, we made the decision to try a different meet.
This trip is a down-and-back trip, but it is an extremely competitive meet for our teams. Our
boys’ and girls’ programs have won the last three division championships and the women
are the reigning two-time state champions, so we are always looking to find quality
competitions and a fun environment to honor their hard-work. Our student-athletes have
worked hard fundraising by hosting two invitationals and the cost of the trip will be paid for
by their efforts. Thank you for your considering this great opportunity for our student-
athletes.

Best,
Bob Frey
