EDMONDS SCHOOL DISTRICT NO 15
SNOHOMISH COUNTY, WASHINGTON
RESOLUTION NO. 25-05

ACCEPTANCE OF COMPLETED PUBLIC WORKS CONTRACT FOR
MOUNTLAKE TERRACE HIGH SCHOOL FIELD IMPROVEMENTS PROJECT

WHEREAS, the public works contract was awarded to Coast to Coast Turf Inc. for
the Mountlake Terrace High School Field Improvements Project.

WHEREAS, Coast to Coast Turf, Inc. was given Notice to Proceed on July 10, 2023
to begin work, and

WHEREAS, Substantial Completion of the project was attained on February 16, 2024
and

WHEREAS, Final Completion was issued to Edmonds School District on November
14, 2024, now therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED THAT; the Board of Directors does hereby accept the public works contract
for the Mountlake Terrace High School Field Improvements Project.

Adopted this 14th day of January, 2025

EDMONDS SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 15
BOARD OF DIRECTORS

Thom Garrard, Board Member

Rebecca Miner
Superintendent/Secretary to the Board
