AGENDA ITEM: Foster FCCLA Members attending 2024 State Leadership Conference and Star
Events Competition

Foster High School Family, Career and Community Leader of America (FCCLA) members are
seeking permission to attend State Leadership Conference and Star Events Competition in March
2024. State Leadership Conference and Star Events Competition will be held at Spokane Convention
Center in Spokane, Washington from March 5-8, 2024. The purpose of the trip is to provide students
with leadership opportunities to promote the opportunity to advance to the National Leadership
Conference in Seattle, WA, earn recognition and attend stimulating leadership sessions, meet and
network with fellow members, advisers, and industry and community leaders. Participate in inspiring
general sessions and earn honors and possible scholarships as Career and Technical Student
Organizations. Students will have an opportunity to Foster positive school community and build
comradery among the Washington State FCCLA Community. The cost of the trip is $650 for each
student, which covers registration, meals and lodging. The trip will be supervised by the FCCLA
Advisor of (Tracy Robnett) and Foster CTE Teacher (Tim Renz).
