Regular Board Meeting
October 22, 2024

Responses to the Board’s questions/comments on Agenda Item 9.F — School Safety Plans for the October 22, 2024 Regular
Board Meeting:

1. Agenda Item 9.F — School Safety Plans

1. Should a definition and associated procedures of the lockdowns and modified lockdowns be included in
these documents?
Scott Whitbeck: Yes, this can be done.

2. Could a parent guide to emergencies be produced and given to parents?
Scott Whitbeck: Yes, this can be done.

3. The documents say that cell phone use should be discouraged. Is that realistic?
Scott Whitbeck: Schools define parameters for appropriate cell phone use. An example is for phones to be put
away during class time.

4. What are the specific mechanisms that will be used to communicate with parents?
Scott Whitbeck: Schools share about their safety plans with families via newsletters and parent meetings.
Schools also have safety teams that meet multiple times per year to address safety issues that arise, to
create resolutions, and to communicate what those resolutions are.

5. How is the performance of the district’s network infrastructure being monitored?
Scott Whitbeck: Please schedule a time with tech. staff to learn more about this.
