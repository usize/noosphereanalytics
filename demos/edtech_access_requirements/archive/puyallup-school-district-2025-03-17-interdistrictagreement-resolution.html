RESOLUTION #102 2024-25

WHEREAS, each school district of the State of Washington is authorized by
RCW.13.030 to participate in the interdistrict agreement, and secure appropriate
educational opportunities for its’ students with disabilities.

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Superintendent of Puyallup
School District No. 003 be authorized to enter into an interdistrict agreement with the
Steilacoom Hist. School District No. 001 to provide or receive educational services for
students with disabilities ages 3 through 21.

ADOPTED by majority of the Board of Directors at the meeting held on Monday,
March 17, 2025.

BOARD OF DIRECTORS
PUYALLUP SCHOOL DISTRICT

ATTEST:

Secretary of the Board
