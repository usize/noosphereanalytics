THIS SERVICES AGREEMENT (The“Agreement’) is entered into this 6th day of March,
2024 by and between Universal Speech Therapy (UST) having its principal place of business at
53 Cleveland Street, Greenfield, Massachusetts, and Oak Harbor School District having its
principal place business at 350 S Oak Harbor St, Oak Harbor, WA 98277.

WHEREAS, UST will use Google Meet as it preferred platform from time to time may use
other in it sole discretion for teletherapy sessions allowing the therapist and the child to
join a computer-based session at a designated time through Google Meet, to provide
speech language therapy using the same type of materials used in astandard therapy
room at school hereafter “teletherapy”.

WHEREAS, UST desires to perform certain services for Oak Harbor School District as
an independent contractor basis as set forth in this Agreement. WHEREAS, the
American Speech and Hearing Association (ASHA) defines tele-practice (the act of
providing telehealth services) as the application of telecommunications technology to
delivery of professional services at a distance by linking clinician visits to client, or
Clinician to clinician, for assessment, intervention, and/or consultation.

NOW THEREFORE, in consideration of the above Recitals, which are hereby incorporated into
the below Agreement, and in consideration of the mutual promises made herein, the receipt
and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the UST and Oak Harbor School District
further agree as follows:

Mutual Understanding

Oak Harbor School District hereby consents to engage in teletherapy using the
Google Meets platform with the speech language pathologist assigned by
Universal Speech Therapy, who has an active state license in speech language
pathology in the state they reside in, as well as, in the state of Washington.

A. Oak Harbor School District understands that teletherapy
includes treatment using interactive audio, video, or data
communications. Oak Harbor Public Schools understands that
teletherapy also involves the communication of a child’s medical
information, both verbally and visually.

B. Oak Harbor School District understands the following with
respect to teletherapy: We have the right to withhold or withdraw
consent at any time without affecting our right to further care or
treatment. The laws that protect my confidentiality of my medical
information also apply to teletherapy. As such, we understand that
the information disclosed by us during the course of a child’s
therapy or consultation is confidential.

C. Oak Harbor School District understands that there are risks
and consequences from teletherapy, including but not limited to, the
possibility, despite reasonable effort on the part of the speech
language pathologist assigned by Universal Speech Therapy that:
the transmission of information could be disrupted or distorted by
technical failures; the transmission of information could be
interrupted by unauthorized persons; and/or the electronic storage
of my medical information could be accessed by unauthorized
persons.

D. Oak Harbor School District understands that we are
responsible for (1) providing the necessary computer,
telecommunications equipment and internet access for teletherapy
sessions, (2) the information security on computers, and (3)
arranging a location with sufficient lighting and privacy that is free
from distractions or intrusions for teletherapy session.

E. Teletherapy has been determined as an appropriate service
delivery model for children. Teletherapy will only be used if it is
determined that it is equally effective as in-person treatment.

F. If Teletherapy is not deemed as effective, you will be notified
and referred back to in- person treatment.

Additional Terms

Term-Duration-Termination. The term of this Agreement shall be from March 6,
2024 to June 2024, with a possibility for Extended School Year (ESY) where said
agreement shall, unless notice of termination is served no less than 90 days for
the expiration date above renewing for the following school year.

Termination. The Agreement may be terminated earlier by final completion of
the Services by UST or termination by either party to this agreement at any time.
Any services previously rendered will be payable immediately. All provisions of
the Agreement which by their nature should survive termination, including,
without limitation, ownership provisions, warranty disclaimers, indemnity, and
limitations of liability.

Relationship. The parties understand and agree that UST is an independent
service provider and is not an employee, agent or servant of the Oak Harbor
School District

Direct Service. This includes conducting speech language evaluations and
speech language therapy to students on 504 plans, Individualized Education
Plans and Response to Intervention programs, and conducting screenings.

Indirect Service. This includes all communications with teachers, staff, parents
and administrators including electronic and telephonic communication, lesson
planning, material gathering and preparation, reviewing records, completing
evaluation reports, analyzing test results, progress report writing, IEP writing and
attending IEP meetings and/or staff meetings if required, review of records,
including IEPs/goals and objectives/ reports, participation in student advisory
teams.

Student Absences/No Shows/Unavailability of Students: Oak Harbor School
District understands that the assigned speech language pathologist will bill for set
hours per week as long as school is in session. Oak Harbor School District will
be billed for student absences, student unavailability and/or lack of participation
on the part of the student.
