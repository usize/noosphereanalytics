The following data is included in the 2024/2025 report of the current
building condition of schools enrolled in the Asset Preservation

Program:

e Summary sheet condition evaluation scoring.
e Individual component scoring breakdown by school.

e Aylen Junior High: The standard building condition assessment score for a 12-year-old building is 86%.
Aylen Junior High will receive an updated HVAC control system and a new HVAC chiller. Once completed,
this will raise the score at or above the standard 86%.

e Sunrise and Northwood elementary schools indicate the next building condition assessment certified
scoring is due in 2025 & 2026 respectively. OSPI has confirmed to wait on both schools until the next BCA
certified scoring is due in 2027 to align all schools listed with the same BCA certified scoring date moving
forward. This will allow BCA scoring to take place once every six years. Both schools will continue to
indicate current dates until they are updated in 2027.


Footnote: The Asset Preservation Program evolved from a State Board of Education policy developed in 1992. The
intent of the program is to ensure that taxpayer investment in state assisted construction of school facilities is
protected through a system of building maintenance.

The Asset Preservation Program applies to school districts that received state assistance for new and school
buildings accepted by local school board directors after December 31, 1993. Participation in the APP is one
requirement of eligibility for future state assistance.
