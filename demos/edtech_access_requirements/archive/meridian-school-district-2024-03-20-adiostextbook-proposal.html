Meridian School District
Core Curriculum Proposal
March 20, 2024

The Meridian School District Instructional Materials Committee recommends the following core instructional materials for adoption
by the Meridian School Board.

Adios Textbook Copyright: 2023
Publisher: Creative Language Class
Subject/Course: Exploratory Spanish, Spanish L,II, Ill

Grade Level: 6-12
https://www.adiostextbook.com/

Identification Committee members: Heather Knox, Keifa Canup

Publisher statement:

We believe that learning a language should be about communication. Lessons should be inspired by authentic resources and target
cultures. Instruction should be 90% in the target language.Learning should connect with real experiences and future careers. Student
performance should be measured by proficiency and improved by individualized feedback. Languages are for everyone.

Research based unit structure has students learning language through
immersion in the target language. Students practice speaking, writing and
listening in authentic contexts.

The materials demonstrate the following research based practices for
world language instruction:
e use target language for learning; students and teachers speak,

listen, read, write, view, and create in the target language

use authentic cultural resources

emphasize interpersonal communication tasks

teach grammar as concept in context

provide appropriate feedback

The material’s use of proficiency rubrics is aligned with growth practice.
During a unit-long pilot of the materials, teachers found a positive impact
in students’ learning. Unit assessment activities show high levels of

growth.

Each unit is aligned with the 5 C’s in the World Language Standards:
Communication, Cultures, Connections, Comparisons, Communities.

The materials provide teachers with standards alignment information for
each unit. Student activities are connected to each standard.

The units are structured around a language proficiency rubric that allows
a student to improve regardless of their proficiency level entering the
class.
