Board Meeting
February 27, 2024

Responses to the Board’s questions/comments on Agenda Item 9.A — Race and Equity Lead Committee for the February
27, 2024 Board Meeting:

1. Agenda Item 9.A — Race and Equity Lead Committee Presentation

1. What predictive measures other than discipline data should the district use as Indicators of Success?
Scott Whitbeck: Comparing discipline data with data we already collect: attendance; enrollment in advanced
classes, Running Start, and College Bound programs; grades; and pre-school enrollment (same data as
referenced in the following question; using Skyward to access these data points). We also need to collect
“Street Data” including empathy interviews with students, families, and staff. Center for Educational
Effectiveness (CEE) will facilitate climate surveys, including interpreting the results and implementing
recommendations.

2. What skyward Data should be used?
Scott Whitbeck: Attendance data, enrollment in advanced classes, grades, Running Start enrollment, Pre-
school enrollment, college bound data.

3. What should an Equity Audit consist of?
Dr. Pedroza/Aaron Draganov: Equity audit requires an examination of our policies, practices, bargaining
agreements, procedures, and outcomes. This audit should be used to determine what is needed to fully
implement goals and procedures for the coming years. It should also include recommendations from our
facilitators in providing next steps which include the Equity Leads, extended cabinet and the board. All of the
work for this year should provide a two-year plan. Dr. Rogers-Ard and Dr. Knaus will be providing a report to
launch for the next year at the end of year. There are budget implications if we continue with outside
facilitators or additional consultants.

4. How should race and equity measures be incorporated into TPEP?
Dr. Pedroza / Aaron Draganov: The current evaluation criteria used in TPEP should be reviewed to identify any biases or
gaps related to race and equity. This would involve analyzing growth data used in the process such as student learning
outcomes and disciplinary actions. (TPEP group which included UW CEL, Danielson and Marzano received professional
development on racial equity prior to revising their frameworks, these are updated and we will be using the updated
Danielson framework in 24-25).
