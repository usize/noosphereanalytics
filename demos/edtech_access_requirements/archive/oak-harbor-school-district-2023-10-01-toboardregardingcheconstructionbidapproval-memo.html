The Board approves award of the construction contract for Crescent Harbor Elementary Replacement
Project to the lowest qualified bidder, Tiger Construction Ltd., and grants permission for the
Superintendent to sign the contract, contingent upon finalization of Department of Defense grant.

The contract will be executed upon final award of the federal grant (Public Schools on Military
Installations), which is to include appropriate site control as provided by Naval Air Station Whidbey
Island.

The construction contract shall be for a total cost of $53,647,403 which is below the estimate of
$55,379,614.
