ESTIMATE ASSUMPTIONS & EXCLUSIONS

ASSUMPTIONS

- Target Value Pricing is based on the 2-story linear building option shown in the 11-23-24 site options document from
TCF

- Target Value pricing is based on the Bremerton School District Standards provided as part of the RFP

- Pricing assumes ground improvements (aggregate piers or similar) to support foundations only

- Pricing assumes conventional concrete foundations

- Pricing assumes a wood framed 2-story structure. Pricing assumes 3,250 SF of wood framed mechanical mezzanine

- Pricing for exterior finishes assumes fiber cements siding with targeted areas of accent siding / masonry, asphalt
shingle roofing, fiberglass windows with targeted areas of aluminum storefront

- Pricing for interior doors & glazing assumes wood doors with hollow metal frames with targeted areas of interior
aluminum storefront / hollow metal relites

- Pricing for interior wall finishes assumes painted GWB, targeted areas of VWC/TWS, tile wainscot at large restrooms,
plastic paneling wainscot at small restrooms, FRP at Kitchen, MDF wainscot at corridors

- Pricing for floor finishes assumes resilient flooring at corridors, carpet at classrooms, resilient athletic flooring at
gym, resilient flooring at kitchen, tile at large restrooms, resilient flooring at small restrooms, and rubber base

- Pricing for floor finishes assumes ACT ceilings, GWB ceilings at restrooms, and targeted areas of accent ceilings

- Pricing for floor stairs assumes 1 main feature stair with upgraded finishes + 2 wood framed egress stairs with
basic finishes + 1 wood framed back-of-house stairs to the mechanical mezzanine

- Pricing assumes a 2-stop holeless hydraulic elevator with basic finishes

- Pricing assumes a wet fire sprinkler system. Pricing assumes adequate water flow + water pressure is available

- Pricing assumes the existing View Ridge Arts Academy will remain as-is. Demolition is NOT included

- Pricing assumes a 3,200 SF covered play structure

EXCLUSIONS
- Abatement + Demolition - Existing building + portables assumed to remain
- Offsite ROW Work - Assumes separate funding source
- Payment for independent testing & inspection services or fees
- Power, water, sewer, storm, or other utility purveyor design, connection, construction, fees
- Removal of unidentified underground storage tanks (including contaminated soils)
- Cost for remediation of contaminated soils
- Relocation or salvage of existing portables
- Salvage of items from existing school
- Any & all work to the existing school, gym & B&G Club
- 3rd party commissioning fees & services
- Furnishings, fixtures, equipment other than specifically included
- Building & utility permits & permit fees
- Provisions for restrictive specifications & non-competitive bid conditions (i.e. sole source items, etc.)
- Compression of schedule, premium work shift, restriction on contractor work hours unless explicitly
- Cost Escalation beyond a construction start of September 2025
- Washington State Sales Tax
