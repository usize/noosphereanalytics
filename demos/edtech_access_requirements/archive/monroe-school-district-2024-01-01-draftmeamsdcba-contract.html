Preparation and Delivery of Professional Development

If agreed upon by staff, the principal may request that staff prepare for professional development sessions by

completing aligned tasks or readings not to exceed 30 minutes of preparation time. Any employee(s) that are
equired to prepare for a session shall be compensated at the curriculum rate of pay for the necessary time to

prepare, not to exceed the 30 minutes.



Staff who have applicable experience and/or qualifications may volunteer to lead no more than two times per

year, a Professional Development session as individuals or as a team, to be coordinated in collaboration with
