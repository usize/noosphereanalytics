H.E.R.E. We Learn

The Tukwila School District’s Strategic Plan serves as a roadmap to guide the
district in achieving its mission of fostering academic excellence, equity, and
community engagement. Centered around key areas such as high
expectations, engaged learners, strong relationships, and equity, the plan
outlines clear goals and strategies to ensure that all students are supported,
challenged, and prepared for future success. By aligning resources, policies,
and practices with these priorities, the plan provides a cohesive framework for
improving student outcomes, promoting racial equity, and strengthening
partnerships with families and the community.
