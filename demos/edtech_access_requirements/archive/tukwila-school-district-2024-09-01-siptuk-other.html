A INSTRUCTION



At Tukwila Elementary, our mission is to intentionally cultivate a positive, safe, and culturally responsive environment
Where inclusivity thrives. We are dedicated to empowering our students, staff, and community to learn, grow, and
succeed together in a setting that fosters high academic achievement, self-esteem, and the development of

responsible, caring citizens.

ision Statement
Tiger scholars will achieve academic and social-emotional success through the support of equity-driven adults who

foster high expectations and a culture of excellence.
