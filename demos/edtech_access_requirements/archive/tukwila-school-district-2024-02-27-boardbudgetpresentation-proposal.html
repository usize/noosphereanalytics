Review and Analysis Continues

¢ Meeting with staff to review current budgets

¢ Analyzing all grants

¢ Looking at current general fund costs that may be eligible to
charge to the tech levy

¢ Instituted internal controls. Next steps might include hiring
freeze through the end of the year, analyzing and potentially
ending some vendor contracts before the end of this fiscal
year


Next Steps

¢ Re-do the 2023-24 budget with OSPI and ESD
¢ Bring forth budget for re-approval with the board
¢ Planned completion March 2024


2024-25 Budget Development

¢ Provide a balanced budget that brings our fund balance to
SO with further work to begin building fund balance reserves

¢ ESSER funding ends August 31, 2024 totaling $5.9 million

¢ Estimated 23-24 Fund balance negative $3.5 to negative $4.3
million

2024-25 Budget Development

¢ Staff Attrition will not get us to SO
¢ This means there will be cuts:

¢ Certificated staff

¢ Classified staff

¢ Central office staff

¢ Contracts

¢ It is the only way we can get to a SO fund balance and begin
rebuilding reserves

¢ Detailed recommendations are being developed
¢ Considering board priorities and philosophies
¢ Reviewing union priority input

¢ Abiding by the collective bargaining agreement
timeline
