This Field Trip Request form is for all Federal Way Public Schools field trip requests, including: 1) One Day In-State F igld Trips, 2)
Overnight or Out-of-State Field Trips and 3) International Field Trips and Travel. This form should be completed by the Field Trip
Organizer and/or Chaperone Lead, and preliminary approval from the Principal is veg uived prior to all field trips.

Field Trip Request Submission Timelines by Type:
1) One Day In-State Field Trips require preliminary approval and requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks prior to the field
trip.
2) Overnight or Out-of-State Field Trips require preliminary approval and requests must be submitted at least 1 month prior to
the field trip.
3) International Field Trips and Travel require preliminary approval and requests must be submitted at least 6 months prior to the
trip.

Field Trip Permission Form Requirements:

1) One Day In-State Field Trips: During the online enrollment/verification process, parents/guardians annually opt-in or opt-out
their student(s) to attend or not attend all One Day In-State Field Trips throughout the year. Field trip permission forms are not
required for students who have been opted-in to attend One Day In-State Field trips. Field Trip Permission Forms are required
for students who opt-out of One Day In-State Field Trips.

2) Overnight or Out-of-State Field Trips: Field Trip Permission Forms are required for all Overnight or Out-of-State Field Trips.

3) International Field Trips: Field Trip Permission Forms are required for all International Field Trips.

Field Trip Notification Requirements:

All field trips require notification to parent/guardians at least two weeks prior the trip. Notice must include the trip date/time, location,
transportation, any costs or other requirements (such as bringing a lunch), and the chaperone lead’s contact information. The template
below should be completed and used for the notification to parents/guardians. School Nurses must be notified at least two weeks
prior to the trip to review the student list and field trip request to ensure any medical needs are covered. If applicable, Nutrition Services
should be notified to assess whether sack lunches are needed.
