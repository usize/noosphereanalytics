EDMONDS SCHOOL DISTRICT NO 15
SNOHOMISH COUNTY, WASHINGTON
RESOLUTION NO. 25-03

ACCEPTANCE OF COMPLETED PUBLIC WORKS CONTRACT
FOR FORMER ALDERWOOD MIDDLE SCHOOL SEISMIC UPGRADES PROJECT

WHEREAS, the public works contract was awarded to Moon Construction Company
for the Former Alderwood Middle School Seismic Upgrades Project.

WHEREAS, Moon Construction Company was given Notice to Proceed on July, 5,
2023 to begin work, and

WHEREAS, Substantial Completion of the project was attained on March 4, 2024
and

WHEREAS, Final Completion was attained by Edmonds School District on November
25, 2024, now therefore,

BE IT RESOLVED THAT; the Board of Directors does hereby accept the public works contract
for the Former Alderwood Middle School Seismic Upgrades Project.

Adopted this 14th day of January, 2025

EDMONDS SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 15
BOARD OF DIRECTORS

Thom Garrard, Board Member

Rebecca Miner
Superintendent/Secretary to the Board
