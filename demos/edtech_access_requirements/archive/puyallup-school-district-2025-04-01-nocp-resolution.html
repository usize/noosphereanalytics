PUYALLUP SCHOOL DISTRICT #3
RESOLUTION #109 2024-25

A RESOLUTION TO ACCEPT COMPLETION OF A PUBLIC WORKS CONTRACT:
Storm water extension at South Hill Transportation, Project #87-01-22.

Whereas RCW 60.28.011 states that Public Improvement Contracts must provide, and public
bodies must reserve retainage not to exceed 5% (or 10% in lieu of Performance Bond
per RCW 39.08.010) in escrow until the School Board has accepted as complete said
project; and,

Whereas RCW 60.28.011 states that retainage shall be kept for 45 days following the Board's
acceptance or until all releases have been received; and,

Whereas The following project has been approved and recommended as being complete by Dr.
Vincent Pecchia, Assistant Superintendent of Operations and School Support:

Purchase Order #CP4190-Capital Projects Fund
Rivers Edge Environmental Services Inc.
17115 SE 270° Place, Suite 106
Covington, WA 98042

Contract Amount Excluding Taxes and Fees: $ 247,246.10

Total Sales Tax and Fees: $ 24,971.86
Total Amount Paid to Contractor: $ 259,855.66
Total Amount of Retainage Held (5%): $ 12,362.30

Therefore, be it resolved that the above referenced project be accepted as complete.

Adopted this 1° day of April 2025.
