Board Meeting
February 27, 2024

Responses to the Board’s questions/comments on Agenda Item 8.D — Monthly Budget Report — January 2024 for the
February 27, 2024 Board Meeting:

1. Agenda Item 8.D — Monthly Budget Status Report — January 2024

1. Is the district getting any transportation safety net dollars?
Amy Fleming: The district applied for transportation safety net dollars but was not awarded funding. The
majority of the costs are with external contractors and we did not meet the threshold.

2. On Page 12, the district’s expenditures exceeded the revenue for January. When do you expect this
situation to reverse?
Amy Fleming: We may see a brief reversal when property tax revenue is received in April/May. However, we
anticipate expenditure to continue exceeding revenues through the end of the fiscal year.

3. On Page 26 and 27, how much of the unspent ESSER III dollars have been allocated?
Amy Fleming: All ESSER Ill dollars have either been or will be allocated and spent by the end of the fiscal
year.
