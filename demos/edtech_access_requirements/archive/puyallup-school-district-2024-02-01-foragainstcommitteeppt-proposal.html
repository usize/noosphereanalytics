“For” and “Against”
Committee Appointments

¢ For each measure submitted for inclusion in the local voters’ pamphlet, RCW 29A.32.280 requires
local governmental units to appoint a committee to prepare arguments advocating voters’
approval, and arguments advocating voters’ rejection of the measutes.

¢ Interested parties were invited to respond to a survey located on the district’s website November
19-26, 2024. Priority for committee appointment was given to the first survey responders and
those residing within district boundaries. The district is recommending the same appointments
for the April election date as were made for the February election without additional process. The

committee members’ information has been entered on a Committee Member Appointment Form
and will be submitted to Pierce County Elections.
