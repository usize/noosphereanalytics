SATUS’ GUIDING PRINCIPLE STATEMENT.

We believe that all staff should feel safe and respected, Therefore
we must commit to clear communication, effective problem
solving, and respectful conflict resolution. We engage in non-
judgemental analysis, demonstrating honesty and integrity in all
our interactions.

We believe that all staff should feel valued and supported.
Therefore, we hold each other accountable to our commitments,
provide space for reflection when tensions arise, and remain open
to new perspectives. We are dedicated to making compromise™
for the well-being of our school community.
