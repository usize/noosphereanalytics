Jazz Club

Jazz Club is a gathering place for students interested in performing, soloing, and
improvising. Our club performs a variety of musical styles which allow for improvisation,
exploration of different musical cultures, and the opportunity to play a variety of
instruments. The Jazz band will travel to the Commencement Bay Jazz Festival and, in

the future, the Reno Jazz Fest.
Fundraising will be through bake sales and concerts.

The Club has a $50 annual member fee assessed to pay for the advisor stipend.

The advisor/coach is Todd Zimberg

Jazz Club meets weekly in the band room on PDD Fridays at 8:30am.
