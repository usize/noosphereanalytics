Recommendation to Approve Schedule A between Washougal School District 112-6 and
Public School Employees of Washington, Washougal Chapter/SEIU Local 1948 (PSE)

Rationale:

The Collective Bargaining Agreement between the Washougal School District and Public
School Employees of Washougal (PSE) does not expire until August 31,2025. However, per
the Collective Bargaining Agreement, the parties met to negotiate wage increases for the
2024-25 school year. Representative teams of the parties met in 5 bargaining sessions
during the period April 2024 through May 2024 to bargain per the Washington Collective
Bargaining laws. The negotiated agreement includes several modifications that have a direct
financial component. A tentative agreement as to the wage increase was reached between
the parties on May 23, 2024. Members of PSE ratified the tentative agreement at a meeting
on June 10, 2024. Washougal School District Board of Directors approval is required for the
contract to become official.

Additional Information:

Aaron Hansen, Assistant Superintendent, on behalf of the District and Justine Shatney on
behalf of PSE chaired the bargaining sessions. The parties engaged in a collaborative and
inclusive style of bargaining.

Fund Source:

General Fund

Recommendation:

The Board of Directors approves the Collective Bargaining Agreement Schedule A between
the Washougal School District 112-6 and Public School Employees of Washington

Washougal Chapter, September 1, 2022 through August 31, 2025.

Board Meeting: August 27,2024


Washougal School District and Public School Employees of Washington
Collective Bargaining - June - August 2025 Summary of Changes to Schedule A

The current agreement is a 3-year agreement for the 2022-23, 2023-24 and 2024-25 school
years. The negotiated Schedule A was ratified by the PSE members on June 10, 2024.

Included below are some of the modifications to the terms and conditions of the contract:
e PSE staff will receive the following compensation increases:

o 3.6% wage increase during the 2024-25 school year.

o Adjusted the salary schedule from a 6-step schedule to 5-steps for
2024-25. This represents a .4% increase for 2024-25.

o Night custodians who previously had a + $.50 pay differential per hour
increase for employees who work after 6:00 PM had their pay
increased to that additional $.50 for their whole shift regardless of the
start times.

o The Courier position, which was previously a non-union position, was
added to the PSE group.

A Day Custodian Lead position was added to the Schedule A.
A Night Custodian Lead position was added to the Schedule A.
