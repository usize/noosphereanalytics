BREMERTON SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 100-C
BOARD OF DIRECTORS

RESOLUTION 2023/2024-26

Resolution Establishing Process for Creation of
Small Works Roster and Consultant Services Roster

WHEREAS, Chapter 39.04 RCW and District Policies and Procedures regarding
contracting for public works, allow certain contracts to be awarded by a small works roster process;
and

WHEREAS, Chapter 39.80 RCW and District Policies and Procedures regarding
contracting for consulting services allow certain contracts to be awarded by a consultant roster
process;

Therefore, Be It Resolved by the Bremerton School District No. 100-C Board of Directors
as follows:

1. Official Rosters. The District’s official roster for small public works contracts, as
authorized under Chapter 39.04 RCW, and for consulting services contracts, as
authorized under Chapter 39.80 RCW, will be hosted in the Municipal Research and
Services Center of Washington (MRSC) online database for District use. The rosters will
be developed and maintained by MRSC.

2. Small Public Works Roster. At least once a year, MRSC will, on behalf of the District,
publish in a newspaper of general circulation and provide the office of minority and
women's business enterprises’ directory of certified firms a notice of the existence of the
roster and solicit contractors for the roster. MRSC will add responsible contractors to the
roster any time they submit a written request and the necessary records to MRSC.

3. Consulting Services Roster. Consulting services are “architectural and engineering
services” and “professional services” as defined in RCW 39.80.020. At least once a year,
MRSC will, on behalf of the District, publish in a newspaper of general circulation a
notice of the existence of the consulting services roster and solicit the names of
consultants for the consulting services roster. Such publication shall meet the
requirements of RCW 39.80.030—.040. MRSC will add responsible consultants to the
consulting services roster at any time that a consultant completes the online application
provided by MRSC, uploads a Statement of Qualifications, and meets minimum State
requirements for roster listing.
