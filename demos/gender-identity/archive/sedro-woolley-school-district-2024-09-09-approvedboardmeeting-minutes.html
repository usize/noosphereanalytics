Presentations

OSPI Recognition for Mary Purcell, Big Lake, Central and Evergreen Elementary. Dr.

Mickelson recognized these schools for their outstanding work in improving student learning the

2022-23 school year. Recognized schools in the state have made great strides to improve
outcomes for students by closing gaps and showing tremendous growth and achievement. Each
principal was presented with a certificate of achievement and will later receive a banner from

OSPI.

Board Certified Behavior Analyst Data Presentation -- Tamara provided the board with

information on the Board with the Applied Behavior Analysis and data showing the BCBA hours

throughout the district during the 2023-24 school year.

Board Comments
Director Mullen-Doyle will be attending WSSDA’s 2024 General Assembly. The board will be
compiling their votes and Lindy will present that information at the General Assembly.

Director Mullen-Doyle is requesting to add a WIAA resolution and amendment to the next board
meeting agenda. Copies of the drafts were provided to the other board members for their review.

Superintendent's Report:

Dr. Miriam Mickelson reported that the first week of school went well. Fourth day enrollment
count is 4,167 and projection as 4,097. Principals and ELT will continue to review class sizes.

Superintendent Mickelson attended the City Council meeting regarding the Goldeneye Lithium
Battery Storage project. In addition, Dr. Mickelson was happy to announce that the district
celebrated the grand opening of the new modular at Evergreen Elementary with a ribbon-cutting
event.

School Board Representatives, Adriana Rodriguez and Spencer White, provided the donation
and activity report.

Old Business:

Brent Schiefelbein moved and Lindy Mullen Doyle seconded to approve Policy #5254 Staff
Expression. This policy was presented to the board for first reading on August 26, 2024. This
policy is a new policy written by WSSDA with an interest to maintain an orderly and effective
work environment, while balancing employees’ First Amendment rights to freedom of expression
and diverse viewpoints and beliefs. After discussion, Lindy Mullen Doyle moved and Brent
Schiefelbein seconded to TABLE the policy to the next board meeting. Motion passed 3/0.

Agenda Additions:

Director Mullen Doyle requested for first reading of the WIAA resolution and amendment to the
September 23, 2024, board meeting agenda, for first reading. There is an element of time, as the
deadline for submission to WIAA is October 10th or 11th. Director Schiefelbein is also requesting
to have this added to the next board meeting agenda so the board is able to conduct formal
discussion on the matter.

Adjournment:
There being no further business, the meeting adjourned at 7:13 p.m., Brent Schiefelbein moved
and Lindy Mullen Doyle seconded to adjourn the meeting. The motion carried 3/0.
