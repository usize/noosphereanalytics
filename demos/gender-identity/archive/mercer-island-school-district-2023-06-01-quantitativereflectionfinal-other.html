SIP 2023-24

Our School Improvement Plan goal for 2023-24
was to improve the educational outcomes of
Hispanic/Latino, Black/African American
students, as well as students receiving IEPs &
MLLs and 504 accommodations at MIHS by June
2024. Our goal was to eliminate barriers for
these groups of students by building authentic
relationships.

outcomes by June 2024 for these groups of students. It starts with understanding
and supporting who our students are and who they want to become. By focusing on
these marginalized student groups, we can improve the outcome for the entire

These groups of students will have improved indicators for student/educational
outcomes (ie. grades, attendance, EES, etc) that reverse the disproportionality data
identified in our 22-23 SIP analysis and reflection.

SIP 2024-25

Our school improvement goal is to eliminate
barriers and improve educational outcomes for
our Hispanic/Latino, Black/African American
students, and students receiving IEP & MLL
services by June 2025.

Through building authentic relationships and

expressing a deep belief in their potential, we

will ensure these students receive the support
and opportunities they need to thrive.
