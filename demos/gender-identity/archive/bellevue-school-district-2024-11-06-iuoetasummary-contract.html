MEMORANDUM OF AGREEMENT
between the
International Union of Operating Engineers Local 302

and the
BELLEVUE SCHOOL DISTRICT
Regarding
Custodian Schedules for Split Assignments

The Union and District agree to the following related to custodian work schedules:

The parties agree to a review of custodian work schedules enacted during the 2023-2024 contract
year which include positions that are split between worksites. This review will include the
following:

1) Each labor management meeting will have a standing agenda for update on the process
and providing opportunity for the Union and District to jointly monitor this review and
provide feedback and recommendations.

2) Review of current schedules for employees who work at two or more worksites as part of
their regular assignment.

a. Identify how absences are covered and what the additional impacts are for other
employees based upon absences and required coverage.

b. Look at attendance patterns as reasons for absence

c. Compare attendance patterns for split positions compared to positions at a single
site.

3) Review other staffing options previously presented to employees and determine if there is
interest in examining these or other staffing options.

4) Seek custodian input via focus groups and surveys to gather information.

5) Develop a set of recommendations to be presented to labor management no later than
April 30, 2025.

6) Should any recommendations impact mandatory subjects of bargaining or require effects
bargaining, the District and Union agree to do so prior July 31, 2025.

FOR BELLEVUE SCHOOL DISTRICT FOR INTERNATIONAL UNION OF
