Executive Summary/Background Information
Academic Performance

Student-led conferences provide a platform for students to actively engage in reflecting on their
academic journey, showcasing their accomplishments, and articulating personal learning goals,
fostering a sense of ownership and responsibility for their education; this format also promotes
effective communication between students, parents, and teachers, enhancing the overall educational
experience.

In Spring 2024, BSD Student-led Conferences showcased notable improvements in student and family
attendance. Attendance participation rates rose at six of our eight schools, including an impressive 12%
increase at West Hills Stem Academy. This positive trend was underscored by the fact that half of all
schools achieved participation rates at or above 90%, affirming the effectiveness of this model in
cultivation meaningful partnerships among students, parents, and educators.
