MEMORANDUM

To: CEA Bargain Team
From: Stephanie McCleary and Kelly Liske
Date: September 15, 2024

Re: Certificated Contract Negotiations Package — Tentative Agreement 10/14/24 @ 6:19 PM

The offer from Chimacum School District 49 is encompassing all outstanding items and compensation as shown below and per
attached MOUs and proposals. Settlement also includes previous tentative agreements.

Schedule B 2024-2026
Outdoor education stipend will no longer be split by the number of teachers attending. Each teacher will receive one stipend
based on their years of experience on Level D.

MOU Schedule B recognizes those teaching responsibilities that require an extraordinary amount of time with students during
evenings or weekends as part of the required curriculum. The District and the Union will meet with a representative group
during the 2024-25 school year to study Schedule B supplemental contracts and prepare recommendations to review with the
team for June 30, 2025.
