» Establish a SMART goal to continue demonstrating
compliance with the records request rules:

» Follow up to last year’s goal: HR processed 28 requests for
public records. 23 were delivered within 30 days. Four
required additional days, but were all delivered. One was
not delivered because the records were not discovered.

» Continue a focus on the goal: By August 31, 2025, the H.R.
team will produce data that shows all requestors for
public records received an initial response within five

calendar days and delivery of records within 30 days ---
unless a later delivery date was communicated.
