Approved 6/20/24

BOARD OF DIRECTORS
Lopez Island School District #144
April 17, 2024
Library Media Center
4:00 pm
Board Work Session Minutes

A board work session is an open public meeting, and public attendance is welcome. There is no opportunity for public
comment during board work sessions since no action may be taken by the board. The board may choose to invite other
members of district staff, school staff, or outside experts to provide information.

I.

CALL TO ORDER:
Directors present: Chair Price-Morris, Vice Chair Carrier, Director Murphy, Director Taylor. Staff present:
Superintendent Smith, Principal Dr. Martin, Deirdre Steinbrueck
Chair Price-Morris called the meeting to order at 4:20 pm. A quorum was present.

II.

PLEDGE OF ALLEGIANCE AND LAND ACKNOWLEDGEMENT:
Chair Price-Morris let the flag salute and gave a land acknowledgement.

III.

BOARD ITEMS FOR DISCUSSION
A. Town Hall #2
B. Superintendent Evaluation
C. June Board Meeting Rescheduling
D. Budget
E. Policies and Procedures
F. Cameras
The board discussed items A-F.

IV.

ADJOURNMENT
Chair Price-Morris adjourned the meeting at 5:51 pm
