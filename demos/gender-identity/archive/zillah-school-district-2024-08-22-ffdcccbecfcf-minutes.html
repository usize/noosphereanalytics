ZILLAH SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 205
MINUTES
School Board of Directors – Regular Meeting
Thursday, August 22, 2024 - 7:00 p.m.
Zillah Middle School Commons
TIME AND PLACE:

A School Board of Directors-Regular Meeting was held on Thursday,
August 22, 2024 at 7:00 p.m. The meeting was held at Zillah Middle
School Commons.

ATTENDANCE:

Board Members: Jeff Brady, Dennis Jones, Nicole Garcia Schmelzer,
Craig Carlisle, and Nikki Samaniego.
Administrators: Doug Burge, and Justin Irion
Business Manager: Craig McCloud
Recording Secretary: Sonia Ruggles
Guests: Jeff Charbonneau, and Monika Charbonneau

CALL TO ORDER

Meeting called to order at 7:01 p.m. by Jeff Brady. Jeff Brady, Dennis
Jones, Nicole Garica Schmelzer, Craig Carlisle, and Nikki Samaniego
were in attendance.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES:

Motion was made by Craig Carlisle, second by Nikki
Samaniego to approve the July 25, 2024 Board Work Session,
Regular School Board Meeting Minutes. All in favor motion
carried.

APPROVAL OF AGENDA:

Motion was made by Nikki Samaniego, second by Craig
Carlisle to approve the agenda. All in favor motion carried.

HEARING SESSION:
No One addressed the Board.
I.

INFORMATION AGENDA
A.
Superintendent/Assistant Superintendent Report
1.
Start of School Information-Update
Mr. Burge shared everything is off to a good first day of school. Mr.
Burge shared fall sports will be starting this week for some High School
sports and High School Cross Country and Middle school sports will start
the week of August 26th.
2.
Enrollment Update
Mr. Burge shared our current numbers but we won’t have an official FTE
until September 1, 2024. The district is currently sitting at a headcount of
1321 as of August 22, 2024.
3.
Franz Award for Bakery

B.

II.

Mr. Burge shared we received a bid later than normal as they had a
change in staff and once we reached out they sent the bid. We have
awarded Franz Bakery as we had no other bids.
4.
Staffing Update-Information
Mr. Burge shared we hired 2 people for a bus driver position (part time
for both) and a custodian part time.
5.
2024 WASA Legislative Platform
Mr. Burge shared key points that will be discussed special education,
pupil transportation, MSOC (materials, supplies, and operating costs),
update staff allocations, modify regionalization/experience factor, reform
levy/local effort assistance, and support capital facilities. Information
sheet is attached to One Note. Mr. Burge stated he will be attending some
legislature sessions.
6.
ZHS Sports Banner
Mr. Burge shared pictures with the Board of the new Banners with state
placers of each sport that are now displayed in the High School Weight
Room. The pictures are on One Note.
7.
AmeriCorps
Mr. Irion discussed we have Ameri Corps employees back into the district.
The district pays minimal salary and it is a great benefit to our studens.
8.
Zillah School Improvement Plans 2024-2025
Mr. Irion asked the board to approve the zillah school improvement plans
that were presented during the work session. The plans are attached to
One Note.
9.
2024-2025 Out of Endorsements-Brock Anderson, Jamie Anderson,
Michael Brant, Sara Groeneweg, Molly Himes, Tammy Lewis, Cody
McDonald, Traci Patterson, Meghan Schoonover, and Lori Steinmentz
Mr. Irion asked the board to approve the staff that are out of endorsement.
Each staff has a plan in place with their building administrator.
