CERTIFICATE

I, the undersigned, Secretary of the Board of Directors (the “Board”) of Bremerton School
District No. 100-C, Kitsap County, Washington and keeper of the records of the District, DO
HEREBY CERTIFY:

I. That the attached resolution is a true and correct copy of Resolution No. 2023/2024-
18 of the District (the “Resolution’’), as finally adopted at a regular meeting of the Board held on
April 11, 2024, and duly recorded in my office.

us That said meeting was duly convened and held in all respects in accordance with
law, and to the extent required by law, due and proper notice of such meeting was given; that

members of the Board voted in the proper manner for the adoption of said Resolution; that all other
requirements and proceedings incident to the proper adoption or passage of said Resolution have
been duly fulfilled, carried out and otherwise observed, and that I am authorized to execute this
certificate.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand this 11th day of April, 2024.
