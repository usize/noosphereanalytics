  East Valley School District Board of Directors Special Board Meeting
Tuesday, September 10, 2019
    Members present: Justin Voelker, Laura Gates, Mike Novakovich
  Members absent: Todd Weger, Fred Helms
  Others present: Kelly Shea, Jodi Brown, Neale Rasmussen, Karen Cloninger, Jane Stencel, Steve Pointer, Barbara Cruse, Jaena Upton, Lucas Vinyard, Vincent Westerlund-Floyd, David Powell
    1. Opening Items
  1.1 Call Meeting to Order
The meeting was called to order at 6:00 pm.
  1.2 Pledge of Allegiance
Led by Mr. Voelker.
  2. Consent Agenda
  2.1 Agenda
Approved as presented.
  2.2 Approve the minutes of the August 27, 2019 Regular Board Meeting
Approved the minutes of the August 27, 2019 regular board meeting.
  2.3 Personnel
Approved as presented.
  2.4 Business Items
Approved the contract with Frontier Behavioral Health for the FASST Counselor - 2019-2020.
  2.5 Consideration and Approval of Consent Items
With a motion made by Ms. Gates, seconded by Mr. Novakovich, all were in agreement to approve the Consent Agenda as presented.
  3. Information
  3.1 CCS Building Report
Mr. Pointer introduced Lucas Vinyard, David Powell and Vincent Westerlund-Floyd, three 8th grade students. They presented information on Robotics and Science, which has helped the students with problem solving and teamwork. They also shared information on Algebra, Social Studies and discipline in the classroom. Mr. Pointer then introduced Jaena Upton, a 2nd grader, who read The Night Before Kindergarten.
  4. Administrative
  4.1 Board Policies: 2410; 3115; 3120; 3207; 3211; 3231; 5010; 5202; 5251; 5253; 5260; 5411; 5525 - 2nd Reading - ACTION
With a motion made by Mr. Voelker, seconded by Ms. Gates, all were in agreement to approve the following board policies: Board Policy 2410 High School Graduation Requirements; Board Policy 3115 - Students Experiencing Homelessness - Enrollment Rights and Services; Board Policy 3120 - Enrollment; Board Policy 3207 - Prohibition of Harassment, Intimidation, or Bullying; Board Policy 3211 - Gender-Inclusive Schools; Board Policy 3231 - Student Records; Board Policy 5010 - Nondiscrimination and Affirmative Action; Board Policy 5202 - Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration Mandated Drug & Alcohol Testing; Board Policy 5251 - Conflicts of Interest; Board Policy 5253 - Maintaining Professional Staff/Student Boundaries; Board Policy 5260 - Personnel Records; Board Policy 5411 - Staff Vacations; Board Policy 5525 - Professional, Civic and Service Organization Memberships.
  5. Executive Session
  5.1 Review the performance of a public employee
The board recessed at 6:12 pm for 3 minutes and went into Executive Session at 6:15 pm for 15 minutes to review the performance of a public employee with no action to follow.
  6. Adjournment
  6.1 Adjournment
The board reconvened into the special meeting and with no further business appearing before the board, the meeting was adjourned at 6:30 pm.
