  September 11, 2019 Special Business Meeting and Workshop at 6:30pm 
  MINUTES
South Whidbey Elementary School
5380 Maxwelton Rd., Langley, WA
Generated by Kristina Macarro on Thursday, September 12, 2019
    Present: Directors Andrea Downs (via Zoom), Damian Greene, Julie Hadden, Linda Racicot, Shawn Nowlin, and Superintendent Jo Moccia.
  1. SPECIAL BUSINESS MEETING
Director Racicot called the business meeting to order at 6:30 pm.
Information, Procedural: B. SWSD Vision, Mission and Goals
Director Nowlin moved to approve the agenda and minutes. Director Hadden seconded. The motion carried unanimously.
  2. CONSENT AGENDA - ACTION
  Action (Consent): A. Approval of the SWHS Cross Country Team Field Trip to Blue Lake, Oregon
Recommended Action: Approval of the consent agenda as presented.
  Action (Consent): B. Approval of the SWES 6th Grade Field Trip to Langley Marina and Sound
Recommended Action: Approval of the consent agenda as presented.
  Action (Consent): C. Approval of the SWES 4th Grade Field Trip to Langley Marina
  Recommended Action: Approval of the consent agenda as presented.
Director Greene moved to approve the consent agenda as presented.  Director Hadden seconded.  The motion carried unanimously.
3. ADJOURN SPECIAL BUSINESS MEETING
Director Racicot adjourned the special business meeting and moved into workshop format.
  4. WORKSHOP: Discussion with South Whidbey Schools Foundation
Shelly Ackerman discussed the work of the South Whidbey Schools Foundation with the Board noting that teacher grants will be open all year for  application. The Board
thanked the Foundation for their support and the great work they do for the district.  SWSF would like to meet twice a year to update the Board. 
  5. WORKSHOP: Presentation Regarding South Whidbey Community Center Application for Historic Register - Gail LaVassar
South Whidbey Community Center Director, Gail LaVassar, presented to the Board on the Waterman's request to apply to make specific parts of the SWCC part of the Historic Register.  The Board discussed the possible future affects of such application. Requested more information to be presented. 
  6. WORKSHOP: Policy Review
Director Downs led the discussion on all of the listed policies and recommended changes. 
  Discussion: A. Policy 2410 Essential Update: High School Graduation Requirements
  Discussion: B. Policy 2413 Essential Update: Equivalency Credit for Career and Technical Education Courses
  Discussion: C. Policy 3207 Essential Update: Prohibition of Harassment, Intimidation and Bullying
Change type from imitation to intimidation.
  Discussion: D. Policy 3210 Essential Update - Non Discrimination
Need to review federal language that will be released soon.
  Discussion: E. Policy 3211 Essential Update: Gender Inclusive Schools
  Discussion: F. Policy 3231 Essential Update: Students Records
  Discussion: G. Policy 3120 Essential Update: Enrollment

  Discussion: H. Policy 3416 Essential Update: Medication at School
  Discussion: I. Policy 3423 Replace with New Policy: Parental Administration of Marijuana for Medical Purposes

  Discussion: L. Policy 5253 Encouraged Update - Maintaining Professional Staff/Student Boundaries

    Workshop adjourned at 7:55 pm.
