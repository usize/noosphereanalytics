Results 1 - Interpretation

Students must have opportunities to learn a rich
curriculum in inclusive classrooms and have access to
learning opportunities that encourage ongoing growth
and development. Every student learns and achieves at
high levels and it is our responsibility to help each student
learn, grow and be challenged while they acquire
knowledge and skills that will help them to be successful
In an ever changing world. Students must be equipped to
lead a life that is personally rewarding and professionally
meaningful.


~ Commitment to > Equity
(R-1 = R-2 + R-3 + OE-10 + OE-11 + OE-14)

To ensure academic success for all students by closing opportunity gaps
for students and providing equitable and inclusive working and learning
environments for all students, families, staff, and communities.

System: Theory of Action

If we systematically analyze student performance data,
implement evidence-based instructional practices,
utilize high quality curriculum, provide meaningful
professional learning for administrators and teachers,
plan for differentiated support for students, foster a
culture of high expectations and continuous
improvement, and ensure alignment with state
standards, then we will achieve substantial and
sustainable growth in overall academic performance
across all grade levels and subject areas, resulting in
enhanced student achievement, increased graduation
rates, and improved college and career readiness for all
students
