2023-2024 ELA SMARTIE GOAL REFLECTION:

By the end of the 2023-2024 school year 80% of Gause students in
grades 3-5 will meet or exceed the three essential standards in ELA
as identified by Washougal School District grade level teams.

Through the District PLC Grade Level Teams’ work, it was decided that the teams
would continue the work on the Scope & Sequence and Common Assessments in ELA
in the 2024-2025 school year. This was part of the grade level PLC work this past
August during the PD days. The goal is to be able to meet as grade level teams across
the district to analyze data in the 3 Essential Standards in ELA this year.
