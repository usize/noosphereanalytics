Everett Public Schools
Board of Directors
Special Meeting Minutes
Friday, November 17, 2017
      1. Call to Order
1.01 Call to Order
  The Board of Directors of Everett Public Schools, Snohomish County, Washington, attended the WSSDA Annual Conference held Friday-Sunday, November 17-19, 2017, at the Hyatt Regency Bellevue, 900 Bellevue Way NE, Bellevue, Washington. President Mason called the special meeting to order at 9 a.m.
  2. Roll Call
2.01 Roll Call
  Directors attending the conference were Carol Andrews, Pam LeSesne, Caroline Mason, Traci Mitchell, and Ted Wenta. Also present for the conference on Friday, November 17, 2017 was student representative Dhruvik Parikh.
  3. Adoption of Agenda
3.01 Adoption of Agenda
  The agenda was adopted by general consent.
  4. WSSDA Annual Conference 2017
4.01 WSSDA Conference Agenda, Friday

Directors attended the opening general session. President Mason recessed the special meeting at 10:40 a.m. for session attendance. The meeting was reconvened at noon for the general session and awards luncheon and recessed at 2:10 p.m. for a five-minute break. All directors were present. The meeting was reconvened at 2:15 p.m. for directors to attend the Choosing the Right Leader for Tomorrow's Schools conference session. Directors Mason, Mitchell, and Wenta were present. The meeting was recessed at 3:15 p.m. for session attendance to be reconvened at 5 p.m. at the Equis Restaurant at the Hyatt Regency Hotel.

President Mason reconvened the meeting at 5 p.m. at the Equis Restaurant at the Hyatt Regency Hotel for the directors to participate in a networking event. Student Representative Parikh excused himself from the rest of the conference due to a prior commitment. At 6:20 p.m., President Mason announced the meeting would move to McCormick & Schmick's restaurant at Lincoln Square, 700 Bellevue Way NE #115, Bellevue, Washington. Directors walked to the restaurant and President Mason began the meeting at 6:30 p.m. with all directors, the superintendent, and the deputy superintendent present.

Directors reviewed the content of the sessions attended that day, discussed the implications for community engagement activities and major program investment decisions before the board, and priorities for the next day's session attendance.

President Mason recessed the meeting at 8:30 p.m. to reconvene the next morning at 8 a.m. at the Hyatt Regency Bellevue, 900 Bellevue Way NE, Bellevue, Washington.
  4.02 WSSDA Conference Agenda, Saturday
  President Mason reconvened the special meeting at 8 a.m. at the Hyatt Regency Bellevue, 900 Bellevue Way NE, Bellevue, Washington. All directors were present. Directors participated in WSSDA's governance process.

President Mason recessed the special meeting at 9:05 a.m. for a 10-minute break. The meeting was reconvened at 9:15 a.m. for directors to attend The Secret Sauce to Bond Passage conference session. Directors Andrews, LeSesne, and Wenta were present. The special meeting was recessed at 10:15 a.m. for a 30-minute break and reconvened at 10:45 a.m. for director attendance at the 2017 Legislative Session Essentials: McCleary and More conference session. Directors Andrews, LeSesne, Mitchell, and Wenta were present. The meeting was recessed at 11:45 a.m., to be reconvened at noon for the general session. President Mason reconvened the meeting for the general session at noon. All directors were present. President Mason recessed the special meeting at 1:30 p.m. for a 30-minute break. The special meeting was reconvened at 2 p.m. for directors to attend Conducting a Transparent, Legal and Successful Superintendent Search conference session. Directors LeSesne, Mason, and Wenta were present. President Mason recessed the special meeting at 3 p.m. for a 30-minute break to be reconvened at 3:30 p.m. for session attendance. The special meeting was reconvened at 3:30 p.m. for directors to attend the Keynote Breakout with Dr. Jamie Vollmer: Schools Cannot Do This Alone conference session. Directors Andrews, LeSesne, and Mitchell were present. The special meeting was recessed at 4:30 p.m. for session attendance to be reconvened at 6:20 p.m. in the lobby of the Hyatt Regency Bellevue, 900 Bellevue Way NE, Bellevue, Washington.

At 6:20 p.m., President Mason reconvened the special meeting in the lobby of the Hyatt Regency Bellevue and announced the meeting would move to McCormick & Schmick's restaurant at Lincoln Square, 700 Bellevue Way NE #115, Bellevue, Washington. Directors walked to the restaurant and President Mason began the meeting at 6:30 p.m. with all directors, the superintendent, and the deputy superintendent present.

Directors reviewed the content of the sessions attended that day, the implications of other school districts’ election experiences for the board’s upcoming priority decisions, the successes districts are experiencing with various communications strategies and engagement tools and processes, implications of legislative decisions on local school funding options and challenges, and the success rates of various districts’ proposals over the past two years.
