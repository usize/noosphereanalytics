APPROVED 8/28/24
BOARD OF DIRECTORS
Lopez Island School District #144
August 26, 2024
Library Media Center/LISD Zoom
4:00-6:00 PM
Board Special Meeting Minutes

Special Board meetings are open public meetings and public attendance is welcome.

I.

CALL TO ORDER:
Called to order at 4:06 pm.. Directors present: Chair Price-Morris, Director Murphy, Director Taylor,
Director O’Connell. Staff present: Superintendent Smith, J.Hammond. A quorum was present.

II.

PLEDGE OF ALLEGIANCE AND LAND ACKNOWLEDGEMENT:
Chair Price-Morris led the flag salute and gave the land acknowledgement.

III.

ADJUSTMENTS AND APPROVAL OF AGENDA:
Director Murphy moved to approve the agenda. No discussion. Approved unanimously.

IV.

NEW BUSINESS:
A. Board Evaluation
The Board reviewed and discussed the board self-evaluation results.
B. Review of Strategic Plan
The Board reviewed the District’s Strategic Plan and numerated the strategic initiatives.
C. Board areas for growth
The Board identified three goals for board growth.
D. Strategic Initiatives set as Board Priorities
The Board identified three draft strategic priorities and district goals. They plan to discuss
strategic priorities.
E. LISD Board Calendar
Discussion of proposed dates for work sessions and regular meetings each month: 9/10/14,
9/25/24, 10/9/24, 10/23/24, 11/6/24, 11/20/24, 12/4/24, 12/18/24, 1/8/25, 1/22/25, 2/12/25,
2/26/25, 3/12/25, 3/26/25, 4/9/25, 4/23/25, 5/14/25, 5/28/25, 6/11/25, 6/25/25, 7/9/25, 7/23/25,
8/13/25, 8/27/25.

V.

ADJOURNMENT:
Chair Price-Morris adjourned meeting at 6:01 pm.
