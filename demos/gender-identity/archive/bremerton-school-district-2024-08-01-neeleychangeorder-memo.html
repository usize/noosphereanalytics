TO: Bremerton School District Board of Directors
From: Dr. Garth Steedman, Acting Superintendent
SUBJECT: Neeley Construction Company - Change Order #5

DATE: August 1, 2024

ACTION:

Recommending that the Bremerton School District (BSD) Board of Directors approve a $50,505.11
contract increase to the Neeley Construction Company’s contract for additional services performed on
the Mountain View Middle School Multi-Use Sport Complex project.

BACKGROUND:

On March 30, 2023 the board awarded a contract to Neeley Construction Company in the amount of
$13,181,000.00 for construction services on the Mountain View Middle School Multi-Use Sports
Complex project. Several contract anendments have been approved since the initial award bringing the
contract total to $13,236,308.17.

BENEFIT TO THE DISTRICT:
Approval of this project will allow payment for additional services because of unexpected project costs
due to repairs, modifications, and revisions.

COST TO THE DISTRICT:
The contract modification will add $50,505.11 to the current project cost of $13,236,308.17 for a total
contract amount of $13,286,813.28 plus Washington State sales tax.

Original contract amount $13,181,000.00
