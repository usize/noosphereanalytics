Student Referrals at Cape Horn-Skye will decrease
from the 2023-2024 daily average of 1.91 to a 2024-2025 daily average of 1.5.
We will achieve this through continuing to teach and reinforce student behavior around

* Strengthen schoolwide teaching
* Reinforce areas needing improvement
* “Lunch & Learn”
* Increase supervision
