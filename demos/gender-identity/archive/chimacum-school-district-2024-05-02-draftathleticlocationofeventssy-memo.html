To: Chimacum and Port Townsend School Boards
Cc: Superintendents & High School Principals
From: Shelby Box, Athletic Director

Date: May 2, 2024

Re: Location of Athletic Events - Draft

Context: This memo serves to communicate practice and competition venues for this coming
school year (2024-2025).

Football: Practice will be held in PT. Games will be held at Memorial Stadium. PT will host 3
home games, Chimacum will host 1. The proposed date for Chimacum to host will be 9/20.

Girls Soccer: Practice will be held in Chimacum. Most games will be held at Memorial Stadium.
As of right now | don’t have any Saturday games scheduled. | will try for at least 2 and those will
be held at Chimacum.

Volleyball: Practice will be held in Chimacum. Games will be held in Chimacum.

Cross Country: Practice will be held in PT. Meets will be held at PT Golf Course.

Swim: Practice will be held at Mountain View Pool in PT. Meets will be held at Mountain View
Pool in PT.

Boys Tennis: Practice will be held in Chimacum. Matches will be held in Chimacum.

Boys Basketball: Practice will be held in Chimacum. Games will be split as evenly as possible
between Chimacum and PT.

Girls Basketball: Practice will be held in PT. Games will be split as evenly as possible between
Chimacum and PT.

Wrestling: Practice will be held in PT. Meets will be held in PT.

Track & Field: Practice will be held in PT. Meets will be held in PT.


Boys Soccer: Practice will be held in Chimacum. Varsity games will be held at Memorial
Stadium. JV games will be held in Chimacum.

Baseball: Practice will be held in Chimacum. Games will be held in Chimacum.

Fastpitch: Practice will be held in PT. | would like to have 3 games in Chimacum and the rest in
PT. | thought the environment when Baseball and Fastpitch played at the same time made for a
good environment. | have not seen a schedule for either of these sports at the writing of this
memo but my plan would be to have joint games for those 3 games if at all possible.

Golf: Practice will be determined by the Athletic Director with input from coaches. PT Golf
Course is under new management and this last season they made a designated locker room for
the athletes where they can safely store their clubs. Matches will be determined by the Athletic
Director with input from the coaches. Golf seems to be the sport with the most issues when it
comes to transportation.

Girls Tennis: Practice will be held in PT. Matches will be held in PT.
