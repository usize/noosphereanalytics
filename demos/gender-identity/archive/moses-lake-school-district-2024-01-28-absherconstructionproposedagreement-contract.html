Absher Construction Proposed Agreement

1. Board Approval. This agreement is contingent on approval by the MLSD Board.
We anticipate this agreement will be presented for the Board’s consideration on
November 21. If not approved by the Board on November 21 (or at a subsequent
Board meeting if for some reason the agreement can’t be considered on 11/21),
the agreement is null/void/and of no effect.

2. Onor before December 6, MLSD will provide to Absher a comprehensive punch
list / 100% completion list (both building and site). MLSD will endeavor to pare
down open items. Note: this list may not be 100% up to date and may include
some items that are complete, as no updated on-site reviews will be conducted.

3. Week of December 9: the parties will conduct a joint walkthrough to discuss the
punch list and clarify open items as-needed. At this walkthrough, the parties may
agree for some remaining work to be performed over Winter break, if feasible.

4. January 6: Absher will provide a detailed response to the punch list. Absher will
endeavor to address all open items per the contract and minimize disputed /
open items.

5. By January 22: MLSD will provide a response to Absher’s January 6 response
identifying either (a) agree to Absher’s response, (b) any items for negotiation or
(c) identify if MLSD believes Absher’s response is unworkable. At this point,
Absher and/or MLSD may in its discretion cease negotiations and elect not to
proceed per items (6) and after below.

6. January 28: Assuming both parties elect to proceed, the parties will reconvene
to discuss the punch list, including open issues. This meeting will be in-person in
Seattle with Chris Soelling in attendance. Before this meeting (ideally with some
lead time so MLSD has some time to review), Absher will propose for discussion:

1. A detailed schedule and work plan for performance of remaining work.

2. A list of subcontractors that Absher plans to engage to perform remaining
work, with attached proposals/quotes.

1. Absher may request that the District authorize release of
contract balance by joint check to these subcontractors to facilitate
performance of remaining work.

2. MLSD will consider any Absher’s above request to
release payment to these subcontractors in January in connection
with planning for remaining work. Board approval will be required
for any release of contract funds if MLSD elects to agree to
Absher’s proposed subcontractor payments. Note: any payments
must be made following contractual procedures (i.e., submission of
a documented Application for Payment and subcontractor invoices).

3. Absher and MLSD will endeavor to reach an agreement on remaining
work to be performed January 28 — End of May, including that some work
will need to occur in May.

7. June 16: the parties will attend a renewed mediation with Chris Soelling to
consider all open issues, including both parties’ affirmative claims/defenses.

8. This agreement does not modify any terms of the Contract, including but not
limited to the required Contract Time or Contract Documents. Both parties
reserve all claims and defenses under the Contract.
