Approved 1/24/24
BOARD OF DIRECTORS
Lopez Island School District #144
December 28, 2023
Library Media Center/Zoom
6:00 pm
Special Board Meeting

Meeting Link can be found on LISD Website
A Special Board Meeting is an open public meeting, and public attendance is welcome.
I.

CALL TO ORDER:
Directors present: Chair Price-Morris, Vice-Chair Carrier, Director Taylor, Director Murphy. Staff present:
Interim Superintendent Martin.
Chair Price-Morris called the meeting to order at 6:04 pm. A quorum was present.

II.

PLEDGE OF ALLEGIANCE AND LAND ACKNOWLEDGEMENT:
Chair Price-Morris led the flag salute and gave the land acknowledgement.

III.

SUPERINTENDENT CONTRACT:
Chair Price-Morris moved to approve the contract. No discussion. All voted to approve the contract.

IV.

INTERIM SUPERINTENDENT:
A. Oath of Office for Superintendent
Chair administered the Oath of Office to Brady Smith.
B. Resolution 23/24-11 Appointment of District Claims Agent
C. Resolution 23/24-12 Authorization of Warrant Signature
D. Resolution 23/24-13 Authorization to Invest Funds
E. Resolution 23/24-14 Designation of District Agent
F. Resolution 23/24-15 Designation of Auditing Officers
G. Resolution 23/24-16 Authorization of Facsimile Signature
H. Resolution 23/24-17 Certified Signatures of District Personnel Authorized to Sign School Construction
Project Document
I. Resolution 23/24-18 Transfer of Financial Institution and Credit Card Agent
Director Murphy moved to approve Resolutions 23/24-11 through Resolution 23/24-18, instating Brady
Smith, superintendent as authorized party for each. No discussion. Vice-Chair Carrier led a roll call
vote. KT-Yes, SM-Yes, MP-M-Yes, KC-Yes. Resolutions 23/24-11, 23/24-12, 23/24-13, 23/24-14,
23/24-15, 23/24-16, 23/24-17, 23/24-18 were approved unanimously (4/4).

V.

ADJOURNMENT:
Chair Price-Morris moved to adjourn the meeting. No discussion. All voted in favor. The Chair adjourned the
meeting at 6:10 pm.
