TOUTLE LAKE SCHOOL DISTRICT #130
Board of Director’s Meeting
Tuesday, November 26, 2024 @ 6:00 pm in the High School Library
MINUTES
Members Present: Board Chairman Sherry Schwinn, Board of Directors Susan Dorcheus, Lisa Mathes, Heather
Schoonover and Janell Wheatley. Superintendent Chris Schumaker
1. Flag Salute – Flag salute was led by Sherry Schwinn
2. Approval of Minutes of the Regular Board Meeting on October 22, 2024
Motion: Director Janell Wheatley made a motion to approve minutes of the Regular Board
Meeting on October 22, 2024 as presented. The motion unanimously carried by a vote of 5-0.
3. Adoption of Agenda
Motion: Director Lisa Mathes made a motion to adopt the agenda as presented. The motion
unanimously carried by a vote of 5-0.

B. Personnel

A. Second Reading of Revised Policies and Procedures
Policy and Procedure 2410 – High School Graduation Requirements

Motion: Director Heather Schoonover made a motion to approve revised Policy and Procedure 2410 –
High School Graduation Requirements; Policy and Procedure 3205 – Sex Discrimination and Sex-Based
Harassment of Students Prohibited – Grievance Procedure and Implementation Procedure; Procedure
3211P – Gender-Inclusive Schools; Policy and Procedure 3246 – Restraint, Isolation and Other Uses of
Reasonable Force; Policy 3432 – Emergencies; Policy and Procedure 4040 – Public Access to District
Records; Policy 6022 – Minimum Fund Balance; Policy and Procedure 6220 – Bid or Request for
Proposal Requirements; and Policy and Procedure 6513 – Workplace Violence Prevention as presented.
The motion unanimously carried by a vote of 4-0. (Director Susan Dorcheus left prior to Board
Committee Reports).
B. Second Reading of New Policies and Procedures
Policy and Procedure 3206 – Pregnant and Parenting Students
Policy and Procedure 5012 – Parental, Family, or Marital Status, and Pregnancy or Related

Motion: Director Lisa Mathes made a motion to approve new Policy and Procedure 3206 – Pregnant
and Parenting Students and Policy and Procedure 5012 – Parental, Family, or Marital Status, and
Pregnancy or Related Conditions of Staff as presented. The motion unanimously carried by a vote of 4-0.
C. Second Reading of Reviewed Policies and Procedures
Policy 1450 – Absence of a Board Member

Policy 3211 – Gender-Inclusive Schools

Motion: Director Janell Wheatley made a motion to approve reviews Policy 1450 – Absence of a Board

as presented. The motion unanimously carried by a vote of 4-0.
