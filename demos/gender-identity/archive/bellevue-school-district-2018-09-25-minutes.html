  6.2 School Visits Schedule and Discussion
  The board reviewed the school visits schedule 2018.2019 and discussed the purpose of school visits to ensure these meet district needs. The board agreed that school visits remain a valuable tool to understand how to better support schools.  The board discussed the value of meeting with parents, staff, and students. It was agreed that a themed approach to the visits could be useful and agreed to the Superintendent and President setting the format of visits to ensure the theme aligns to the strategic plan.
