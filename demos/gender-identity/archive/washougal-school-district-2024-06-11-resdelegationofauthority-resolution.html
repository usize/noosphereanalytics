WASHOUGAL SCHOOL DISTRICT 112-6
RESOLUTION NO. 2023-24-06
Board/Superintendent Delegation of Authority

WHEREAS, Washougal School District Policy 1620 was revised and approved on May 27,
2014, designating the Superintendent as the chief executive of the board, and;

WHEREAS, Washougal School District Policy 1620 delegates administrative functions to the
Superintendent, and;

WHEREAS, Washougal School District Policy 1620 allows the Superintendent to delegate
administrative functions to staff members,

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the following named Washougal School
District employees are authorized per this Resolution and Washougal School District Policy
and Procedure to sign the following contracts on behalf of the Board:

1.

Purchase Orders
Aaron Hansen, Interim Superintendent
Les Brown, Director of Communications, Technology & Operations

The following are limited to $15,000 per purchase order, unless approved by the
Superintendent or Business Manager:

Aaron Hansen, Interim Superintendent

Tracey MacLachlan, Director of Teaching & Learning (staff development, instruction
and curriculum)

Connor McCroskey, Director of Special Services

Lester Brown, Director of Communications & Technology Director

Margaret Rice, Director of CTE and Culinary Services

Building Principals (for their own building budgets)

Contract Agreements per RCW 28A.335.190 (up to bid requirement amount of
$75,000 as of 9/1/2013)

Aaron Hansen, Interim Superintendent

Les Brown, Director of Communications, Technology & Operations

Small Works Roster per RCW 39.04.155 (up to small works limitation of $300,000)
Aaron Hansen, Interim Superintendent

Capital Outlay Purchases up to $100,000 for major construction
Aaron Hansen, Interim Superintendent

Supplemental Contracts for Certificated Employees
Aaron Hansen, Interim Superintendent

. Auditing of Claims as Specified in RCW 42.24.080

Les Brown, Director of Communications, Technology & Operations


7. Interlocal Agreements (which provide the ability to use other agencies’ bids and
services
Aaron Hansen, Interim Superintendent
Les Brown, Director of Communications, Technology & Operations

8. All Fundraisers
Aaron Hansen, Interim Superintendent

Authorization of the individuals listed above ceases at the end of their employment.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that at the next regularly scheduled Board meeting, the
Board of Directors will ratify such contracts through its approval of payment of bills, through
the consent agenda, or through individual item action, per District practice and procedure, to
comply with RCW 28A.320.035.

ADOPTED by the Board of Directors of the Washougal School District No. 112, Clark
County, Washington, at a meeting thereof held this 11th day of June 2024.

Angela Hancock, President Jim Cooper, Vice-President

Chuck Carpenter Sadie McKenzie

Ida Royer Mary Templeton, Board Secretary
