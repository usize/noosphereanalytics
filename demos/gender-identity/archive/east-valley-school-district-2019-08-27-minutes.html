  4. Information
  4.1 Board Report
Ms. Gates attended the All Staff meeting at EVHS. Mr. Weger attended the Trentwood open house. Mr. Voelker attended the EVMS open house.
  4.2 Financial Report
Mr. Rasmussen reported on the month of July, 2019. The revenues for July were $5,051,705.91 and year to date they were $52,935,777.08. The expenditures for the month of July were $4,960,913.70 and year to date they were $51,922,198.59. The ending fund balance for July was $7,384,564.80.
  4.3 Legislative Report
Mr. Voelker did not have a report.
  4.4 Superintendent Report
Superintendent Shea reported that Alec Vermaire will be at the next board meeting to discuss the GNL/GSL merger. The new parking lot at Trentwood is behind schedule, but accommodations will happen allowing parents to drop students off and the buses will use the cut out areas temporarily. Mr. Shea attended a meeting regarding the Forker Bigelow Gulch road project. The road construction, originally scheduled to start in 2020, between EVMS and EVHS will begin in the Spring of 2021. The Back to School Fair served 748 students with over 25 vendors. The All Staff meeting went well.
  5. Administrative
  5.1 Resolution 2775 - Cancel Stale Dated Warrants
With a motion made by Mr. Voelker, seconded by Mr. Novakovich, all were in agreement to approve Resolution 2775 - Cancel Stale Dated Warrants.
  5.2 Revise Board Policy 3241 - (new title) STUDENT DISCIPLINE (remove the following) CLASSROOM MANAGEMENT, DISCIPLINE, AND CORRECTIVE ACTION - 1st Reading - ACTION Requested
This policy revision requires approval prior to the start of school. With a motion made by Mr. Voelker, seconded by Ms. Gates, all were in agreement to revise Policy 3241 - Student Discipline.
  5.3 Revise Board Policy 6100 - Revenues from Local State and Federal Sources - 1st Reading - ACTION Requested
This policy revision requires approval prior to the start of school. With a motion made by Mr. Weger, seconded by Ms. Gates, all were in agreement to revise Policy 6100 - Revenues from Local State and Federal Sources.
  5.4 Revise Board Policy 2410 High School Graduation Requirements - 1st Reading
This policy requires revision due to HB 1599 which delinks statewide assessment from graduation and establishes graduation pathway options to the class of 2020 and beyond.
  5.5 Revise Board Policy 3115 - Students Experiencing Homelessness - Enrollment Rights and Services - 1st Reading
This policy requires revisions due to changes from SB 5324 to SB5325; a requirement to establish a building point of contact regardless of the number of unaccompanied youth.
  5.6 Revise Board Policy 3120 - Enrollment - 1st Reading
This policy requires revision to clarify language surrounding the enrollment of students meeting the definition of homeless.
  5.7 Revise Board Policy 3207 - Prohibition of Harassment, Intimidation, or Bullying - 1st Reading
This policy requires revision due to SB5689 which requires a revision clarifying that harassment, intimidation and bullying are related but separate behaviors and each must be addressed appropriately.
  5.8 New Board Policy 3211 - Gender-Inclusive Schools - 1st Reading
This is a new Essential policy required by state law.
  5.9 Revise Board Policy 3231 - Student Records - 1st Reading
This policy requires revision to clarify language surrounding the enrollment of students meeting the definition of homeless.
  5.10 Revise Board Policy 5010 - Nondiscrimination and Affirmative Action - 1st Reading
This policy revision is required to reflect changes in legal references and testing accommodations.
  5.11 Revise Board Policy 5202 - Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration Mandated Drug & Alcohol Testing - 1st Reading
This policy needs updating to reflect current laws and to remove out of date language.
  5.12 Revise Board Policy 5251 - Conflicts of Interest - 1st Reading
This policy was completely out of date and requires major updating including legal updates and use of district property.
  5.13 Revise Board Policy 5253 - Maintaining Professional Staff/Student Boundaries - 1st Reading
This policy requires updates to clarify language and add legal references and cross references.
  5.14 Revise Board Policy 5260 - Personnel Records - 1st Reading
This policy requires revisions to clarify rules surrounding personnel records.
  5.15 Revise Board Policy 5411 - (new title) Staff Vacations (remove the following) And Leave Provisions for Non-Represented Personnel - 1st Reading
This policy requires revisions to accurately reflect the rules for staff vacations as the current policy is completely out of date.
  5.16 Revise Board Policy 5525 - (new title) Professional, Civic and Service Organization Memberships (remove the following) Association - 1st Reading
This revision will strengthen the board of directors’ encouragement of administrative staff participation in professional, civic and service organizations.
