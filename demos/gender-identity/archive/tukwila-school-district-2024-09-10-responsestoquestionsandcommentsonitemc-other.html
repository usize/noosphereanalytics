Responses to the Board’s questions/comments on Agenda Item 5.C — Annual Report — Policies 3205 & 5011 for the
September 10, 2024 Regular Board Meeting:

1. Agenda Item 5.C — Annual Report — Sexual Harassment Prohibited per Policies 3205 & 5011

1. What is the reporting protocol for a student who wants to report an incident?
Aaron Draganov: Students filing a claim may report it to any school staff member or directly to a school or
district administrator. Any claims not submitted directly to a school administrator will be redirected to a for
immediate visibility and initial processing.

2. Policy 3205P requires an update since it lists Harvard Jones as the Title IX coordinator.
Aaron Draganov: Thank you. The uploaded version will be updated with Gabriel Gutierrez's name
