Policy No. 2023
Instruction

LESSON PLANS

To insure proper planning and continuity of instruction, each teacher will prepare lesson plans
for daily instruction. To facilitate effective instruction and in preparation for possible substitute
teachers, lesson plans must be prepared sufficiently in advance of class presentation. The
format for the lesson plans will be specified by the building principal, will be reviewed on a
regular basis and must be readily available in the event a substitute teacher is needed.
