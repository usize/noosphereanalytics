MASTER PROFESSIONAL SERVICES AGREEMENT
This Master Professional Services Agreement (“Agreement”) effective May 1, 2024, is by and between
OAC SERVICES, INC., a Washington corporation (hereinafter referred to as “OAC”) and BREMERTON
SCHOOL DISTRICT, a Washington School District (hereinafter referred to as “Client”) whose address is
134 Marion Ave N, Bremerton, WA 98312 for the Project identified below. OAC and Client are each a
“Party,” and together they constitute the “Parties.”

This Agreement is incomplete without the following attachments:
v¥ Exhibit A (GENERAL CONDITIONS)
¥ Exhibit B (SAMPLE SERVICE DIRECTIVE)
vY Exhibit C (REIMBURSABLE EXPENSES)

By signing below, Client acknowledges receipt of the foregoing attachments and their incorporation herein
as if fully set forth. This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties respecting its
subject matter and supersedes all prior and contemporaneous commitments, negotiations,
representations, or agreements, whether written or oral, concerning its subject matter.

A. PROJECT:

Project Title: Bremerton SD Bond Program

Project No. R14-240082.00

Project Address: 134 Marion Ave N, Bremerton, WA 98312

Project Description: Support the initial Bond Program startup, prepare for the various projects
identified in the Bond Program, and manage projects as more specifically described in each Service
Directive.

B. SCOPE: On the terms set forth in the attached Exhibit A, OAC shall perform the services set forth
in each Service Directive mutually executed by the Parties describing the scope (the “Services’).
Each Service Directive shall be in substantially the same form as attached hereto as Exhibit B. The
Parties will work collaboratively to establish a schedule for performance following execution of each
Service Directive.

C. COMPENSATION: Client will pay for such Services at OAC’s then-current hourly rates, except as
otherwise specifically set forth in the Service Directive. If the Service Directive indicates that
compensation is on a Fixed Fee basis, the amount shall be paid in equal installments over the course
of the Term, except as otherwise defined in the Service Directive.

D. TERM: This term of this Agreement shall extend not more than Thirty-Six (36) months after the
Effective Date (the “Term’), unless terminated sooner as provided herein. Notwithstanding the
expiration of the Term, any Service Directive effective prior to the end of the Term shall remain in
effect until the completion of Services described therein, unless earlier terminated by the written
agreement of the Parties. The Parties may mutually agree to extend the Term by executing an
amendment to this Agreement.

Each individual executing this Agreement on behalf of a Party warrants that such person is authorized to
bind such Party to this Agreement and its terms.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the Parties have made and executed this Agreement effective as set forth
above.

OAC Services Inc. BREMERTON SCHOOL DISTRICT
