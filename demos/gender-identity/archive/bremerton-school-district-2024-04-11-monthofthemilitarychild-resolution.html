BREMERTON SCHOOL DISTRICT 100-C
134 Marion Avenue N.
Bremerton, Washington 98312

Resolution 2023/2024-16
Month of the Military Child

WHEREAS, Washington is the 5th in the nation in the number of active duty military, with more than
55,155 military personnel and more than 90,246 dependents and almost 11,366 reservists;

WHEREAS, Kitsap County is home to over one third of the region's defense workforce of which 90”
are civilian employees with more than 35,642 daily workers, including 11,220 military personnel,
19,922 civilian personnel and 7,500 defense contractors;

WHEREAS, approximately 350 active military families, and 800 students with families in
DOD/sub-contractors for a total of approximately 1,150 military-connected children enrolled in
the Bremerton School District;

WHEREAS, military children face unique challenges related to military transitions and parental
deployment;

WHEREAS, military children and their families serve as an essential source of support and
encouragement for armed service members;

WHEREAS, military children should be acknowledged for the sacrifices they make and celebrated
for the courage they display as the children of our nation's armed service members; and

WHEREAS, the Department of Defense, school districts, and organizations recognize April as the
Month of the Military Child:

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, that the Bremerton School District Board of
Directors recognizes and salutes military children by recognizing the month of April 2024 as the
Month of the Military Child and April 15,2024 as Purple Up! Day.

ADOPTED AND APPROVED, this 11" day of April, 2024, at a regular meeting of the Board of
Directors, Bremerton School District 100-C.
