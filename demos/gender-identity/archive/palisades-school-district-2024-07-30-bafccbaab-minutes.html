Pomeroy School Board Meeting Review
July 30th, 2024
.
1. Board Director Allison McKeirnan called the board meeting to order at 6:00 pm. All board members
were present. Rachel Gwinn and Kelly McKeirnan were also in attendance.
2. Minutes for the June 27th, 2024 meeting was approved, as well as the bills and vouchers.
3. Rachel gave a brief overview of the new language that OPSI is requiring for all schools to have placed in
their handbook regarding Harassment, Intimidation and Bullying and Gender Inclusive policies and
procedures. This updated requirement did not allow any local control and is being dictated by OSPI,
therefore the following statement will be added to the handbook document: “The following language
is mandated by the Office of the Superintendent of Public Instruction (OSPI) in Washington State and
must be included in each handbook without modification by local school districts. Pomeroy School
District and its Board of Directors do not have local control over this section of the handbook. If you
have concerns about the content or the requirement to include it in the student handbook, please
contact OSPI:
Superintendent Chris Reykdal
Email: superintendent@k12.wa.us
Phone: (360) 742-7825
4. Enrollment seems to be increasing, as we have had a few elementary kiddos enroll in the past few
weeks, so hopefully numbers will continue to grow.
5. The recommendation to hire Alyssa Lehmitz for lead cook and supervisor, Sarah Povey, cafeteria
supervisor and kitchen support, and Jim Warren as bus driver were all approved. We are excited to
welcome them all to our team!
6. The fuel bid from 4-Star for the 2024-2025 was approved by the board of directors.
7. School Board Resolution #322-2024-2025 PSD budget was approved.
8. Approval of Resolution #323-Repayment of Capital Project Fund loan was also given by the board of
directors.
9. School Board Resolution #324-Loan for CPF was also approved.
10. Football coach Kyle Kimble submitted a request to approve the Atavus Tackling Certification program--the school board approved this request.
11. The next board meeting is scheduled for Tuesday, August 20th at 6:00 PM in the high school library.
12. Kelly McKeirnan gave a brief business manager’s report. He also shared the quote for an updated
sounds system at the football field and will move forward with this project. Tayn shared that he has
been getting familiar with different policies, contracts and handbooks and looks forward to meeting
staff soon. Rachel shared that 9 elementary staff attended an MTSS conference in Pullman, back to
school letters have been sent out, schedules for the elementary are being finalized and the elementary
office has completed its remodel.
13. There was no executive session, and the meeting was adjourned at 8:35 PM.
14. Any forms or handouts at the board meeting may be requested from the district office electronically or
in person. Call 843-1651 or email rgwinn@psd.wednet.edu
