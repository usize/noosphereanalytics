Strategic plan focus:

Each learner has access to support systems and partnerships that meet their
individual needs.

District improvement goal:

Meridian school district will support each school in developing multi-tiered
systems of support to respond to student needs in math, reading and on-track
to graduate status resulting in a 10% reduction in the discrepancy between the
achievement of all students and the achievement of students with disabilities
and multilingual learners.


The district will utilize a variety of assessment processes to:

1. Determine the effectiveness of the instructional programs;

2. Assess the progress of individual students in attaining student learning
goals or standards;

3. Identify the needs of individual students who are not progressing at their
expected rates; and

4. Identify students who are in need of specialized programs.
