School Counseling Consulting Agreement

This agreement is made on May 30, 2024 between the American School Counselor Association
(ASCA), 277 S Washington St., Suite 390, Alexandria, VA 22314, and Bremerton School District, 134
Marion Ave N, Bremerton, WA 98312.

It is agreed that ASCA will provide the services of ASCA certified trainer on six (6) agreed upon
training dates throughout the course of a two-year period to provide the following services:
Districtwide Training and Implementation of the ASCA National Model/RAMP (Recognized ASCA
Model Program) Program.

It is agreed that ASCA will be paid the total sum of $30,186 for this program. The Year 1 payment is
$15,093 and the Year 2 payment is $15,093. Training dates are to be determined and mutually
agreed upon between the district and trainer(s). If the school or district decides to cancel this
training after submitting a signed contract the district will be responsible for the reimbursement of
any non-refundable or non-transferable travel expenses that may have been already incurred.

All intellectual property rights in and to this training ("the Event"), the content and all materials
distributed at or in connection with the Event are owned by the American School Counselor
Association (“ASCA”). The district may not use or reproduce or allow anyone to use or reproduce,
any of ASCA’s trademarks, and/or any materials distributed at or in connection with the Event for
any reason without the prior written permission of ASCA and the presenting speaker. Further,
during the term of this agreement and for one year after, it is agreed that the district will not hire or
otherwise engage the services of the ASCA Certified trainer assigned on this project in any other
capacity without prior written consent of ASCA.

For Bremerton School District for American School Counselor Association
