WASHOUGAL SCHOOL DISTRICT 112
RESOLUTION NO. 2024-25-02:
Capital Projects Commitments

WHEREAS, the Washougal School District Board of Directors wishes to change the assigned
commitments in the Capital Projects Fund from Future Building Site Purchase Commitment to:

¢ Assigned to fund for needed replacement/upgrade projects $ 500,000
and

WHEREAS, the necessary funds are not impact fee revenue and are available in the Capital
Projects Fund currently assigned for future building purchase.

THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Board of Directors of the Washougal School
District No. 112 approves the change in assignment of $500,000 for the above purpose effective
as of October 22, 2024.

DATED this 22"4 day of October, 2024.
