Laurel Valley Unified Basketball Team

» Partnership with Nooksack Valley to field a unified basketball
team made up of students in our Lifeskills program with
students hoping to support them.

» “Meridian is proud to announce we have partnered with the
Nooksack Valley School District to provide an opportunity for
our students receiving SPED services to play sports! We are
now offering a Unified Basketball team that will play 6 regular
season games with a tournament at the end of the year. The
team's name will be ‘Laurel Valley.’ A nod to the location of both
Meridian and Nooksack. We are excited about this program
and the opportunity it provides to our students who would
regularly not participate in athletics.”

Our process for hiring the new principal for MHS:

Leadership Change

Friday, January 12: Meridian High School job announcement will be posted.

Sunday, February 11: Meridian High School job application will be closed.

Monday, February 12 - Feb. 16: Engage in the applicant screening.

Tuesday, February 20: Invite selected applicants to interview.

February 26 - March 1: Interview process conducted, reference checks, and job offered.

Our hiring process involves multiple interviews. The details of the interviews will be shared at a later
time. The interview team will consist of students, MHS staff, building principals, directors, and
district leadership. Every member of the interview team will have an opportunity to provide
feedback.

In late January, staff members, students, families, and the community will be asked to provide
feedback on the desired characteristics of the next Meridian High School principal. The feedback
received from this process will be provided to the interview team and referenced in the questions
and activities during the interview process.

We are dedicated to ensuring we find the right person for Meridian High School.
