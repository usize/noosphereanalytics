OPA:
At DVS our staff recently attended a virtual session on our Student Learning Plan management system. Staff at both DVS and OPA utilize School Data Solutions ALE platform as our Student Learning Plan software tool. This resource allows for seamless communication between families and ALE instructors. Additionally, the tools within the software are aligned to Alternative Learning Experience guidelines and expectations.
The OPA community recently celebrated a new school sign displayed on our campus. Many students, families, staff members and even a local business contributed to the success of this project. Way to go OPA!
