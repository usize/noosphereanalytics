TUKWILA SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 406
KING COUNTY, WASHINGTON

RESOLUTION NO. 952

A RESOLUTION of the Board of Directors of the Tukwila School District No. 406,
King County, Washington, approving
WHEREAS, WAC 392-123-140 allows school district to make interfund loans; and

WHEREAS, the Capital Projects Fund has sufficient funds to make such a loan without
detriment of any function or project for which the fund was established; and

WHEREAS, the Tukwila School District No. 406 has a need for a temporary loan to the
General Fund for the purpose of covering its negative ending fund balance;

NOW, THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED, that the Board of Directors of Tukwila School
District #406, King County, State of Washington make an Interfund Loan from the
Capital Projects Fund in the amount up to $250,000, effective as of the date of this
resolution. Said loan to be repaid by August 5, 2024 from the date of this resolution
with repayment to include interest based on the prevailing interest rate in King
County as of the date of repayment.

ADOPTED this 23rd day of July 2024 in Tukwila, Washington.

President Vice President
