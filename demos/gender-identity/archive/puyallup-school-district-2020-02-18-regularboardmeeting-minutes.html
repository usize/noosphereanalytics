Friday, June 11, 2021
Walker High School - 5:30 p.m.
Puyallup Digital Learning (PDL) - 7:30 p.m.
Saturday, June 12, 2021

Rogers High School - 9:00 a.m.
Emerald Ridge High School - 1:00 p.m.
Puyallup High School - 5:00 p.m.

Public Content
Presented by:
Mario Casello, Chief Operations Officer
Issue:
We are asking the board to consider approval of the Stormwater Agreement with the City of Puyallup for Sunrise Elementary
School, Resolution #119 2019-20.
Background:
This maintenance agreement is required by the City of Puyallup where stormwater improvements on district property flow into
the city system. The agreement refers to BMPs or best management practices.
Discussion:
This agreement addresses Sunrise Elementary School. The district will maintain the system to the standards outlined in the
attachment to the agreement. By carefully maintaining the on-site stormwater facilities, the district can avoid potential
enforcement issues outlined in the agreement.
The Stormwater Management and Best Management Practices Facilities Agreement (Exhibit 1) is attached. The legal description
(Exhibit A), Operations and Maintenance Manual (Exhibit B) and Stormwater Facilities Maintenance Logs (Exhibit C) are
attached that represent the exhibits for the agreement.
This agreement has been reviewed by our legal counsel. Operations staff recommends board acceptance of this proposed
agreement.
