APPROVED 8/28/24

BOARD OF DIRECTORS
Lopez Island School District #144
July 10, 2024
Library Media Center
4:00 pm
Board Work Session Minutes

A board work session is an open public meeting, and public attendance is welcome. There is no Zoom meeting access for board
work sessions. There is no opportunity for public comment during board work sessions since no action may be taken by the
board. The board may choose to invite other members of district staff, school staff, or outside experts to provide information.
Doors to the board meeting area will be locked before the meeting and opened five minutes prior to start time.

I. CALL TO ORDER: Chair called the meeting to order at 4:12 pm. Present at the meeting: Directors: Malia
Price-Morris, Sarah Murphy, and Bob O’Connell; Staff: Superintendent Smith, and J.Hammond. A quorum was
established.
II. PLEDGE OF ALLEGIANCE AND LAND ACKNOWLEDGEMENT: Director Price-Morris led the pledge,
and read the land acknowledgement.
III. BOARD ITEMS FOR DISCUSSION:
A. Policy & Procedure Review - Director Murphy discussed moving Policy drafts to shared drive.
1. Policy 3242 - Closed Campus - Sup Smith - rezoning school campus. Proposed language 1) first phrase in second paragraph - remove ‘basically’ and 2) definition does not include Life
Trail, K-8th students are expected to stay on campus,
Reading and approval will occur at the August board meeting.
Student parking potentially changing to bus lot.
2. Policy 4040 - Public Access to District Records - Awareness
a) Washington State Public Records Act (RCW’s Title 42, Chapter 42.56)
Washington State Public Records (RCW’s Title 430 Chapter 430-1)
b) Procedure 4040P - Procedure Public Access to District Records
3. Policy Series 6700 - Wellness - second reading at board meeting - for review and approval
4. Policy 1733 Board Compensation and Procedure - second reading at board meeting - for

Malia to confirm with Kirm intentions
a) Procedure 114P
