Regular Board Meeting
September 10, 2024

Responses to the Board’s questions/comments on Agenda Item 5.E — Public Records Transparency Report for the
September 10, 2024 Regular Board Meeting:

1. Agenda Item 5.E — Public Records Transparency Report per Policy 6030

1. Is it feasible to digitize the old paper records to make searching easier?
Aaron Draganov: We have discussed this option and estimate that it would take several hundred hours,
possibly more, to complete. While we don’t have a formal quote yet, we anticipate the cost would be
substantial, as we’ve heard this type of work tends to be expensive. Given the low volume of requests,
maintaining the paper records is not currently burdensome.

2. Is there a rough cost estimate for how much it costs the district to comply with all these requests?
Aaron Draganov: We estimate the 2023-24 costs to be as follows:

26 requests, averaging 2.5 hours of processing each, at a rate of $75 per hour = $4,875. Plus,
approximately $200 in materials and supplies. $5,075 total estimated costs for this work.
