Approved 12/20/23

BOARD OF DIRECTORS
Lopez Island School District #144
December 6, 2023
Library Media Center
4:30 pm
Board Work Session Agenda Minutes

A board work session is an open public meeting, and public attendance is welcome. There is no Zoom meeting access for board
work sessions. There is no opportunity for public comment during board work sessions since no action may be taken by the
board. The board may choose to invite other members of district staff, school staff, or outside experts to provide information.

I.

I. CALL TO ORDER AND FLAG SALUTE:
Directors present: Chair Carrier, Director Taylor, Director O’Connell, Director Price-Morris, Director
Murphy. Staff present: Interim Superintendent Dr. Martha Martin
Chair Carrier called the meeting to order at 4:30 pm. A quorum was present.

II.

II. LAND ACKNOWLEDGEMENT:
Chair Carrier gave the land acknowledgement.

III.

III. BOARD ITEMS FOR DISCUSSION (No action will be taken):
A. Facility & Safety (Camera policy)
B. Superintendent Search/Contract discussion
C. Financial Committee and Issue Management Deck

The board discussed items A - C.
IV.

IV. ADJOURNMENT:
Chair Carrier adjourned the work session at 6:43 pm.
