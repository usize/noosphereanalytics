BREMERTON SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 100-C
KITSAP COUNTY, WASHINGTON

UNLIMITED TAX GENERAL OBLIGATION BONDS, 2024

RESOLUTION NO. 2023/2024-18

A RESOLUTION of the Board of Directors of Bremerton School District
No. 100-C, Kitsap County, Washington, authorizing the issuance and
sale of unlimited tax general obligation bonds of the District in the
ageregate principal amount of not to exceed $150,000,000 to provide
funds to acquire, construct, equip, modernize and make other capital
improvements to the facilities of the District, as authorized by
Resolution No. 2023/2024-09 of the Board, and approved by the
qualified electors of the District at a special election held therein on
February 13, 2024; providing for annual tax levies to pay the principal
of and interest on the bonds; and delegating to the Superintendent and/or
the Assistant Superintendent of Finance, Operations and Human
Resources of the District the authority to determine the structure of the
bonds, approve the interest rates, maturity dates and principal maturities
for the bonds under the terms and conditions set forth herein and to
apply for participation in the State of Washington guaranty program.

ADOPTED: April 11, 2024

K&L GATES LLP
Seattle, Washington

RESOLUTION NO. 2023/2024-18

A RESOLUTION of the Board of Directors of Bremerton School District
No. 100-C, Kitsap County, Washington, authorizing the issuance and
sale of unlimited tax general obligation bonds of the District in the
ageregate principal amount of not to exceed $150,000,000 to provide
funds to acquire, construct, equip, modernize and make other capital
improvements to the facilities of the District, as authorized by
Resolution No. 2023/2024-09 of the Board, and approved by the
qualified electors of the District at a special election held therein on
February 13, 2024; providing for annual tax levies to pay the principal
of and interest on the bonds; and delegating to the Superintendent and/or
the Assistant Superintendent of Finance, Operations and Human
Resources of the District the authority to determine the structure of the
bonds, approve the interest rates, maturity dates and principal maturities
for the bonds under the terms and conditions set forth herein and to
apply for participation in the State of Washington guaranty program.

WHEREAS, at an election held in Bremerton School District No. 100-C, Kitsap County,
Washington (the “District”), on February 13, 2024, the number and proportion of the qualified
electors of the District required by law for the adoption thereof voted in favor of a proposition
authorizing the issuance of unlimited tax general obligation bonds of the District in the aggregate
principal amount of not to exceed $150,000,000 (the “Bond Authorization”) for the purpose of
providing funds to enable the District to acquire, construct, equip, modernize and make other
capital improvements to its facilities (the “Improvements”) as authorized by Resolution
No. 2023/2024-09 (the “Election Resolution”) of the Board of Directors (the “Board”), adopted
on December 4, 2023; and

WHEREAS, it is deemed necessary and advisable that the District now issue and sell a first
series of the Bond Authorization in the principal amount of not to exceed $150,000,000, to provide
part of the funds necessary to pay for a portion of the Improvements (the “Bonds”); and

WHEREAS, the District expects to receive an offer from the Underwriter (as defined

herein) and desires to issue and sell the Bonds to the Underwriter as set forth herein; and

Section 2. Plan of Finance, Authorization and Description of Bonds. The District shall
now issue and sell in the aggregate principal amount of not to exceed $150,000,000 (the “Bonds”),
as the first series of unlimited tax general obligation bonds authorized by the qualified electors of
the District held at a special election on February 13, 2024, for the purpose of providing funds to
pay the cost of constructing, equipping, modernizing and making other capital improvements to
the facilities of the District as authorized by the Election Resolution. The Bonds shall be
designated the “Bremerton School District No. 100-C, Kitsap County, Washington, Unlimited Tax
General Obligation Bonds, 2024”, shall be dated as of the date of original issuance and delivery,
shall be fully registered as to both principal and interest, shall be in the denomination of $5,000
each or any integral multiple thereof, provided that no Bond shall represent more than one
maturity, shall be numbered separately in such manner and with any additional designation as the
Bond Registrar deems necessary for purposes of identification and control, and shall bear interest
payable semiannually on the first days of June and December, commencing on First Interest
Payment Date at rates set forth in the Purchase Contract; and shall mature in the years and in the
principal amounts set forth in the Purchase Contract and as approved by the Designated
Representative pursuant to Section 8.
