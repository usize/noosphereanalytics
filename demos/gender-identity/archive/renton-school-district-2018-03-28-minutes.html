  6. CALL FOR CORRESPONDENCE & DELEGATIONS FROM THE FLOOR

  8. NEW BUSINESS
  8.01 Safety and Security Update
  Jamie Sidell, Director for Safety and Security provided an update on the Safety and Security Department. Sidell stated that the department is fully staffed and is now providing after hour’s supervision. A district-wide assessment is being done on the security cameras, emergency response procedures have been put into a poster format to replace the old flip chart, emergency snacks and water are now available at each school site and the department has implemented a new incident reporting system called “Report Exec”. Sidell will continue to review and improve district safety by providing ongoing training to District staff.
