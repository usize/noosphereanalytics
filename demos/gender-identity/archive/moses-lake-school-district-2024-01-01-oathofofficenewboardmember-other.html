MOSES LAKE SCHOOL DISTRICT #16
BOARD OF DIRECTORS
OATH OF OFFICE

STATE OF WASHINGTON, COUNTY OF GRANT

I, do solemnly swear (or affirm) that I will
Support the Constitution of the United States and the Constitution and
laws of the State of Washington, and that I will faithfully and
impartially perform and discharge the duties of the office of School
Director of Moses Lake School District No. 161, according to the law,
and to the best of my ability.

Signature

Subscribed and sworn to before me this day of____———C—is
2024.

, Interim Superintendent,
Moses Lake School District
