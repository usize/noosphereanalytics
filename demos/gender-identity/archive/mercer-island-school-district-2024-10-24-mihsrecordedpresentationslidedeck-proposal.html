Bond Priority Projects
Critical take-aways

The proposed bond projects create meaningful impact through targeted student, educator, and
community-focused improvements.

The programmatic updates are designed to enhance safety, access, academic opportunities,
and the high school experience for students inside and outside of the traditional classroom.

By optimizing the existing infrastructure, strategic renovations can uphold the legacy of
excellence and align our learning spaces with the high academic standards that we hold
ourselves to.

Through responsible investment we can competitively equip our students with the facilities and
resources they need for health and success.
