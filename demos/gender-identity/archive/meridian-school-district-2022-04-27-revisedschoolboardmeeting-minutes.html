MERIDIAN SCHOOL DISTRICT #505
MINUTES OF EXECUTIVE SESSION AND REGULAR SCHOOL BOARD MEETING
MERIDIAN SCHOOL DISTRICT
MEETING HELD VIA ZOOM
April 27th, 2022
Revised February 8, 2023
EXECUTIVE SESSION
The Board moved into executive session at 6:15 PM. The Executive Session was to review the performance of a
public employee. The Executive session was expected to last forty minutes and the executive session was adjourned
at 6:55 PM.
The regular meeting was called to order by Board Chair, Craig Mitchell, at 7:00 p.m. Chair Mitchell welcomed those
in attendance.
Board members present at the meeting were Craig Mitchell; Sara Bernardy; Jarrod Karuza; Marty Gray; Steve
Corwin; Marty Gray.
The Pledge of Allegiance was recited.
Sara Bernardy made a motion to adopt the agenda. Jarrod Karuza seconded the motion; the motion passed 5-0.
CONSENT AGENDA
A. Approval of Meeting Minutes
B. Accounts Payable
C. Personnel Report
D. Student Travel
E. Superintendent, “Contract Extension”
A motion to adopt the consent agenda was made by Jarrod Karuza and seconded by Sara Bernardy. The motion
passed 5-0.
PUBLIC COMMENT
Bruce Baumann – He voiced the need to continue to push students to go into trades as much as we are pushing
students to go to college. He stated that we are short of a lot of employees right now – lot of people out there
looking for employees.

Harriet Baumann – Came to talk about communications. She expressed it’s been two years since she brought
up communications issues and is still not receiving some emails. She also voiced concerns about voices being
heard by the board through surveys.
NEW BUSINESS
2021-22 School Waiver
Superintendent, Dr. James Everett presented the 2021-22 School Waiver asking for Board Approval. A motion to
approve the application for the 2021-22 School Waiver was made by Jarrod Karuza and seconded by Steve Corwin.
The motion passed 5-0.

Revised and re-signed at Feb. 8, 2023 Board meeting to correct typographical errors that were
previously made in original minutes

OLD BUSINESS
2021-2022 Board Yearly Plan of Work Update Draft
Superintendent, Dr. James Everett presented a second reading of the 2021-2022 Board Yearly Plan of Work Update
Draft. A motion to approve the 2021-22 Board Yearly Plan of Work Draft was made by Marty Gray and seconded
by Sara Bernardy. The motion passed 5-0.
Second Reading: Policy and Procedure 3211
Assistant Superintendent, Kurt Harvill presented a second reading of Policy & Procedure 3211—Gender Inclusive
Schools. A motion to approve Policy and Procedure 3211 was made by Sara Bernardy and seconded by Marty Gray.
The motion passed 3-2.
Second Reading: Policy and Procedure 3416
Assistant Superintendent, Kurt Harvill presented a first second reading of Policy and Procedure 3416—Medication
at School. A motion to approve Policy and Procedure 3416 was made by Steve Corwin and seconded by Sara
Bernardy. The motion passed 5-0.
Second Reading: Policy and Procedure 3424
Assistant Superintendent, Kurt Harvill presented a first second reading of Policy and Procedure 3424— OpioidRelated Overdose Reversal. A motion to approve Policy and Procedure 3424 was made by Sara Bernardy and
seconded by Steve Corwin. The motion passed 5-0.
Second Reading: Policy and Procedure 4200
Assistant Superintendent, Kurt Harvill presented a first second reading of Policy and Procedure 4200— Parent
Access and Safe and Orderly Learning Environment. A motion to approve Policy and Procedure 4200 was made by
Sara Bernardy and seconded by Jarrod Karuza. The motion passed 5-0.
FINANCIAL UPDATE
Director of Business and Finance, Daniel Yorton, presented a financial update to the Board.
PRESENTATIONS

Mike Baranick, a representative from Hargis presented to the Board on the Clean Building Standards for the state
of Washington.

SUPERINTENDENT’S REPORT
Superintendent, Dr. James Everett presented a COVID 19 update, a HR update, WA DOT, and an update on the
Northwest Career and Technical Academy.
INFORMATION FROM BOARD MEMBERS
The next Regular Board Meeting is scheduled for Wednesday, May 11 th at 7:00 p.m.
BOARD SIGNATURE OF OFFICIAL DOCUMENTS
ADJOURNMENT
There being no further business, the Regular meeting was adjourned at 8:40 p.m. by Board Chair, Craig Mitchell.
