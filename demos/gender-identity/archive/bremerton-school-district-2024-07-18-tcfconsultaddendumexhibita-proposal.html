Dear Garth,

Congratulations on passing your bond in February! It is exciting to see how all the hard work and community
engagement paid off. | am pleased to provide you with this proposal for predesign services to assist you in
getting the bond projects underway.

PROJECT UNDERSTANDING

Following the passage of your bond in February, we understand you have engaged with OAC Services to
assist you in managing the projects and procuring the services of design and construction teams. While
that process moves forward, OAC reached out to us to participate in some limited predesign services
including additional programming, development of district design and construction standards, and
completing the Study & Survey for the OSPI D-Form process. This work will begin immediately and run
through the summer to support your schedule.

We understand that the projects included in the bond are Armin Jahr Elementary, View Ridge Elementary,
Consolidated Facilities and district-wide HVAC upgrades.

PREDESIGN SCOPE OF SERVICES
The following tasks are included in this proposal. See attached Scope and Fee Matrix for more detail.

1. Programming: During the prebond phase, we met with BSD representatives to develop initial
programming documents. This scope includes meeting again with your representatives to
further vet the programming documents and edit and expand as needed. The deliverable will
be a room & area summary with some commentary about key characteristics of various spaces.
This does not assume a full educational specification.

2. District Standards: The team will host a workshop with your representatives to develop
detailed design and construction standards. The workshop will include architectural/structural,
civil/landscape, mechanical/electrical and commissioning categories. The deliverable will be a
narrative broken down by specification section, outlining the key criteria, basis of design
preferences, and other pertinent data for each space and product type.

3. Study & Survey: Complete the OSPI Study & Survey Workbook and upload to ICOS. This will
benefit from the work done during the Long Range Facility Planning process. This study includes
all SCAP-recognized facilities.

SCOPE OF SERVICES

Our scope of services and resulting fees are based on providing seismic evaluations for the
ten school campuses listed above. The evaluations will consist of a site visit to review the
existing facility to identify structural concerns as well as to identify the structural systems.
Our site reviews will be limited to areas exposed to view as selective demolition is not a part
of our scope of service.

Our deliverable will be a summary report for each school, which will identify the date(s) of
construction, general structural system, and general structural concerns. We will also work
with your team to fill out the |COS/EPAT database information for each school.
