    12. REPORTS-7:45 pm
  Information: 12.1 Career and Technical Education (CTE) Plan of Work for 2023-2024
  Mark Madison, Director of STEM & Career/ College Readiness, presented the CTE Program and Plan of Work for the 2023-24 school year. He highlighted some achievements from the 2022-23 school year such as the On the Job training program and the growing number of students who received an industry recognized certification. He also shared that CTE is creating a student advisory committee this year. Over the next four years, the CTE Department plans to work on increasing the number of students participating in work based learning.
  Information: 12.2 Panorama Spring 2022-23 Data
  Lisa Gonzalez, Executive Director of Student Learning, presented regarding the Panorama Spring 22-23 Data. She shared how the Panorama survey works and how the questions are tailored to goal one of our strategic plan. She then shared student response data about students feeling a sense of belonging and having positive teacher-student relationships.
