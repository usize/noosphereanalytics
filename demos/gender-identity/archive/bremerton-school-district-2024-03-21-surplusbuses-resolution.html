RESOLUTION 2023/2024-15
Surplus School Buses

WHEREAS, the Bremerton School District 100-C, Kitsap County, Washington, is the
owner of one 2006 Thomas 22-passenger Bus (VIN 1T88J4E2461270920), known as Bus 36;
and

WHEREAS, said bus is not now being used for school purposes and will not be used for
school purposes in the foreseeable future;

NOW THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED that the Board of Directors of the Bremerton
School District No. 100-C, Kitsap County, Washington, hereby determines that the surplus bus,
more particularly described above, is no longer required for school purposes.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Board of Directors of Bremerton School
District No. 100-C, Kitsap County, Washington, hereby declares that the 2006 22-passenger
Thomas Bus, #36, is surplus and should be sold.

APPROVED by the Board of Directors of Bremerton School District No. 100-C, Kitsap
County, Washington, in a meeting thereof held on the 21st of March, 2024.

Bremerton School District No. 100-C
Kitsap County, Washington

Alyson Roftet, President, Board of Directors
