MERCER ISLAND SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 400
KING COUNTY, WASHINGTON

DECLARATION OF SURPLUS PROPERTY

RESOLUTION NO. 735

A RESOLUTION of the Board of Directors of Mercer Island School District No. 400, King County,
Washington, declaring personal property surplus and authorizing its sale or disposal.

WHEREAS, RCW 28A.335.090 gives the Board of Directors of the Mercer Island School District No. 400 the
exclusive power to sell or otherwise dispose of school property; and

WHEREAS, the District maintains a school property inventory and it has been determined that the following
listed items are no longer needed for school purposes and are considered surplus personal property; and

1. Miscellaneous technology-related items
2. Miscellaneous vehicles and buses

WHEREAS, any school district or educational service district, after complying with the requirements of RCW
28A.335.180, may dispose of surplus educational equipment; and

WHEREAS, the Board of Directors wishes to sell or dispose of the personal property described above.

NOW, THEREFORE, THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS OF MERCER ISLAND SCHOOL
DISTRICT NO. 400 HEREBY RESOLVES AS FOLLOWS:

1. The personal property described above is surplus property.

2. The District’s Superintendent is directed to follow the notice and other requirements contained in RCW
28A.335.180 applicable to the sale or disposal of the surplus property described above.

3. After complying with the requirements of RCW 28A.335.180, the District's Superintendent is directed
to auction and/or solicit bids for the purchase and/or proper removal of identified surplus property. Bids
shall be awarded to the persons who submit the highest reasonable bid.

ADOPTED by the Board of Directors of Mercer Island School District No. 400, King County, Washington, at a
regular meeting thereof, held this 24th day of October 2024.
