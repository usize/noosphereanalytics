The Sedro-Woolley School District Board of Directors met for a regular meeting at 6:00 p.m.

Superintendent's Report:

Superintendent Mickelson recognized the Sedro-Woolley Lions Club Tribe with a certificate of
appreciation for being a steadfast partner, and for demonstrating that believing in schools and
ensuring that every child has the tools, resources, and support they need to succeed. This
partnership amplifies the importance of public education as a foundation for a thriving
community.

School Board Representative, Adriana Rodriguez, provided the donation and activity reports.

Executive Director Daniel Lee provided the December Enrollment Report.

Old Business

Danielle Russell moved and Lindy Mullen-Doyle seconded to approve the
recommendation from Dr. Mickelson to maintain the current 8-period bell schedule at
Sedro-Woolley High School. Motion carried 3/0.

Lindy Mullen-Doyle moved and Danielle Russell seconded to amend the draft Policy #3205 -- Sex
Discrimination and Sex-Based Harassment of Students Prohibited, as presented by Executive
Director Daniel Lee. The amendment is to strike “currently Central Elementary”. Motion carried
3/0. Lindy Mullen-Doyle moved and Danielle Russell seconded to approve Policy #3205 — Sex
Discrimination and Sex-Based Harassment of Students Prohibited as amended. Motion carried 3/0.

Lindy Mullen-Doyle moved and Danielle Russell seconded to amend the draft Policy #3206 —
Pregnant and Parent Students, as presented by Executive Director Daniel Lee. The amendment is
to strike “currently Central Elementary”. Motion carried 3/0. Lindy Mullen-Doyle moved and
Danielle Russell seconded to approve Policy #3206 — Pregnant and Parent Students as amended.
Motion carried 3/0.

Lindy Mullen-Doyle moved and Danielle Russell seconded to amend the draft Policy #5011 —Sex
Discrimination and Sex-Based Harassment of Staff Prohibited, as presented by Executive Director
Daniel Lee. The amendment is to strike “currently Central Elementary”. Motion carried 3/0. Lindy


Mullen-Doyle moved and Danielle Russell seconded to approve Policy #5011 — Sex
Discrimination and Sex-Based Harassment of Staff Prohibited as amended. Motion carried 3/0.

Lindy Mullen-Doyle moved and Danielle Russell seconded to amend the draft Policy #5012 —
Parental Family or Marital Status and Pregnancy or Related Conditions of Staff, as presented by
Executive Director Daniel Lee. The amendment is to strike “currently Central Elementary”.
Motion carried 3/0. Lindy Mullen-Doyle moved and Danielle Russell seconded to approve Policy
#5012 — Parental Family or Marital Status and Pregnancy or Related Conditions of Staff as
amended. Motion carried 3/0.

Lindy Mullen-Doyle moved and Danielle Russell seconded to approve Policy #1240 — Committees
— Dr. Mickelson presented for first reading Policy #1240 — Committees, at the November 25, 2024,
board meeting. This is a new policy from WSSDA that supports the creating of committees of the
board of directors. Motion carried 3/0.
