ADDENDUM TO AGREEMENT FOR CONSULTING SERVICES

THIS ADDENDUM is made and entered into by and between BREMERTON SCHOOL
DISTRICT NO. 100-C, a Washington quasi-municipal corporation (“District”), and TCF
ARCHITECTURE, a Washington corporation (“Consultant”).

RECITALS:

WHEREAS, the District and the Consultant entered into an Agreement for Consulting
Services (the “Agreement”) for certain technical, engineering, architectural, professional, and
other services, and such Agreement expired on June 30, 2023;

WHEREAS, the District desires to continue receiving said services from Consultant, and
Consultant desires to continue providing said services for the District;

WHEREAS, the parties desire to extend the Agreement, subject to the modifications
described below.

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

1. Section 4 of the Agreement, entitled “Time of Performance,” is stricken in its entirety and
replaced with the following:

The timing for performing the Services is of the essence. The Services shall
be undertaken so as to assure their expeditious completion in the light of the
purpose of this Agreement. The Services shall be completed by, and the
Agreement shall remain in effect until December 31, 2024, unless cancelled
prior to that date pursuant to this Agreement

2; Section 5.a, entitled “Fee,” is stricken in its entirety and replaced with the following:
The School District agrees to pay the Consultant the following Fee:

Compensation for Services of the Consultant shall be on an hourly
basis at the rates listed in Exhibit A. The Compensation for Services,
including any subconsultants, shall not exceed $134,139.00, without
the prior written approval of the School District.

3. Section 5.b, entitled “Reimbursable Expenses,” is stricken in its entirety and replaced with
the following:

In addition to the Fee, the School District agrees to pay the Consultant a
multiple of 1.1 times the following Reimbursable Expenses to the extent
paid by the Consultant in the interest of the Project and shall not exceed
$1,000.00:

Expenses in connection with pre-authorized travel, mileage, and ferry costs;
