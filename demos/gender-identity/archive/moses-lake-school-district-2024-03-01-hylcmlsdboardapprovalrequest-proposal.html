WASHINGTON STATE UNIVERSITY

Ggyreanms RI-CITIES GEARUD

MLHS GEAR UP would like the MLSD school board’s approval to attend the Hispanic Youth
Leadership Conference at the University of Idaho in Moscow, ID. The agenda and supervisors are
listed below. Once approved we will begin recruiting and send a final student roster.

GEAR UP is funding the registration fees, transportation, and any food not provided by the
conference. We will travel with GEAR UP participants from area high schools including Vanguard,
Warden, and Othello.

This leadership opportunity for ten 10th and 11th graders will help them develop skills, network with
students from other schools and states, and engage in team building activities and workshops.
Students will be given strategies for engaging their peers in college and career interests, and
ultimately lead to helping students at MLHS identify resources and opportunities to help achieve their
post-secondary goals. This event offers a college fair, scholarship interviews, workshops, and on-site
admission opportunities.

Participants will be selected by teacher and staff referrals. Selection criteria included: grades,
attendance, behavior, and overall potential to be student leaders. We are very excited to offer this
opportunity for growth to our Moses Lake High School students.

Supervisors:

Lacy McFadden, GEAR UP Site Manager
Jen Ruiz, Program Coordinator
Rosa Valdivia, Student Achievement Specialist

And several other GEAR UP Staff members and managers from throughout the area.
If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact me.

Lacy McFadden
