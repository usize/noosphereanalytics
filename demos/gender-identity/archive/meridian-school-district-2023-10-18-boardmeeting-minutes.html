MERIDIAN SCHOOL DISTRICT #505
REGULAR SCHOOL BOARD MEETING
MEETING HELD IN PERSON & VIA ZOOM
October 18, 2023
REGULAR MEETING
The regular meeting was called to order by Board Vice Chair Craig Mitchell at 6:00 p.m.
The pledge of allegiance was recited.
Board Vice Chair Craig Mitchell welcomed everyone that was in attendance.
Board members present at the meeting were Craig Mitchell, Marty Gray, Steve Corwin, and Jarrod Karuza.
Board Chair Sara Bernardy was excused from attending.
Student Board Representative Kennedy Brzozowski was present at the meeting. Morgan Adams was
excused from attending.
A motion to adopt the agenda was made by Steve Corwin and seconded by Marty Gray. The motion
passed 4-0.
CONSENT AGENDA
A. Approval of Meeting Minutes
B. Accounts Payable
C. Personnel Report
D. Student Travel
E. Adult Meal Pricing
F. PT Interlocal Agreement
A motion to adopt the consent agenda was made by Jarrod Karuza and seconded by Steve Corwin. The
motion passed 4-0.
REPORTS and PRESENTATIONS
School Data Review
Director of Teaching and Learning, Adrienne Somera presented a fall data review to the Board. Adrienne
shared that this is a beginning snapshot of data, with more details to be shared at a future meeting after
additional information is loaded into our Washington State report card. Adrienne shared assessment and
SBA data and reviewed and the districts improvement goals. Adrienne shared how the improvement goals
have been directly aligned with the 2023-24 strategic plan commitment focus areas.
PUBLIC COMMENT
There was no public comment.
NEW BUSINESS

First Reading: Policy and Procedure 3207 – Prohibition of Harassment, Intimidation, and
Bullying of Students

Assistant Superintendent Kurt Harvill presented Policy and Procedure 3207 – Prohibition of Harassment,
Intimidation, and Bullying of Students to the Board for a first review. This will be brought back before the
Board for second review and approval at the November 1, 2023, Regular Board meeting.

First Reading: Policy 3210 - Nondiscrimination and Equity

Assistant Superintendent Kurt Harvill presented Policy 3210 - Nondiscrimination and Equity to the Board
for a first review. This will be brought back before the Board for second review and approval at the
November 1, 2023, Regular Board meeting.
First Reading: Policy and Procedure 6240 – Meals with Meetings

Assistant Superintendent Kurt Harvill presented Policy and Procedure 6240 – Meals with Meetings to the
Board for a first review. This will be brought back before the Board for second review and approval at the
November 1, 2023, Regular Board meeting.
First Reading: Procedure 3421F – Child Abuse, Neglect and Exploitation Prevention

Assistant Superintendent Kurt Harvill presented Procedure 3421F – Child Abuse, Neglect and Exploitation
Prevention to the Board for a first review. This will be brought back before the Board for second review
and approval at the November 1, 2023, Regular Board meeting.
SUPERINTENDENT’S REPORT
In the absence of Dr. Everett, Assistant Superintendent Kurt Harvill reported to the Board on district
activities. Kurt started his report by sharing information about the safety work that is being done
throughout our district. Kurt also spoke about the work he is engaged in around collective bargaining
agreements and Educational Grant Management.
INFORMATION FROM STUDENT BOARD REPRESENTATIVES
Student Representative Kennedy shared that she and Morgan have been working on ways to hear more
student voice.
INFORMATION FROM BOARD MEMBERS
There was no information shared from board members.
BOARD SIGNATURE OF OFFICIAL DOCUMENTS
ADJOURNMENT
There being no further business, the Board meeting was adjourned at 7:16 p.m. by Board Vice Chair
Craig Mitchell.
