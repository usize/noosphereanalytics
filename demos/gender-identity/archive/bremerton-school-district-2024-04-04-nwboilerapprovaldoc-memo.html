TO: Bremerton School District Board of Directors
THROUGH: Dr. James Crawford, Superintendent
FROM: Dr. Garth Steedman, Assistant Superintendent of Finance, Operations, & Human Resources

SUBJECT: Recommending the approval of the NW Boiler, Inc proposal for the Crownhill boiler
replacement project.

DATE: April 04, 2024

ACTION:

Recommending that the Bremerton School District (BSD) Board of Directors approve the NW Boiler, Inc
proposal for the Crownhill boiler replacement project in the amount of $79,653.00 plus Washington
State sales tax.

BACKGROUND:
Two of the three water heaters at Crownhill Elementary have failed and the third one is at the end of its
life. New boilers have been chosen for replacement to increase efficiency.

BENEFIT TO THE DISTRICT:
Approval of this project will allow for upgrades which will increase system efficiency and longevity.

COST TO THE DISTRICT:

Total estimated costs of the Crownhill boiler project are $79,653.00 plus Washington State sales tax. The
price includes shipping, removal and disposal of three existing water heaters and the installation of two
new boilers.
