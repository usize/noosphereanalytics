DIRECTOR, SAMANTHA BURGESS »° ASSISTANT DIRECTOR, LINDSAY SCOTT

September 6, 2024
Dear Board of Directors,

The Summit Program at Moses Lake High School is receiving a donation of $2,520.00 from The Women's
Division of the Moses Lake Golf Club. This amount will be used for supplies in the 2024-2025 school year
to support the students attending the program. The Summit (18-22 program) works with students who
have an IEP (Individualized Education Program) to help them transition from high school to their
post-secondary goals. The funds will be used to purchase supplies that support teaching independent
living and workplace skills. This includes items for personal care and hygiene, home management,
cooking and nutrition, transportation, and time management. We are grateful for the continued support
of this program and its students.

Sammi Burgess
Director of Special Services
