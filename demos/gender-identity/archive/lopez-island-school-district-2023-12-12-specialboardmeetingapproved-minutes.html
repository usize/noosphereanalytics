Approved 12/20/23
BOARD OF DIRECTORS
Lopez Island School District #144
December 12, 2023
11:45 am
Library Media Center
Special Board Meeting Minutes

I.

CALL TO ORDER AND FLAG SALUTE:
Directors Present: Chair Carrier, Director O’Connell, Director Taylor, Director Price-Morris, Director
Murphy.
Chair Carrier called the meeting to order at 11:45 am and gave the flag salute. A quorum was present.

II.

ADJUSTMENTS TO THE AGENDA:
No adjustments.

III.

EXECUTIVE SESSION (11:45 am - 1:15 pm):
A. To conduct a final Superintendent interview with Candidate A (To evaluate the qualifications of
an applicant for public employment)
The board entered Executive Session at 11:55 am and expects to reconvene in open session at
1:15.
The Board reconvened in open session at 1:20 pm.

IV.

RECESS:
The board recessed at 1:20 pm.
Chair Carrier reconvened the meeting at 1:53 pm.

V.

EXECUTIVE SESSION (1:45 pm - 3:15 pm):
A. To conduct a final Superintendent interview with Candidate B (To evaluate the qualifications of
an applicant for public employment)
The board entered executive session at 1:54 pm and expects to reconvene in open session at 3:15
pm.
The board reconvened at 3:00 pm.
RECESS:
Chair Carrier called a recess at 3:00 pm.
The board reconvened the meeting at 5:40 pm.

VI.

VII.

EXECUTIVE SESSION (5:30 PM):
A. To evaluate the qualifications of an applicant for public employment
B. Estimated Time: 2 hours
The board entered executive session at 5:40 pm and expects to reconvene in open session at 7:30
pm. The board extended the executive session to 9:00 pm.
Chair Carrier called a recess with a plan to reconvene at 11:00 am, Wednesday, December 13th.
The board reconvened at 11:00 am, 12/12/23. The board reentered executive session and expects to
reconvene in open session at 12:00 pm.
The board returned to open session at 12:05 pm.

Approved 12/20/23
Director Taylor moved to offer the position of superintendent to Brady Smith. Director O’Connell
seconded the motion. Chair Carrier led a roll call vote: KT-Yes, RO-Yes, MP-M- Yes, SM-Yes. KC-Yes.
The board passed the motion unanimously. (5/5)
VIII.

ADJOURNMENT:
Chair Carrier adjourned the meeting at 12:06 pm.
