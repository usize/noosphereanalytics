SCHOOL DISTRICT

2023-24 End of Year Projection
As of July 17, 2024

Amy Fleming, CFO


Fund balance projection has been updated as of July 17, 2024 and
shows an improved ending fund balance projection of ($3.1M)
compared to the previous month projection ($3.5).

¢ Projected revenue of $832,186 has been added to revenue line
items 3100, 3121 and 4121 based on average annual enrollment
increases through June 2024.

¢ Projected revenue for Property tax earnings— Other Revenues 1100
Taxes collected was decreased from $8,186,543 to $8,057,143
based on actual revenue received and historical collection amounts
through the end of the fiscal year.

¢ Projected revenue for 4158-07 BEST Grant was reduced by $60,790
and partially reallocated to new 4158 grant awards: Language
Access Coordinator, Digital Equity and Inclusion, Computer Science,
CTE Aerospace.


Federal Revenue 6164 LEP and 6152 Fed Title IV 523X were
reduced $150,000 to reflect updated projected revenue.

Expenditures remain consistent and will continue to be
reviewed for the remainder of the year.

Estimated ending fund balance is ($3.1) million. The range
of negative ending fund balance at August 31 has improved
from previous months and will most likely be between
negative ($2.8 to $3.5) million.
