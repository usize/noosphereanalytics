She proceeded to provide highlights about Mr. Watrin's accomplishments in earning the award. Ms. Springs LeCain presented Mr. Watrin with a plaque and showed a video honoring him.

Denny Waters announced that Ricardo Martin Del Campo's term ends June 30, 2023, and proceeded to present him with a service plaque. The Board thanked Mr. Martin Del Campo for his service.

  D. Finance Report
Chief Financial Officer Michelle Scott reported the 2021-22 audit has come to an end. She explained that the audit was divided into two parts, financial statements and federal programs. She reported that the district received a clean audit on the financial statements. She told of all the programs that fall under federal programs, and the new Emergency Connectivity Fund grant for 1,900 Chromebooks. The auditors determined that there was a documentation issue with the ECF grant and issued an audit finding. Ms. Scott announced that rest assured there is no loss of public funds. All Chromebooks are accounted for and they are in the district’s inventory system and available for student learning. In addition, Ms. Scott shared that there was a large number of school districts statewide that received the same audit finding.
