APPROVED 9/27/23

BOARD OF DIRECTORS
Lopez Island School District #144
Sept 13, 2023
Library Media Center
4:00 pm
Board Work Session Minutes

A board work session is an open public meeting, and public attendance is welcome. There is no Zoom meeting access for board
work sessions. There is no opportunity for public comment during board work sessions since no action may be taken by the
board. The board may choose to invite other members of district staff, school staff, or outside experts to provide information.

I. CALL TO ORDER AND FLAG SALUTE:
Directors present: Chair Carrier, Vice-Chair Guenther, K.Taylor, R.O’Connell, M.Price-Morris
Staff present: M.Martin
Chair Carrier called the work session to order at 5:00 pm.
II. FLAG SALUTE & LAND ACKNOWLEDGEMENT:
Vice-Chair Guenther led the flag salute. Director Price-Morris gave the land acknowledgement.
III. BOARD ITEMS FOR DISCUSSION (No action will be taken):
A. School Board Meeting Calendar
B. Superintendent Search
C. Town Hall
D. LISD Advocacy Group
Discussion notes: Chair Carrier led discussion on items A-D.
IV. ADJOURNMENT:
Chair Carrier adjourned the work session at 6:39 pm.
