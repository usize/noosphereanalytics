July 2025

To the Moses Lake School District Board of Directors:

L, David Holloway, current Moses Lake High School Choir Director am submitting this letter to gain approval
for a performance opportunity involving the Moses Lake High School Chamber Choir in New York from April

2-7, 2025.
The MLHS Chamber Choir has been invited to participate in “Nationals for top choirs” via “Choirs of America”
in New York. The performance activities of this trip will include:
e Adjudicated performance
Full one-hour clinic
Observational opportunities
xChange Workshop
Pen 2 Podium Workshop
Mass choir performance with choirs from across the country
performance of a world premiere piece under the direction of world renowned composer and director

Andre Thomas

The further/educational sight-seeing activities of this trip will include some of the following:
Ellis Island/Statue of Liberty

911 museum

American Museum of Natural History

Edge

Top of the Rock Observation Deck

Attending a Broadway Musical performance

Individual choir adjudicated performance will be held at Queens College Aaron Copland School of Music and
the Mass Choir and World Premiere Performance will be held at the world renowned Carnegie Hall.

The total cost of this trip is estimated at $1,909 per student, and $1,967 per chaperone.
All costs associated with this trip will be paid through the Choir ASB and Choir Booster club.

Chaperones will be at a ratio of 3:1
Thank you for your consideration!

Respectfully Submitted,

David Holloway

Choral Director
Moses Lake High School
