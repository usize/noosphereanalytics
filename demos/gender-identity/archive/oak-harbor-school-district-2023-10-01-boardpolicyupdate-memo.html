6220/6220P Bid or Request for Proposal Requirements: Changes in this policy center on changes to
the Interlocal Cooperation Act and require the school district to look at the following criteria when the
district enters into a cooperative purchasing agreement with other governmental agencies. To enter
into such agreements the district must ensure:

1. The agency has its own procurement process relating to the contract

2. The contract is available to other entities

3. The agencies specifications and scope of work need to be essentially the same as the districts

4. Public works projects contain essentially the same specifications and scope of work as the

district

5. Ensure the cost is reasonable based on a cost/price analysis
These rules apply to the use of both non-federal and federal funds and includes small works projects of
less than $350,000.
