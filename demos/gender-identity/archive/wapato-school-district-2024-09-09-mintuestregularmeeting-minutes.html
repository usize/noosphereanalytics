Regular Board Meeting at 5:30 PM (Monday, September 9, 2024)
PUBLIC MEETING OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS
Meeting Location: District Office Boardroom, 212 West 3™ Street, Wapato, WA 98951

1. Opening Item
Call to Order: Board President John Francisco called the meeting to order at 5:30pm.

Pledge of Allegiance

Verification of Quorum: All members present: John Francisco, Sarah Divina, Lorenzo Alvarado, Lori
Garcia, Javier Vela

Agenda: Approval of the agenda as presented.

Motion by Lorenzo Alvarado, second by Sarah Divina. _ Final Resolution: Motion Carries

Yea: John Francisco, Sarah Divina, Lorenzo Alvarado, Lori Garcia, Javier Vela

2. Public Comments
Vanessa Tahkeal commented the Roots to Wings Program is currently not offered at Wapato Schools.

3. Reports
Superintendent's Report: Kelly Garza, Superintendent, reported:

e An update of an incident that occurred regarding a bus.

e Enrollment has slowly increase and the District has met its enrollment projection for the 2024-25
school year.

e He participated in the Harvest Parade over the holiday weekend. The District handed out books and
candy.

e He has been working on the Board of Distinction application and it will be submitted before the next
school board meeting. He noted a large part of the application is focused on the successful passing
of the Bond.

e Attendance and chronic absenteeism are vital data and are currently being reviewed and there will
be a report to the Board at an upcoming meeting of the District process regarding Attendance.

e Safety and Security are a high priority and Tavis Peterson will share more information on this topic
at the next regular meeting.

e Fall sports are underway and 193 athletes are participating at Wapato High School and 135 athletes
at Wapato Middle School.

Construction Update: Kevin Hill, Executive Director, reported an Energy company will visit the District
tomorrow to review and gather more information for a possible new grant for 2 new wells.

The Architect team will be on site this month to meet with PACE staff to begin gathering feedback for
design process. Mr. Garza is working on assembly a committee of staff, families, and students to
participate in the design process. The Board engaged in discussion regarding the design process, building
specs and plans. Mr. Hill reported there are other schools in the State of similar building size and the
District is planning to visit and tour some of those schools. ALSC shared their understating of the
students, needs and the importance of every student every day and will consider all spaces even the
hallways being useful areas.

The Board engaged in discussion regarding the details of the design process.

Enrollment Report September 2024: Sylvia Bazan, Director of Fiscal Services, presented the September
enrollment report. She reported enrollment data in each grade. She noted there are 3 grades that are
just shy of projection but overall a positive report. The Board engaged in discussion regarding funding
amounts for transitional kinder, and enrollment and funding for those students attending YVTech,
Running Start or students that are attending other districts.

4. Consent Action


Consent action approval of items 4.01-4.04 as presented:

4.01 Payroll Fund X-Cycle Warrant No. 164939-164940

4.02 Personnel Recommendations

4.03 Ratification of (2022-2025) WEA/WSD CBA Reopeners Agreement

4.04 Satus/Camas Elementary Library Book Surplus
Motion by Lorenzo Alvarado, second by Sarah Divina. _ Final Resolution: Motion Carries
Yea: John Francisco, Sarah Divina, Lorenzo Alvarado, Lori Garcia, Javier Vela

5. Information/Discussion

Superintendent Garza opened the opportunity to a guest speaker regarding the Roots to Wings program
in response to today’s public comment regarding Wapato SD’s participation in the program for grades 6-
12. The board engaged in discussion. John Francisco noted this discussion to continue at a future
meeting.

6. Closing/Announcements

No closing remarks.

7. Executive Session

At 6:15pm, Board President John Francisco moved to recess until 6:20 pm and then reconvene to
executive session for 30 minutes to discuss the performance of a public-school employee and to
consider the selection of real estate by purchase when public knowledge regarding such consideration
would cause a likelihood of increased price.

Executive session concluded at 6:50pm.

8. Adjourn
The Board completed its order of business as scheduled on the agenda and with no further business to

consider, Board President John Francisco declared the meeting adjourned at 6:50pm.

Recording Secretary President of the Board
