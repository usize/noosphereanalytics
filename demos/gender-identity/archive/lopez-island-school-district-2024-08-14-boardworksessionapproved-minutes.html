APPROVED 8/28/24

BOARD OF DIRECTORS
Lopez Island School District #144
August 14, 2024
Library Media Center
4:00 pm
Board Work Session Minutes

A board work session is an open public meeting, and public attendance is welcome. There is no Zoom meeting access for board
work sessions. There is no opportunity for public comment during board work sessions since no action may be taken by the
board. The board may choose to invite other members of district staff, school staff, or outside experts to provide information.

I.

CALL TO ORDER:
Directors present: Chair Price-Morris, Director O’Connell, Director Taylor, Director Murphy. Staff
present: Superintendent Smith.
Chair Price-Morris called the meeting to order at 4:03 pm. A quorum was present.

II.

PLEDGE OF ALLEGIANCE AND LAND ACKNOWLEDGEMENT:
Chair led the flag salute and gave the land acknowledgement.

III.

BOARD ITEMS FOR DISCUSSION
A. Procedure 3242P Closed Campus - redundant, board will not pursue a procedure here, but will
request 3242F, the referenced form.
B. Policy 3243 Student Driving and Procedure 3243P
C. Policy 3122 Excused and Unexcused Absences and Procedure 3122P Absences - Page 2, #2,
Director Murphy suggested removing excused/unexcused absences and only count absence as
absence.
Compare against RCW requirements and OSPI; Does law permit a max # of days a student can
miss in the school year? Workshop to revise attendance policy and make it more operational.
Focus on the strategic plan and include in the goals/focus for this year
D. School Facilities Use- tennis courts (4260)
Liability - Teresa provided a USTA certificate of insurance; Brady to present an upgraded facilities
use form.
E. Water Utility
1. District Statement of Intended Ownership Transfer Statement review, amend by-laws to
identify election process of directors, Malia to bring amendment to August Board Meeting.
2. Cross-Control Plan and Established Legal Authority - not ready yet. Ensuring there are
backflow prohibitors. FLIP redesigning catchment pond.
F. Nutrition and Wellness Policy Series 6700 - Malia to review and highlight what requirements are
driven by an RCW. Revisit discussion during goal setting.
G. Superintendent Evaluation - revisit discussion during board evaluation meeting and goal setting.
H. Student Withdrawals - 13 students not returning. 6 we were aware of, then 7 left, 2 were
unexpected..

APPROVED 8/28/24

IV.

ADJOURNMENT
Chair Price-Morris adjourned the meeting at 5:45 pm.
