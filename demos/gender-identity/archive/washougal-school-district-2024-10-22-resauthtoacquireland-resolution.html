WASHOUGAL SCHOOL DISTRICT 112-6
RESOLUTION NO. 2024-2025-01:
AUTHORIZATION TO ACQUIRE LAND
CLARK COUNTY TAX PARCEL NO. 986039-602

WHEREAS, the Washougal School District entered into an option agreement with Kysar
Development, LLC dated November 28, 2020, giving it the right to purchase property for a new
school site, known as Tax Parcel No. 986039-602 (Property); and

WHEREAS, the option agreement expires on December 31, 2024.

WHEREAS, the District adopted a capital facilities plan in 2022, that has been incorporated into
the comprehensive land use plans of Clark County and the City of Washougal in 2022 and 2023,
respectively, which identifies the Property as needed for a future school.

WHEREAS, the Property is currently outside the Washougal Urban Growth Area (UGA), and
because of a covenant recorded against title, the Property cannot be used for school purposes
until the Property is included in the UGA.

WHEREAS, Clark County and the City of Washougal are currently updating their
comprehensive land use plans, including potentially revising UGAs, as mandated by state law
that must be complete by December 31, 2025.

WHEREAS, both Clark County and City of Washougal, through adopted policies and planners’
statements, have demonstrated that each is supportive of including the Property in the Washougal
UGA by the end of 2025.

WHEREAS, the District has collected impact fees from new development which may only be
expended on school facilities listed in the District’s capital facilities plan, and that the District
has sufficient impact fee revenue to purchase the Property.

WHEREAS, the fair market value of the Property was established by an appraisal prepared by
Jarvis Appraisal Co. dated April 11, 2024, confirming the value of the Property at $1,025,000;
and

WHEREAS, the District has completed its due diligence for the Property and has determined
that there are no significant adverse conditions that would prevent the District from using the
Property for its intended purposes once it is included in the Washougal UGA; and

WHEREAS, the District has determined that it is in the District’s best interest to acquire the
Property now because its option will expire at the end of 2024, it is in a location that can serve
the District’s future needs, it is reasonably likely it will be included in the UGA, and it is
necessary to prevent the property from being sold to another party.

BE IT RESOLVED that the Washougal School District Board of Directors authorize the
interim superintendent or his designees to exercise the Kysar option and acquire the real
property, improvements, and any additional interests in the property located in Clark
County, Washington, identified as Tax Parcel No. 986039-602, legally described in
Exhibit A, and to take all steps to immediately encumber the impact fees for the purchase
of the Property.

ADOPTED by the Board of Directors of Washougal School District at an open public meeting
on this 22nd day of October 2024.
