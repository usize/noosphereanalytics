Travel Request
Wapato High School

To: Mr. K. Garza, Superintendent
Wapato School District Board of Directors

From: Christopher Beyrouty, Engineering Teacher

Date: Sept 5, 2024

RE: Request the approval of the Board of Directors for permission to travel to Maple Valley, Washington
on Saturday October 12™ to 13".

Number of Students Involved: 16

Names & Number of Chaperones: 2
1. Christopher Beyrouty, HS CTE Engineering Teacher
2. Jacqueline Benson, Mentor

Background:

For the last few years WREC has been participating in off-season FRC competitions. These
competitions are a rerun of the competition from last year. We will use this opportunity to test
some new skills and students in their ability to play the game. We also use this competition to
get new recruits, freshman and upperclassmen, excited about FRC. This gives them a brief
introduction and example of what the competitions are like.

Transportation:
We will be taking a van and a suburban to the competition. We would need to leave about
3:30am in the morning to make it to the venue for the 6:30am load in.

Lodging:

We will be reserving rooms at a hotel in Kent. We are budgeting $160/night/room. We will be
putting 4 students in a room and two mentors in a room. We are planning on getting 4 rooms
for students and two rooms for mentors.

We are going to get breakfast for the students Saturday morning which will consist of pastries
and juice. We will provide lunch from Jersey Mike’s sub shop on Saturday and Sunday. Dinner
both days will be at a fast food restaurant.

Costs:

The cost to the club will be $2400. See Budget Worksheet attached.

This request will not be approved and forwarded to the Board until funding is secured.
