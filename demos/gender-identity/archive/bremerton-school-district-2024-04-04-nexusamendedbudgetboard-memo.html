TO: Board of Directors

THROUGH: Dr. James Crawford, Superintendent

FROM: Dr. Garth Steedman, Assistant Superintendent of Finance, Operations & Human Resources
SUBJECT: Recommending amending the Nexus, Building Envelope Inspections contract to include

Construction Phase Services: submittal reviews for Building Envelope elements, Design
Consultation during construction, participation in Pre-Construction/Pre-Installation
Meetings & Inspections and Quality Control On-Site Reviews.

DATE: April 4, 2024

ACTION:

Recommend Bremerton School District (BSD) Board of Directors amend the Nexus, Building Envelope Inspections
contract to include Construction Phase Services in the amount of $74,865.00. This will include submittal reviews for
building envelope elements, design consultation during construction, participation in Pre-Construction/Pre-Installation
meetings and Inspections and Quality Control On-Site Reviews.

BACKGROUND:

On March 15, 2023, the Board approved entering into an agreement with Nexus Building Envelope Consultants for the initial
preliminary phase of the WSTSC project in the amount of $23,400.00. Nexus addressed the Technical Design Review,
providing an assessment of the overall condition of the building envelope elements, most commonly applied to roofing.
Construction Services for the project were not part of the original scope of work.

BENEFIT TO THE DISTRICT:
Review and monitor construction to verify and document the project condition assessments, construction site inspections,
window water penetration testing, and air barrier testing on the new building.

COST TO THE DISTRICT:

Total estimated costs for Nexus Building Envelope Construction Services are not-to-exceed $74,865.00, and will be billed on
atime and materials basis. The costs for Nexus’s services have been determined to be fair, reasonable and in accordance with
industry standards.
