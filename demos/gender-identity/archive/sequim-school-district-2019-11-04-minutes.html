    Superintendent Report
Former student has filed a tort claim with the school district. Student was injured in 2017. A tort can be a pre-cursor to a lawsuit. Student has until age of majority which is 21. Have spoken with claims and insurance and will keep you updated.
Negotiations are going well and is enjoying not being the lead person. Lorraine Wilson does a great job. Will be meeting with 5 strategic plan point people. Goal is to have Strategic Plan rolled out at first of the year.
Audit finding happened in 2016. Money was not spent through the right channels. There was no fraud. People involved are no longer employed with the district. Darlene has put a lot of things in place for oversight.
Looking forward to Former Board Luncheon is November 14 and receiving RSVP’s. Please RSVP if you can attend.
After WSSDA, we need to talk about PD for the board. We need to look at various avenues. Quarterly is not out of line.
We have had marijuana incidents at the school. We are bringing law enforcement in on all cases. After the first of the year it will be more specific.
Before this school year is out we will have an emergency drill at a board meeting. Will be attending a funeral this weekend.
