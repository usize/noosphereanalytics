October 25, 2024
Dear Moses Lake School Board,

On behalf of the Sage Point Parent-Teacher Organization (PTO), | am pleased to inform you
that we have successfully raised the necessary funds to purchase Lexia, a highly effective
supplemental English Language Arts (ELA) program, for all our students. This initiative aligns
with our goal to enhance literacy and language skills across grade levels, ensuring every

student has access to additional tools for academic growth.

The total cost of the Lexia program is $11,392.50, which covers licensing for our entire student
body. With the PTO's dedicated efforts and the generous contributions from our school
community, we have raised the full amount needed. Our intent is to make Lexia a resource
available to every student, providing personalized learning pathways to support their individual

reading and comprehension skills.

We are excited about the potential impact this program can have on our students’ literacy
development and are hopeful for the board's approval to proceed with the purchase. The PTO is
confident that Lexia will be a valuable asset in complementing our current ELA curriculum and

enhancing the educational experience for all.
Thank you for your consideration of this initiative and your ongoing support of our students and
school community. Please feel free to reach out if you require further information or have

questions about this proposal.

Sincerely,

GAL,

Robin L Kimbro, Principal - Sage Point Elementary
