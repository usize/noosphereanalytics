    11. REPORTS-7:55 pm
  Information: 11.1 Graduation Rates
  Assistant Superintendent Greg Schwab presented a report about the district's 2022 Graduation rates. The graduation rates were reviewed over time, in comparison to other districts, disaggregated by race/ethnicity and disaggregated by special program. Mr. Schwab then discussed what the district is currently doing to try to improve graduation rates. Director Chase asked how we can keep students connected and involved at school (hopefully increasing their likelihood of graduation). Mr. Schwab discussed what we are currently doing to encourage involvement in school activities and clubs as well as involvement in CTE programs. Dr. Miner noted that attendance is a very important piece of graduation rates and this is something we will be focusing on in the coming year.
