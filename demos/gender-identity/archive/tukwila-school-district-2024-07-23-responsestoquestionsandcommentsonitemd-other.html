Regular Board Meeting
July 23, 2024

Responses to the Board’s questions/comments on Agenda Item 6.D—2024-2025 Budget for the July 23, 2024 Regular Board
Meeting:

1. Agenda Item 6.D — 2024-2025 Budget

1. 1 would like to see a breakdown of how local levy dollars are being budgeted.
Amy Fleming: | invite individual board members to schedule a meeting with me to review the requested
documentation.

2. | would like to see a more granular breakdown of Apportionment, State Revenue Program, Federal
Revenue, Federal Grant Other Agency and Other State Agency.
Amy Fleming: | invite individual board members to schedule a meeting with me to review the requested
documentation.

3. On Page 15, what specifically is causing Supplies/Materials to go from $2.9 million in 23-24 to $1.1 million
in 24-25?
Amy Fleming: When the 2023-24 budget extension was done, additional budget capacity was placed in
Supplies and Materials as a placeholder. Cost-cutting measures have been implemented for the 2024-25
budget, especially in administration activities. Loss of ESSER funding has also caused a reduction in
supplies/materials budgets.

4. | would like to see a section that specifically calls out the amounts and conditions of the loans from the
Capital Projects Fund.
Amy Fleming: This will be updated in the next draft provided to the board.

5. How are the inter-fund loans reflected in the Fund Balance History on page 23?
Amy Fleming: The interfund loan was not included in the fund balance history.
