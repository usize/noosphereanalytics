DRAFT MINUTES OF SCHOOL BOARD VISIT TO
BELLINGHAM HIGH SCHOOL
BELLINGHAM SCHOOL DISTRICT BOARD OF DIRECTORS
November 5, 2019

1.1

1.2

Meeting Time/Date and Location
7:30 a.m., November 5, 2019 – Bellingham High School – 2020 Cornwall Ave.,
Bellingham, WA
Roll Call/Attendees
Board President Camille Diaz Hackler called the meeting to order at 7:30 a.m. Present:
Directors Kelly Bashaw, Douglas Benjamin, Camille Diaz Hackler, Jenn Mason and
Superintendent Greg Baker. Absent: Director Quenby Peterson.
Other Attendees
• Bellingham High School staff, students and parents
• Executive Team members

2.0

DISCUSSION
Bellingham High School students shared about their work to create a fun and inclusive
school-centered trademark for t-shirts and signs around the school: “We ___ BHS.” The
middle “verb” in their brand can be anything that a student (or staff) has pride in and
identifies with: cheer, soccer, chemistry, basketball, math, QSA, etc., or it can simply be
their mascot the red raider hawk. Bellingham High School staff talked about the inclusive
culture of the school.
