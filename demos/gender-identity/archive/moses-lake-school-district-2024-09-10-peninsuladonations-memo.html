Moses Lake School District School Board,
This letter is to inform the board about donations that have been made fo Peninsula

In June of 2024, the Peninsula PTO purchased radios and a secure radio channel. They also
paid the set up fees for the secure channel and radios. The total cost of this donation was
$7,710.97. Attached is the quote from Racom Digital Communications.

In September of 2024 the PTO purchased a school license for Lexia Core 5. The total cost of
this license was $11,392.50. Attached is the quote from Lexia Learning Systems LLC.

The funds raised by our PTO and school community heloed meet safety needs and have
helped provide academic support. We are thankful and appreciative of these generous

donations.

Respectfully,

Amy Wright
