To: Dr. Concie Pedroza, Superintendent

From: Scott Whitbeck, Chief of Academics and Schools
Date: September 10, 2024

Subject: Instructional Hours Verification Report - 2024/25 School Year

Data provided by Aaron Draganov, Executive Director of Human Resources, Labor, and
Technology.

This report provides data which verifies that the number of instructional hours in Tukwila School
District (TSD) in the 2024/25 school year meets Washington State requirements. One way of
meeting this requirement is to provide a K-12 average of at least 1,027 hours of instructional
time per year. As shown in the table on the “Instructional Minutes/Hours Calculations 2024-25”
page, TSD is providing 1,045 hours in 2024/25 as an average of our K-12 grade levels, which
exceeds the annual state minimum by 18 hours.

The “Month-By-Month School Day Counts 2024-25” page provides the numbers of instructional
days for each month in the 2024-25 school year.
