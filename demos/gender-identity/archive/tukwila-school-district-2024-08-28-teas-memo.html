MEMORANDUM
To: Dr. Concie Pedroza, Superintendent
FROM: Aaron Draganov, Human Resources

SUBJECT: Information on the TEA-CERT and TEA-ESP 2024-26 Contracts

DATE: September 10, 2024

The collective bargaining process for the TEA-CERT and TEA-ESP units
concluded in the early morning hours of Friday, August 23, 2024. The
enclosed document, which includes changes for both TEA contracts
organized in a table, is posted for review and approval by the Tukwila School
District Board of Directors.

On Wednesday, August 28, 2024, the TEA units held their ratification
general assembly and passed votes to ratify each of the agreements.

Association and district leadership are currently working on updating
the contract copies and anticipate having them completed and ready to post

on the district website before the end of September 2024.
