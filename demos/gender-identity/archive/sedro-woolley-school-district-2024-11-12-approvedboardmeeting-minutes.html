Presentations

Central Elementary — Principal Karissa Sokol provided the board with an update on teaching and
learning strategies at Central.

TATU Presentation (Teens Against Tobacco Use) — Angie Washington and members of the
Upper Skagit Indian Tribe provided the board with a presentation on the work being done by


their teens and the opportunities for the Upper Skagit Indian Tribe Teens to learn about the
dangers of substance abuse.

2023-24 Year-End Financial Report — Joli Valentino, PSESD, provided the board with the year-
end report that included information on the five funds: Associated Student Body Fund,
Transportation Vehicle Fund, Capital Projects Fund, Debt Service Fund and the General Fund.

the descriptors used in the SBA testing data.

Superintendent's Report:

Dr. Mickelson reported that many of the schools in the district celebrated Veteran’s Day with
wonderful assemblies and parades.

Superintendent Mickelson also recognized the Upper Skagit Indian Tribe with a certificate of
appreciation for going above and beyond in supporting the district schools through funding
iReady, providing vital technology, supporting the new playground at Evergreen, funding

_ paraprofessional positions, and much more. The contributions have made a lasting impact on the
student’s educational experience.

School Board Representative, Adriana Rodriguez, provided the SWHS activity report.

Daniel Lee provided the November enrollment report.

Executive Session — RCW 42.30.110 — to evaluate the qualification of a

candidate for appointment to elective office.

Vice President Russell called for an Executive Session of the Board at 7:33 p.m. to evaluate the
qualifications of a candidate for appointment to elective office pursuant to RCW 42.30.110.
Director Schiefelbein attended via phone. Superintendent Mickelson was invited to attend the
executive session. The board will resume regular session at 8:10 p.m. At 8:10 p.m., Vice
President Russell extended the executive session until 8:20 p.m. At 8:21 p.m., Vice President
Russell resumed the regular session of the meeting.

Old Business

Danielle Russell moved to approve the Facility Use Feed Change Proposal. The motion died for
lack of a second.
