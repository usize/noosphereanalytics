TO: Bremerton School District Board of Directors
THROUGH: Dr. Garth Steedman. Acting Superintendent
FROM: Dr. Garth Steedman, Assistant Superintendent of Finance, Operations, & Human Resources

SUBJECT: Recommending the approval of the Cabling & Technology Services proposal for the Mountain
View Middle School camera upgrade project

DATE: August 1, 2024

ACTION:

Recommending that the Bremerton School District (BSD) Board of Directors approve the Cabling &
Technology Services proposal for the Mountain View Middle School camera upgrade project in the
amount of $243,157.14 plus Washington State sales tax.

BACKGROUND:
The cameras at Mountain View Middle School are outdated and require significant use of the aging
servers. The replacement cameras will be easier for staff to use and require less server use.

BENEFIT TO THE DISTRICT:
Approval of this project will allow for upgrades which will increase security with a lower impact on the
network.

COST TO THE DISTRICT:

Total estimated cost of the Mountain View Middle School camera upgrade project is $243,157.14 plus
Washington State sales tax. The price includes the purchase and installation of new Verkada cameras for
the building.
