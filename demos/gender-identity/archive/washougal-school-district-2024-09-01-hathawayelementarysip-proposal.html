MTSS Goal 2023/2024 Clomentary SYnsa

By the end of the 2023/2024 school year, we will increase the implementation
fidelity of the tier one PBIS System from 50% to at least 70%. We will increase
the implementation fidelity of tier two PBIS System from 54% to at least 70%.

e Wewere able to make the 70% goal for implementation of PBIS tier 1 but only made
60% for tier 2. While we only made it halfway, we are excited with the increase in
implementation and are motivated to continue improving our processes next year.

e Teachers following the plan for teaching and reteaching common area expectations
was helpful towards implementation growth, although we have plans for streamlining
this in the 2024/2025 school year.


This Year’s Goals



ELA Goal/Action Steps

By the end of the 2024/2025 school year, based on current cohort data K-5, we
will increase the percentage of students reaching proficiency on FastBridge/SBA
ELA by 15%.

e Teachers will run intervention cycles including intervention, extra practice, and
extension for each of the 3 essential standards.

e 3-5 grade students will engage in focused IABs at least twice a month before testing
January- May.

e Students will engage in AVID WICOR strategies for vocabulary development at least
once a week.


Math Goal/Action Steps

By the end of the 2024/2025 school year, based on current cohort data K-5, we
will increase the percentage of students reaching proficiency on FastBridge/SBA
Math by 15%.

e Teachers will run intervention cycles including intervention, extra practice, and
extension for each of the 3 essential standards.

e 3-5 grade students will engage in focused IABs at least twice a month before testing
January- May.

e Teachers will use the Illustrative math curriculum for their core math time.


Student Engagement Goal/Action Steps

By the end of the 2024/2025 school year, Hathaway will increase the percentage
of students attending 90% or more days from 67% to 80%.

e The Attendance team will meet monthly to analyze the data and identify any students who are
chronically absent to come up with a plan of support.

e Monthly information is shared regarding the benefits of consistent attendance in the Monthly school
newsletter via ParentSquare.

e Creation ofa Tier 2 Attendance Team to create plans for chronically absent students.

e Monthly incentives and assemblies to celebrate consistent and improved attendance.
