BOARD OF DIRECTORS
Lopez Island School District #144
June 12, 2024
Library Media Center
4:00 pm
Board Work Session Agenda

A board work session is an open public meeting, and public attendance is welcome. There is no Zoom meeting access for board
work sessions. There is no opportunity for public comment during board work sessions since no action may be taken by the
board. The board may choose to invite other members of district staff, school staff, or outside experts to provide information.

I. CALL TO ORDER: Chair called the meeting to order at 4:07 pm.
Present: Chair Price-Morris, Vice Chair Carrier, Direct Murphy, Director O’Connell, Superintendent Smith
II. PLEDGE OF ALLEGIANCE AND LAND ACKNOWLEDGEMENT: Vice Chair Carrier leads Pledge, Chair
Price-Morris leads Land Acknowledgement
III. BOARD ITEMS FOR DISCUSSION: Chair Price-Morris leads discussion.
A. Policy Series 6700
B. Budget
C. Water Utility
IV. ADJOURNMENT: Chair Price-Morris motions to adjourn 6:06 pm. Approved unanimously..
