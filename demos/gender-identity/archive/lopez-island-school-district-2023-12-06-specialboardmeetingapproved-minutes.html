Approved 12/20/23
BOARD OF DIRECTORS
Lopez Island School District #144
December 6, 2023
Library Media Center/Zoom
4:00 pm
Special Board Meeting Minutes

Meeting Link can be found on LISD Website

A Special Board Meeting is an open public meeting, and public attendance is welcome.

I.

CALL TO ORDER:
Directors present: Chair Carrier, Director O’Connell, Director Taylor, Staff present: Dr. Martha Martin.
Chair Carrier called the meeting to order at 4:03 pm. A quorum was present.

II.

PLEDGE OF ALLEGIANCE AND LAND ACKNOWLEDGEMENT:
Chair Carrier gave the Flag Salute and led the land acknowledgement.

III.

OATH OF OFFICE BOARD DIRECTOR:
Chair Carrier administered the Oath of Office to board elect Sarah Murphy.

IV.

NEW BUSINESS:
A. Policy 1400 Meeting Conduct, Order of Business and Quorum - First Reading
First reading discussion of Policy 1400.
B. December Regular Board Meeting
Chair Carrier moved to hold the regular December Board meeting in the Library Media
Center. Discussion: none. The board approved the motion unanimously.
ADJOURNMENT:
Director Taylor moved to adjourn the meeting. No discussion. Motion approved unanimously.
Chair Carrier adjourned the meeting at 4:18 pm.
